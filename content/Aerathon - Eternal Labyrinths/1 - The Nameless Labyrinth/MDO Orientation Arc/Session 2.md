## **IX. Dinner at the Cracked Cauldron – “A Meal to Remember”**

**Location:** The Cracked Cauldron Tavern, Turnwell Lane off the Silver Causeway

**Atmosphere:**

- Cozy interior with enchanted lanterns and moss-lined stone floors
- Warm aromas of roasted herbs, bread, and slow-cooked stew fill the air
- Quiet harp music plucks itself from an invisible instrument near the hearth
- Locals chat softly at surrounding tables; magic is present, but not ostentatious
    

**NPCs Present:**

- **Mera of the Downhill Flock** – Lamb beastfolk waitress, gentle and attentive, scented with lavender
- **Mr. Thatch** – Elderly feline beastfolk, owner of the tavern; speaks with gravitas and warmth
- Various background patrons – a couple of alchemists, a trio of delvers, and one bard writing alone
    

**Events:**

- Party arrives after sundown, drawn in by savory smells and a warm glow spilling onto the street
- Mera greets the group with familiarity and kindness, seating them near a window and offering menus
- Each party member orders something different, including Delver’s Stew, Prismfish, and Woolcake
- Small banter begins at the table: soft questions, a few jokes, and light teasing about tomorrow’s trials
- Partway through the meal, Mr. Thatch emerges from the back kitchen and recognizes **Corvo Lykoi**
- It’s revealed that Corvo’s family helped fund the tavern and the meal is **on the house!**
- Corvo responds with composed gratitude.
- The meal continues in comfort: warm food, low voices, and a touch of understanding shared
    

**Departure:**

- The party finishes their meal and steps back out into Louvain’s lamplit streets, bellies full and moods softened
- Behind them, the Cracked Cauldron glows with quiet magic, and Mr. Thatch watches from the doorway
- The group begins showing off their 'flashiest skills' to one another while they walk back to the dormitory

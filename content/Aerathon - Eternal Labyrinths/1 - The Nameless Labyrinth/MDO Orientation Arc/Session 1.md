- **Ceremony Overseen By:** **Overseer Atria Vael (MDO)**

    - Tone: Cold, impartial, strictly professional
        
- **Key Concepts Introduced:**
    - **Merit Index:** Tiered ranking system (C → B → A → S)
    - **MDO Oversight:** All delver activity is recorded, measured
    - **Delver Medallions:** Bound by soul-signature, tracked magically
    - **Only the Ministry defines official rank.**
        
- **Important Visuals:**
    - Divine Oculi recording stones in rafters
    - Scribes of the Arcanet Core whispering to data crystals
    - Elite delvers watching silently from aside
        
- **OOC Prompt:** How does your character react when their name is called?
	- *Antekhotep* - Proudly walked up to receive his medallion, yet was sure to remain looking regal. 
	- *Chandelle* - Dramatically flew up to the stage, flaunting their wings as wind produced from the Arcanet rushed past them.
	- *Milo* - Mist stepped on stage to receive their medallion. Yet with such a proud entrance, they begrudgingly walked to their seat due to hecklers. 
	- *Solaris* - Pranced to the stage - happily accepting the medallion with open arms. He turns to the crowd and gives his best speech, only to sit down disappointed in the crowd's reaction. 
	- *Yyna* - Quickly moves onto stage. Grabs the medallion. Gives a brief acknowledgement before quickly going back to seat.

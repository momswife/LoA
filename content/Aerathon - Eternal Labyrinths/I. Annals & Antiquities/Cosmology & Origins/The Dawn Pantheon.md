## **I. Overview**

The **Dawn Pantheon** consists of the gods who claimed stewardship over Aerathon after the withdrawal of the Old Beastfolk Pantheon into the Far Heaven. These deities manifested not through ancestral bloodlines but through **the shaping of mortal culture**—agriculture, war, poetry, magic, storms, fortune, and death.

They preside from the **Near Heaven**, a structured divine realm formed around mortal belief, societal values, and symbolic dominion. Unlike the Far Heaven, which reflects primordial cycles, the Near Heaven is shaped by:

- civilization,
    
- cultural memory,
    
- mythmaking, and
    
- mortal need.
    

All members of the Dawn Pantheon remain fully bound by the **Quiet Edict**, preventing direct intervention, divine conflict, or overt displays of cosmic power.

This dossier records their dominions, symbols, and known interactions with labyrinthic space.

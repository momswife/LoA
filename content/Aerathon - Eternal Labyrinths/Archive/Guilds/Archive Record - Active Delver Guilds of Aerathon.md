## **Overview: The Delver Guilds**

The Labyrinths of Aerathon are deadly, unpredictable, and often beyond mortal comprehension, yet their promise of treasure, power, and forgotten knowledge continues to lure the brave, the foolish, and the desperate into their depths.

With the rise of Livecasting, corporate-backed expeditions, and the dominance of the Halvaskian Delving Authority (HDA), Delvers have increasingly turned to organized guilds for protection, sponsorship, and influence.

While some guilds operate as elite professional organizations, others function as mercenary brotherhoods, criminal syndicates, or religious orders. Some are officially recognized by the HDA, while others operate outside the law, pursuing their own agendas.

Below is a classified record of the most influential, notorious, and enigmatic Delver Guilds in Aerathon.

---

## **Major & Recognized Delver Guilds**

_(These guilds are officially sanctioned by the HDA and permitted to operate under legal jurisdiction.)_

## **The Free Company of Stalwart Souls** - *Everyone's Welcome*

**Motto:** _"We go together, or we don’t go at all."_  
**Headquarters:** **Halvask, The Rusted Lodge (West District, Delver’s Row)**  
**Guild Type:** Open-Entry, Mixed-Rank, Unaffiliated Delver Collective  
**Notable Members:** No single leader—operates as a loosely structured fraternity of Delvers.

The Free Company of Stalwart Souls (often simply called “The Stalwart Souls”) is a last refuge for Delvers with nowhere else to go. Unlike the prestigious, sponsor-backed Delver guilds, or the shadowy and selective black-market groups, the Stalwart Souls are the guild of the unwanted, the unchosen, and the forgotten.

- No sponsor? No problem.
- No reputation? You’ll earn one.
- No team? You’ll find one.

Anyone willing to sign the charter and take an expedition contract is welcome—regardless of skill, background, past failures, or personal grudges.

### **The Argent Banner** –  _The Noble’s Choice_

**Motto:** _"For Gold, Glory, and the Grand Story."_  
**Headquarters:** Halvask, The Silver Hall  
**Guild Type:** Elite Sponsored Delvers, Wealth & Prestige-Driven  
**Notable Members:** **Ser Victor Kordane (Guildmaster, Ex-Knight of the Radiant Covenant), Lydia Velmire (Gold-Ranked Duelist & Livecast Celebrity)**

The Argent Banner is the most prestigious and commercially successful Delver Guild, known for producing some of the most famous (and marketable) adventurers in history. It is backed by noble patrons, wealthy merchant lords, and corporate sponsors, ensuring that its members have the best gear, the best training, and the best Livecasting exposure.

- **Gold-Plated Reputation:** Members of the Argent Banner are often seen as the "face" of professional Delving, maintaining a chivalric, honorable, and heroic public image.
- **Tightly Controlled Membership:** Only sponsored Delvers may join, making this guild inaccessible to common adventurers.
- **Profit Over Principle:** Critics argue that the guild is more concerned with making a spectacle than actual exploration, with some suspecting certain expeditions are staged to ensure high viewership.

**Affiliated With:** The HDA, the Grand Scrying Network (GSN), and various noble families.

---

### **The Hollow Pact** – _The Secrets Beneath_

**Motto:** _"The Labyrinths speak. We listen."_  
**Headquarters:** Veilshroud Expanse, The Obsidian Archive  
**Guild Type:** Scholarly, Arcane Research, and Forbidden Knowledge Seekers  
**Notable Members:** **Scholar-Magus Illithane Valros (Master Historian), Whisperer Therion (Diviner of Lost Paths)**

The Hollow Pact is a guild of scholars, archaeologists, and arcane researchers who believe that the Labyrinths hold truths that could reshape the world. They are sanctioned by the Arcane Guild of Labyrinth Studies (AGLS) and maintain strict regulations on artifact recovery, research ethics, and knowledge distribution.

- **The Quest for Understanding:** Unlike mercenary Delvers, the Hollow Pact does not seek gold or fame—it seeks truth.
- **Restricted Membership:** Only those with academic or arcane backgrounds are allowed entry.
- **Dark Accusations:** Some believe the Hollow Pact deliberately withholds discoveries, keeping dangerous knowledge out of the hands of the public.

**Affiliated With:** The Arcane Guild of Labyrinth Studies (AGLS), certain Veilborn Theocracy factions.

---

### **The Crimson Reavers** –  _The Mercenary Horde_

**Motto:** _"No Chains, No Masters—Only Coin and Steel."_  
**Headquarters:** The Titan Highlands, The Reaver’s Hold  
**Guild Type:** Bloodthirsty Warbands, Mercenary Delvers-for-Hire  
**Notable Members:** **Orik "The Bloodmarked" (Guild Leader, Ex-Gladiator), Vasha the Stormwolf (Reckless Berserker & Delving Champion)**

The Crimson Reavers are a loose coalition of mercenary warbands, feared for their ruthlessness, skill in combat, and willingness to take suicidal contracts.

- **No Questions Asked:** As long as the pay is good, they will take any job—monster hunting, artifact recovery, or assassination within a Labyrinth.
- **The Bloody Spectacle:** Some of the most brutal and controversial Livecasts feature Crimson Reaver expeditions, often ending in high casualty counts and gory battles.
- **Loyalty to the Coin, Not the Guild:** The Reavers constantly shift allegiances, with internal fights often breaking out between warband factions.

**Affiliated With:** The GSN (for spectacle-based expeditions), certain black-market sponsors.

## **Unofficial & Rogue Delver Guilds**

_(These guilds operate outside HDA regulations, existing as underground organizations or criminal factions.)_

### **The Gilded Shadows** – _The Treasure Hoarders_

**Motto:** _"What’s in the Labyrinth is ours—gods and kings be damned."_  
**Headquarters:** **Unknown (Suspected to operate in Halvask’s undercity)**  
**Guild Type:** Treasure Hunters, Smugglers, and Black Market Artifact Dealers  
**Notable Members:** **"Velka the Unseen" (Master Thief), Kael Riven (Ex-Noble turned Smuggler Lord)**

The Gilded Shadows are a secretive network of rogue Delvers, artifact smugglers, and black-market historians who believe that the wealth of the Labyrinths should belong to those who claim it, not bureaucrats or nobles.

- **Highly Skilled Delvers:** Members are masters of evasion, trap-breaking, and artifact retrieval.
- **Enemies of the HDA:** The Delver Marshals hunt them relentlessly, viewing them as grave robbers and criminals.
- **Unknown Agendas:** Some believe that the Shadows are not just thieves but preservers, hiding artifacts that the ABA and HDA would rather see destroyed.

**Enemies Of:** The HDA, The ABA, and Noble-backed Delver guilds.

---

### **The Hollow Masks** – _The Identity Shifters_

**Motto:** _"No Name. No Face. No Past."_  
**Headquarters:** **Unknown (Suspected Cells in Veilshroud Expanse & Umbral Dominion)**  
**Guild Type:** Espionage, Disguises, and Information Manipulation  
**Notable Members:** **Unknown (Identities constantly shift).**

The Hollow Masks are a mysterious collective of Delvers who operate in absolute secrecy, using illusion magic, stolen identities, and false histories to infiltrate, deceive, and manipulate the Delving industry.

- Some say they work for the ABA, controlling the narratives of Livecasted Delvers.
- Others believe they are former Delvers who were erased from history, now seeking revenge.
- Their true purpose remains unknown.

## **Overview: The Crimson Reavers**

**Motto:** _"The Labyrinth does not judge—only the weak do."_  
**Headquarters:** **The Reaver’s Hold, Titan Highlands**  
**Guild Type:** Mercenary Delvers-for-Hire, Bloodsport Gladiators, Cutthroat Treasure Hunters  
**Notable Members:**

- **Orik "The Bloodmarked"** (Guild Leader, Ex-Gladiator, Last Champion of the Drowned Colosseum)
- **Vasha the Stormwolf** (Reckless Berserker & Delving Champion)
- **Riven "The Red Fox" Dain** (Master Duelist, Tactician, and Ruthless Opportunist)

The Crimson Reavers are not a guild in the traditional sense—they are a roving warband, a brotherhood of blood, and a chaotic force of destruction that thrives in the Labyrinths of Aerathon.

Unlike noble-backed guilds like The Argent Banner or scholarly institutions like The Hollow Pact, the Crimson Reavers operate on one rule alone:

Strength is survival. Weakness is death.

- **Mercenary Delvers for Hire** – The Reavers do not delve for honor or knowledge—they do it for gold, blood, and the sheer thrill of combat.
- **Warriors, Gladiators, and Madmen** – Their members come from gladiatorial pits, war-torn battlefields, and criminal underworlds, each carrying their own reasons for embracing the chaos of the Labyrinths.
- **Glory Through Violence** – While other guilds prepare, strategize, and tread carefully, the Reavers charge in first and worry about the consequences later.

To the wealthy noble houses, the Crimson Reavers are brutal, uncivilized, and dangerous.

To the Grand Scrying Network, they are ratings gold.

## **Structure & Leadership**

The Crimson Reavers do not believe in rigid hierarchies. Instead, they operate under a warband system, where the strongest warriors naturally rise to lead—until someone stronger kills them and takes their place.

### **The Current Chain of Strength (Leadership Roster)**

Unlike most guilds, leadership within the Reavers is fluid, based entirely on combat dominance and reputation.

## **What the Crimson Reavers Offer**

Unlike structured, disciplined guilds, the Crimson Reavers embrace chaos. Their members live and die by the sword, and those who survive gain infamy beyond measure.

### **Guild Benefits**

- **No Laws, No Restrictions** – The Reavers do not answer to the HDA, noble sponsors, or corporate backers. They take what they want, when they want.
- **Blood for Gold, Gold for Blood** – Members keep the majority of their earnings, taking spoils directly from the Labyrinths and rival Delvers.
- **Absolute Freedom** – Reavers are not bound by contracts—if they grow tired of a warband, they can challenge a leader, start their own, or strike out alone.
- **Gladiatorial Fame & Grand Scrying Network Sponsorships** – Some of the most violent and dramatic Livecast moments come from Crimson Reaver expeditions, leading to high-profile sponsorships for the bloodiest fighters.
- **A Brotherhood of Killers & Survivors** – While Reavers may fight among themselves, there is an unspoken bond between those who bleed together in the Labyrinths.

To join the Crimson Reavers is to abandon restraint—once you are a Reaver, the only way out is through blood.

---

## **Who Joins the Crimson Reavers?**

The Crimson Reavers do not recruit—they take.

New members are either:

- **Gladiators, Pit Fighters, and War Criminals** – Those who live for the thrill of battle and have no home beyond their own strength.
- **Fallen Soldiers & Disgraced Knights** – Warriors who seek redemption through violence, or revenge against the world that cast them aside.
- **Blacklisted Delvers** – Those who broke contracts, killed teammates, or betrayed their former guilds, finding a new home among the lawless.
- **Exiled Nobles and Bastards** – Runaway aristocrats who have embraced a life of bloodshed and infamy.
- **Those Who Fear Nothing** – Some simply join because they want to see how far they can go before the Labyrinths break them.

To the Crimson Reavers, it does not matter who you were before.

Only who you are when the battle begins.

## **Relationship with Other Governing Bodies**

### **Hunted by the Halvaskian Delving Authority (HDA)**

- The HDA considers the Crimson Reavers a dangerous rogue guild, yet they cannot outlaw them outright, as their expeditions are technically legal.
- The Delver Marshals have standing orders to arrest or execute rogue Reavers operating outside of sanctioned Labyrinth dives.
- However, the HDA also secretly allows them to exist, as their reckless approach often leads to the discovery of artifacts that structured guilds would take years to find.

### **Exploited by the Grand Scrying Network (GSN)**

- The GSN thrives on the Reavers' brutality, broadcasting their most violent battles for record-breaking audiences.
- Some Reavers become Livecast celebrities, with personal sponsors who encourage their bloodlust.
- However, the ABA (Arcane Broadcast Authority) carefully edits their footage, ensuring certain moments are "enhanced" for entertainment value.

### **Enemies of the Argent Banner**

- The Argent Banner sees the Crimson Reavers as dishonorable killers, viewing them as a stain on the legacy of Delving.
- The Reavers actively sabotage and steal from Argent Banner expeditions, taking artifacts meant for noble-backed warriors.
- Many duels and blood feuds exist between members of both guilds, leading to open street battles in Halvask’s underground districts.**

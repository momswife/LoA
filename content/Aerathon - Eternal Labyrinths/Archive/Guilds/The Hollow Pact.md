## **Overview: The Hollow Pact**

**Motto:** _"What is lost must be remembered. What is hidden must be uncovered."_  
**Headquarters:** **Veilshroud Expanse, The Obsidian Archive**  
**Guild Type:** Scholarly, Arcane Research, and Forbidden Knowledge Seekers  
**Notable Members:**

- **Scholar-Magus Illithane Valros** (Master Historian, Head of The Hollow Pact)
- **Whisperer Therion** (Seer of Lost Paths, Diviner of Forbidden Truths)
- **Archivist Malcera Vayne** (Scholar of Anomalous Artifacts and Reality Shifts)

The Hollow Pact is not a guild of warriors, mercenaries, or treasure-seekers—it is a collective of scholars, diviners, and arcane researchers dedicated to the study and understanding of the Labyrinths.

Unlike other Delver guilds, which seek gold, fame, or glory, the Hollow Pact exists to uncover the truth, no matter how forbidden or dangerous that truth may be.

- **Knowledge First, Survival Second** – Members of the Hollow Pact believe the Labyrinths hold forgotten knowledge that must be recovered, regardless of the risks involved.
- **Guardians of Forbidden Truths** – They are not only explorers but custodians, ensuring certain discoveries never fall into the wrong hands.
- **A Shadow Between Academia and Heresy** – While many scholars admire them, others whisper that the Hollow Pact is playing with forces beyond mortal comprehension.

To some, they are the last hope of understanding the Labyrinths.

To others, they are reckless seekers of ruin.

---

## **Structure & Leadership**

The Hollow Pact operates less like a traditional guild and more like an esoteric order, with ranked scholars, archivists, and field researchers.

### **The Inner Circle (High Council of The Hollow Pact)**

The highest ranking members are known as the Whisperers, individuals who have been touched by the Labyrinths in ways that cannot be fully explained.

|**Title**|**Name**|**Field of Study**|
|---|---|---|
|**Master Historian of the Hollow Pact**|**Scholar-Magus Illithane Valros**|Oversees all research and decisions on recovered knowledge.|
|**Whisperer of Lost Paths**|**Therion the Listener**|Studies Labyrinth geography, shifting corridors, and non-Euclidean spaces.|
|**Archivist of Anomalous Relics**|**Malcera Vayne**|Manages the recovery, classification, and containment of artifacts.|
|**Seer of Forbidden Truths**|**Orwyn the Veil-Walker**|Specializes in prophecy, dream-divination, and scrying hidden dangers.|
|**Guardian of the Sealed Vaults**|**Lucian Ravel**|Enforces internal secrecy, ensuring that certain knowledge remains hidden.|

While the Inner Circle controls the direction of research, the majority of the Hollow Pact consists of field scholars, scribes, and artifact retrievers, all working to unravel the mysteries of the Labyrinths.

## **What the Hollow Pact Offers**

Joining the Hollow Pact is not a choice made for wealth or prestige, but rather a commitment to the pursuit of truth.

### **Guild Benefits**

- **Access to the Obsidian Archive** – The largest repository of Labyrinth-related knowledge, filled with ancient texts, recovered records, and restricted tomes.
- **The Rite of Memory** – Members undergo a mental fortification ritual, granting them resistance against the mind-warping effects of the Labyrinths.
- **Artifact Study & Research** – Unlike other guilds, which turn relics over to the HDA, the Hollow Pact preserves and studies them independently.
- **Divination & Prophecy** – Senior members receive guidance through rituals, dreams, and cryptic messages from the past.
- **Anonymity & Protection** – Many members use false names and hidden identities, ensuring that outside forces cannot manipulate or silence them.

Unlike other Delver guilds, which seek to conquer the Labyrinths, the Hollow Pact understands a darker truth:

The Labyrinths do not want to be conquered. They want to be understood.

---

## **Who Joins the Hollow Pact?**

The Hollow Pact **does not recruit warriors, mercenaries, or spectacle-seekers**. Instead, it seeks **those who are willing to embrace knowledge, no matter the cost.**

- **Scholars & Historians** – Those who seek lost knowledge, unearthing forbidden history buried within the Labyrinths.
- **Arcane Researchers & Diviners** – Those with a gift for magic and prophecy, willing to decipher the cryptic messages hidden within the Labyrinth walls.
- **Survivors of Labyrinth-Induced Madness** – Some members are former Delvers who have witnessed horrors beyond reason, seeking to understand what they saw.
- **Deniers of the Grand Lie** – There are those who believe that the true nature of the Labyrinths has been hidden by the ruling authorities—and the Hollow Pact is the only group seeking the truth.

---

## **Relationship with Other Governing Bodies**

### **Uneasy Alliance with the Halvaskian Delving Authority (HDA)**

- The HDA tolerates the Hollow Pact, but deeply distrusts its independence.
- The Pact’s refusal to turn over every relic they recover has led to repeated confrontations with the HDA’s artifact enforcers.
- Certain HDA officials believe the Hollow Pact is hiding dangerous knowledge, and some wish to shut them down permanently.

### **Scholarly Partnership with the Arcane Guild of Labyrinth Studies (AGLS)**

- The Hollow Pact and the AGLS share knowledge, though the Hollow Pact operates outside of academic bureaucracy.
- Some AGLS researchers have secretly defected to the Hollow Pact, believing that the Guild is willing to study what the AGLS fears.

### **Hostility with the Arcane Broadcast Authority (ABA)**

- The ABA has repeatedly censored Hollow Pact discoveries, fearing that certain truths would destabilize society.
- Some Pact researchers have disappeared, leading many to suspect ABA involvement.
- The Pact openly opposes the censorship of Delver Livecasts, believing that if the world saw what was truly inside the Labyrinths, everything would change.

---

## **The Pact’s Darkest Secret: The Sealed Vaults**

While the Hollow Pact seeks truth, there are some truths that even they fear.

Deep within the Obsidian Archive, there are vaults containing knowledge too dangerous to be released.

- Lost Records of the First Labyrinth – An account of what was found in the deepest depths of Labyrinth #1, The Argent Spire—and why its final level was sealed shut.
- The Echoing Name – A name recovered from a forgotten Labyrinth, which no one can remember after reading it.
- The Labyrinth That Was Erased – Proof that there was once a 22nd Labyrinth—and that it was somehow removed from history itself.

Some within the Pact believe these truths must remain buried.

Others believe they are the key to understanding everything.

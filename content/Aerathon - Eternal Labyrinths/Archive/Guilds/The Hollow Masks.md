## **Overview: The Hollow Masks**

**Motto:** _"Identity is a burden. Wear a better one."_  
**Headquarters:** **Unknown (Rumored to Operate in the Veilshroud Expanse, the Umbral Dominion, and Halvask’s Criminal Underground)**  
**Guild Type:** Espionage, Disguises, Information Manipulation, and Rogue Delver Syndicate  
**Notable Members:**

- **"The Nameless"** (Suspected Guildmaster, true identity unknown)
- **"Mother Midnight"** (Master of Disguises and Forgeries)
- **"The Painted Man"** (Elite Assassin and Silent Enforcer)

The Hollow Masks are not a guild of warriors, scholars, or mercenaries—they are an enigma, a whisper in the dark, a conspiracy given flesh.

No one knows when they were founded or who truly leads them, only that they exist within the shadows, controlling information, stealing faces, and ensuring that certain truths are never uncovered.

- **Masters of Disguise & False Identities** – The Hollow Masks can be anyone, anywhere, at any time.
- **Infiltrators & Information Brokers** – They specialize in manipulating records, altering historical accounts, and erasing or fabricating Delver identities.
- **Erasers of the Forgotten** – Some whisper that the Hollow Masks work with the Arcane Broadcast Authority (ABA), ensuring that certain Livecast footage never reaches the public.

To the Halvaskian authorities, they are a criminal menace.

To the wealthy elite, they are a secret weapon.

To those who disappear into the Labyrinths and return changed, they are the last hope of escaping a past that no longer belongs to them.

## **Structure & Leadership**

The Hollow Masks operate with no central leadership—or at least, none that anyone can confirm.

Their structure is intentionally obscured, ensuring that even members do not always know who they truly serve.

## **What the Hollow Masks Offer**

Unlike guilds that seek power, glory, or wealth, the Hollow Masks offer something more intangible—freedom from the past.

### **Guild Benefits**

- **New Identities & Vanished Pasts** – Members who join the Hollow Masks abandon their former names, faces, and histories, adopting completely new identities.
- **Mastery of Disguise & Illusion** – Every Mask learns how to alter their voice, posture, mannerisms, and magical aura, ensuring that no one ever knows who they truly are.
- **Protection from the Authorities** – Those who betray noble families, flee criminal syndicates, or carry forbidden knowledge can vanish into the Hollow Masks and never be found.
- **Control Over Information** – The Hollow Masks have agents embedded within noble courts, government agencies, and the Grand Scrying Network, ensuring that what they want to be true becomes reality.
- **The Right to be Forgotten** – In a world where Livecasting makes Delvers famous overnight, the Hollow Masks can erase you from history.

---

## **Who Joins the Hollow Masks?**

The **Hollow Masks do not recruit**—they **find you when you need them**.

- **Runaways & Exiles** – Those who **cannot return to their former lives**.
- **Survivors of Labyrinth Corruption** – Those who **have been changed by the Labyrinths** and need **a new identity before the HDA finds them.**
- **Disillusioned Nobles & Disgraced Officials** – Those who **wish to erase their past sins and forge a new path.**
- **Killers & Ghosts** – Assassins, spies, and infiltrators who **no longer wish to be known.**
- **The Lost & The Forgotten** – Those who **have no past to return to, no future to claim, and nothing left except the mask they wear.**

Unlike **other guilds**, the Hollow Masks do not **ask for loyalty or oaths**—only that you **never remove the mask once it is worn.**

## **Relationship with Other Governing Bodies**

### **Feared & Hunted by the Halvaskian Delving Authority (HDA)**

- The HDA sees the Hollow Masks as a major threat, as they erase criminal records, falsify documents, and hide fugitives.
- Some suspect that certain high-ranking HDA officials are actually Hollow Masks, operating from within to manipulate Delving records.
- The Delver Marshals have standing kill orders for known Hollow Mask operatives, but they have never successfully captured one alive.

### **A Shadowed Alliance with the Arcane Broadcast Authority (ABA)**

- The ABA denies working with the Hollow Masks, but suspiciously, certain censored Livecast footage aligns with the guild’s interests.
- Some whisper that the Hollow Masks serve as the ABA’s “cleaners”, ensuring that certain Delvers disappear before they can say too much.
- Some Delvers who exposed secrets during Livecasts have vanished overnight—only for figures wearing Hollow Masks to be seen in the area.

### **Mysterious Connections to The Gilded Shadows**

- Some believe that the Hollow Masks and the Gilded Shadows are connected, with the Shadows stealing artifacts and the Masks ensuring they remain hidden.
- However, others suspect that the two factions are rivals, as some stolen relics have the power to reveal hidden truths.

One thing is certain—if the Hollow Masks do not want you to be found, no one will ever find you.

---

## **The Guild’s Darkest Secret: The Maskless Ones**

While every member of the Hollow Masks wears a face, there are whispers of those who lose themselves completely.

- The Maskless Ones – Those who have worn too many faces, spoken too many false names, and lost all sense of self.
- It is said they wander the deepest parts of the Labyrinths, shifting from one identity to the next, no longer bound to a single existence.
- Some believe that The Nameless—their supposed leader—is not a person at all, but a Maskless One who forgot they were ever human.

If this is true, then what happens to those who remain in the Hollow Masks too long?

Do they become something more?

Or do they simply cease to be?

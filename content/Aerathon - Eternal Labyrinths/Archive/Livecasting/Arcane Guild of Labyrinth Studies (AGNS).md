## **Overview: The Arcane Guild of Labyrinth Studies (AGLS)**

The Arcane Guild of Labyrinth Studies (AGLS) is the premier research institution dedicated to the study, documentation, and theoretical control of the Labyrinths of Aerathon. Unlike the Halvaskian Delving Authority (HDA), which seeks to regulate and contain Delving operations, the AGLS focuses on unlocking the mysteries of the Labyrinths themselves, pursuing scientific and arcane discovery over bureaucratic control.

Originally founded as a scholarly institution dedicated to mapping and understanding the earliest Labyrinths, the AGLS has since grown into a powerful academic and magical force, wielding influence over governments, Delver guilds, and secretive research projects.

Though the Guild’s stated mission is to study the Labyrinths for the benefit of Aerathon, many suspect that their true goals are far more dangerous, with rumors circulating about forbidden experiments, secret artifact vaults, and knowledge hidden from the world for reasons unknown.

The AGLS operates more like a university than a government agency, with multiple divisions specializing in different aspects of Labyrinth research.

### **The Five Research Divisions**

The AGLS is divided into five primary divisions, each responsible for a specific aspect of Labyrinth study:

1. **Department of Arcane Phenomena** – Investigates how the Labyrinths function magically, including their apparent self-generation, time dilation effects, and reality-bending properties.
2. **Department of Artifacts & Relic Containment** – Studies and classifies artifacts recovered from Labyrinths, deciding which may be publicly examined and which must be hidden.
3. **Department of Theoretical Control** – Works on containing, manipulating, or potentially controlling Labyrinth entrances through advanced spellcraft.
4. **Department of Mapping & Dimensional Analysis** – Specializes in navigating and recording Labyrinth structures, working alongside Delvers to map their depths.
5. **Department of Ethics & Anomalous Threats** – Supposedly ensures responsible research, though some believe this division exists solely to silence dangerous discoveries.

## **Research & Areas of Study**

Unlike the HDA, which focuses on regulating Delving as an industry, the AGLS is obsessed with uncovering the truth about the Labyrinths. Their primary fields of study include:

### **The Origins of the Labyrinths**

- Were the Labyrinths constructed by an ancient civilization, or are they naturally occurring phenomena?
- Do the Labyrinths have a purpose, or are they random constructs of chaotic magic?
- Why do the Labyrinths seem to shift, grow, and consume surrounding land over time?

### **The Labyrinths as Living Entities**

- Some scholars propose that the Labyrinths are not merely structures, but living, conscious entities.
- Certain Labyrinths appear to “respond” to Delvers, altering their layouts in real time.
- Do the Labyrinths consume those who enter, or do they “repurpose” them?

### **The Preservation & Destruction of Knowledge**

- The AGLS has recovered historical texts, forgotten languages, and records of lost civilizations within the Labyrinths.
- Some documents predate all known history, suggesting that Aerathon’s timeline may be incomplete—or falsified.
- The Grand Arcanist has personally sealed certain records, claiming that some truths “would only invite ruin.”

### **Theories on the Nameless Labyrinth**

With the appearance of the Nameless Labyrinth in Velgrove Square, the AGLS has rushed to be the first to document its properties. Theories include:

- It is the first naturally forming Labyrinth within a city, suggesting that the Labyrinths are “spreading” rather than appearing randomly.
- It may be a convergence point, linking multiple Labyrinths into a singular entity.
- Some scholars believe it is incomplete—meaning that it is still growing, and what lies within is only the beginning.

## **Relationship with Other Governing Bodies**

### **Conflict with the Halvaskian Delving Authority (HDA)**

- The HDA treats Delving as a controlled industry, while the AGLS seeks unrestricted research access.
- Many scholars resent the HDA’s monopoly over Labyrinth expeditions, believing science is being suppressed in favor of bureaucracy.
- The HDA has repeatedly confiscated artifacts the AGLS deems necessary for study, leading to accusations of corruption and hoarding.

### **Alliance (and Rivalry) with the Grand Scrying Network (GSN)**

- The AGLS and the GSN collaborate on Livecasting Delver expeditions, using broadcasts as public research opportunities.
- However, the GSN often prioritizes entertainment over scientific discovery, much to the AGLS’s frustration.
- The AGLS has demanded the release of censored footage, arguing that the public has a right to know the full truth of the Labyrinths.

### **Dangerous Tensions with the Order of Iron Ascendants**

- The Iron Ascendants believe that all Labyrinths must be destroyed, branding the AGLS as heretical for studying them.
- The Order has openly threatened AGLS scholars, calling them "corruptors of fate" who risk dooming the world.
- Certain high-ranking AGLS members have disappeared under suspicious circumstances, leading some to suspect the Ascendants are assassinating key researchers.

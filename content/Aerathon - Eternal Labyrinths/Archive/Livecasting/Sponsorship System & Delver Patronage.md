## **Overview: The Modern Sponsorship System & Labyrinth Assistance**

The era of Delving has changed.

Once, Delvers entered the Labyrinths with only what they carried on their backs. Now? They enter with the full force of corporate, noble, and magical sponsorship at their side—provided they can afford it.

The rise of Livecasting, high-stakes competitive Delving, and the Grand Scrying Network (GSN) has transformed sponsorship into a real-time, high-profile system where sponsors don’t just fund Delvers before an expedition—they actively support them _inside_ the Labyrinths.

Through the use of Divine Oculi, Arcane Supply Drops, and Customizable Sponsor Showcases, modern Delvers can request, receive, and advertise sponsorships while mid-expedition, giving them the tools they need to survive while maximizing entertainment value for viewers.

- Delvers who attract top-tier sponsors gain access to life-saving interventions, emergency weapons, and tactical advantages.
- Sponsors compete to have their products showcased in the deadliest environments, ensuring their brand remains in the spotlight.
- Audience members and investors can watch in real time, making high-stakes sponsorship deals _live_ based on performance.

For some Delvers, this is a godsend—proof that survival isn’t just about skill, but about _who backs you_.

For others, it’s a dangerous game—one where corporate interests decide who lives and who dies.

---

## **🏛️ How Sponsorship Works Inside the Labyrinths**

Sponsorships are no longer just about funding before an expedition—they now offer real-time assistance during a Delve.

This is done through three core methods:

### **1. The Divine Oculi (Livecasting & Sponsor Interaction)**

At the heart of modern sponsorship is the Divine Oculi, a floating, arcane scry-orb that follows Delvers as they navigate the Labyrinths.

- These enchanted orbs act as Livecasting cameras, broadcasting every moment to the Grand Scrying Network.
- Sponsors can communicate with Delvers through the Oculi, offering deals, challenges, and tactical assistance.
- Oculi are indestructible and magically linked to a Delver’s sponsorship contract.

💬 EXAMPLE SPONSOR INTERACTION _(Captured from a Livecast of the Sunken Maw Expedition, Year 940)_

**[Supply Drop Incoming…]**

The Oculi ensures that sponsors remain integrated into the Delver’s journey—and that their brand gets the attention it deserves.

### **📦 2. Arcane Supply Drops (Real-Time Item Assistance)**

Sponsors have access to a magical delivery system that allows them to teleport items, weapons, and consumables directly to their Delvers during expeditions.

- Limited Supply Slots: Delvers may only receive a set number of supply drops per expedition, preventing abuse.
- "Branded" Equipment: Any gear received via sponsorship must bear the sponsor’s emblem and name, ensuring visibility for Livecast audiences.
- Live Contract Negotiations: Some sponsors require Delvers to perform tasks, make endorsements, or accept long-term deals before sending aid.

**EXAMPLES OF SPONSORSHIP SUPPLY DROPS**

| **Sponsor**                | **Item Sent**                       | **Conditions**                                                                        |
| -------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Velmire Weaponworks**    | A **Runebound Greatsword**          | Must be wielded on camera for at least **five minutes**.                              |
| **Aurum Arcana Potions**   | **A Full Restoration Elixir**       | Delver must state "Aurum Arcana, the taste of true power!" before drinking.           |
| **Silverhand Expeditions** | **A Tactical Map of the Labyrinth** | Delver must publicly thank Silverhand and sign a long-term contract for future dives. |

**Risk & Strategy:**

- Some sponsors offer genuinely useful gear—while others provide flashy but impractical items meant only for entertainment.
- Rogue Delvers sometimes reject sponsor assistance entirely, choosing to remain independent at the cost of survival.
- A sponsor may withdraw assistance if a Delver’s performance hurts their brand.

### **3. Sponsor Showcases & Branded Challenges**

In addition to gear and assistance, sponsors often introduce "Brand Challenges", requiring Delvers to complete staged objectives in exchange for better rewards.

These challenges are often meant to increase audience engagement while testing a Delver’s ability to adapt to unexpected variables.

🎭 **EXAMPLES OF SPONSOR CHALLENGES**

| **Sponsor**                                       | **Challenge**                                                          | **Reward**                                 |
| ------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| **The Argent Banner (Elite Backers)**             | Defeat a notorious Labyrinth guardian while wearing their custom armor | A suit of enchanted, high-end combat gear. |
| **The Grand House of Valcathis (Merchant Elite)** | Complete a puzzle room using only Valcathis-brand tools                | A rare magical lockpicking set.            |
| **Crimson Reavers (Bloodsport Investors)**        | Engage in a dramatic one-on-one duel against another Delver            | A substantial monetary bonus.              |

**THE DOWNSIDE:**

- Some challenges are borderline suicidal, forcing Delvers into dangerous situations for the sake of entertainment.
- Rejecting a challenge may result in losing all sponsorship support for the rest of the expedition.
- Some rogue sponsors offer "under-the-table" challenges, rewarding Delvers for sabotaging rivals or stealing relics outside of regulation.

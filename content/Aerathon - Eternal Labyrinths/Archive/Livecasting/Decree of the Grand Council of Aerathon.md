## **THE PRESERVATION OF ORDER AND CONTROL OF LIVECASTED MATERIALS**

### **BY ORDER OF THE GRAND COUNCIL OF AERATHON, LET IT BE KNOWN:**

With the rise of Livecasting as the dominant form of public spectacle and historical documentation, it has become evident that certain dangers lie in the unrestricted preservation of recorded Labyrinth transmissions.

Therefore, effective immediately, all Livecasting footage of Delver expeditions—regardless of location, nature, or outcome—shall be subject to the following restrictions:

---

## **ARTICLE I – LIVECASTING IS TO BE VIEWED ONLY IN REAL-TIME**

- Livecasting exists for the purpose of immediate viewing. It is to be experienced as it happens, preserving the raw spectacle, the emotion, and the uncertainty of fate.
- Any unauthorized recording, reproduction, or redistribution of Livecasted footage is hereby outlawed, punishable by severe legal action, including financial penalties, exile, or imprisonment.

---

## **ARTICLE II – ALL RECORDED FOOTAGE TO BE ARCHIVED & RESTRICTED**

- All recorded footage from Divine Oculi will be secured within the Grand Archive of Halvask, accessible only under explicit governmental and arcane authority.
- No private entity, noble house, or individual may request access to, purchase, or distribute recorded Delving footage without direct clearance from the High Tribunal of Halvask.

---

## **ARTICLE III – UNAUTHORIZED POSSESSION IS A CRIME AGAINST THE STATE**

- The possession, sale, or copying of any Delving recordings, unedited transmissions, or raw arcane data is hereby classified as a Level II Federal Offense under the Laws of Scrying and Arcane Transmission Regulation.
- Individuals found guilty of unauthorized possession will face confiscation of all scrying devices, a lifetime ban from Livecast viewership, and potential imprisonment.

---

## **ARTICLE IV – EXCEPTIONS FOR GOVERNMENTAL AND SCHOLARLY USE**

- Select footage may be reviewed under restricted conditions for tactical, arcane, or historical study.
- Clearance may only be granted to official government archivists, sanctioned arcane scholars, and licensed investigative authorities.
- Any misuse of restricted footage—including but not limited to unauthorized leaks, personal gain, or manipulation of events—will be met with the highest severity of legal and magical consequence.

---

## **ARTICLE V – FINALITY OF LAW & THE NECESSITY OF CONTROL**

- The Grand Council of Aerathon decrees that history is to be controlled, not left to chaos.
- Livecasting is an instrument of unity, not an archive of past horrors.
- What has been seen in the moment is enough. What is buried shall remain buried.

Thus, let this decree stand as law, unchallenged and absolute.

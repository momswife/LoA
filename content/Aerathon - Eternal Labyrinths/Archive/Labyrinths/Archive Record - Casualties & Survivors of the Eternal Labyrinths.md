_Transcribed from the Grand Archives of Halvask, 7th Era, Year 943_

_(Below is the official record of Delvers who have entered each known Labyrinth, including confirmed casualties and survivors. Due to the nature of certain Labyrinths, some numbers are estimated or incomplete. Discrepancies in records are noted where applicable.)_

---

### **1. The Nameless Labyrinth** _(Halvask – Appeared at Velgrove Square)_

- **Casualties:** _Uncountable_ (Entire marketplace erased; survivors do not remember those lost.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _Unknown_ (Some citizens reported "feeling displaced" but had no memory of what transpired.)

---

### **2. The Black Star Descent** _(Stormcall Isles – The Abyss That Watches)_

- **Casualties:** _32_ (Official expeditions lost, along with at least _four ships_ that vanished into the abyss.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _1_ (Captain Seraphine Vex, found aboard her drifting vessel with no memory of her crew.)

---

### **3. The Vault of Forgotten Crowns** _(Titan Highlands – The Throne of the Dead)_

- **Casualties:** _17_ (Four expedition leaders, thirteen royal seekers. All found seated upon empty thrones, lifeless.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _4_ (One survivor returned unable to recall their own name. Another refused to speak at all.)

---

### **4. The Clockwork Tomb** _(Halvask – The Machine That Waits)_

- **Casualties:** _7_ (From Rhys Valden’s infamous expedition. Five murdered, one crushed by shifting gears, one driven to madness.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _1_ (A scholar from the expedition returned, but their writings became illegible within weeks.)

---

### **5. The Twilight Bastion** _(Veilshroud Expanse – The Throne of Shadows)_

- **Casualties:** _14_ (Seven groups failed the trial, never returning from the fortress halls.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _5_ (The Twilight Vow, who took the throne together.)

---

### **6. The Ashen Labyrinth** _(Sunfire Expanse – The Smoldering Trial)_

- **Casualties:** _Unconfirmed_ (Remains found, but unable to be identified due to complete incineration.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _3_ (Vaelrik Dawnpiercer among them, emerging covered in golden scars.)

---

### **7. The Labyrinth of the Faceless** _(Umbral Dominion – The Masked Nightmare)_

- **Casualties:** _Uncounted_ (Some who enter are erased entirely, their names and records vanishing.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _Varies_ (Many emerge maskless and voiceless, their identities unrecoverable.)

---

### **8. The Black Star Descent** _(Stormcall Isles – The Abyss That Watches)_

- **Casualties:** _Undetermined_ (Abyss swallows those who fall. No bodies ever recovered.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _1_ (Seraphine Vex, found adrift with blackened eyes, whispering in a foreign tongue.)

---

### **9. The Radiant Crucible** _(Sunfire Expanse – The Trial of Ascension)_

- **Casualties:** _41_ (Those unworthy were reduced to cinders; no remains ever recovered.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _1_ (Vaelrik Dawnpiercer, the only known conqueror.)

---

### **10. The Hollow God's Sepulcher** _(Whispering Wilds – The Buried Deity)_

- **Casualties:** _12_ (The entire Verdant Priesthood expedition.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _1_ (A lone acolyte, found wandering the forest, unresponsive.)

---

### **11. The Vault of Eternity** _(Titan Highlands – The Forge of Gods)_

- **Casualties:** _23_ (Believed to have been undone by their own greed, fates unknown.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _2_ (One survivor refused to part with an artifact they retrieved and was later found dead, turned to gold.)

---

### **12. The Silver Ruin** _(Frostlands – The Frozen Metropolis)_

- **Casualties:** _28_ (Frozen upon return, their hearts still beating under layers of ice.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _1_ (A Delver who returned blind, muttering in an unknown dialect.)

---

### **13. The Howling Maw** _(Titan Highlands – The Battlefield of the Damned)_

- **Casualties:** _>400 lives lost_ (Those who fall here never leave; they simply continue fighting. Expeditions recover new corpses, but no one can determine how long they have been there.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _4_ (All ended up dead or insane within weeks of escaping.)

---

### **14. The Hollow Garden** _(Veilshroud Expanse – The Maze That Remembers)_

- **Casualties:** _Unknown_ (Some names vanish from memory after entry; others return altered.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _3_ (All three had missing memories and new, unexplained scars.)

---

### **15. The Drowned Catacombs** _(Tempest Sea – The Sunken Necropolis)_

- **Casualties:** _19_ (Pulled beneath the water by unseen hands.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _1_ (Returned waterlogged, their lungs filled with seawater despite never submerging.)

---

### **16. The Gilded Labyrinth** _(Sunfire Expanse – The Temple of Endless Gold)_

- **Casualties:** _32_ (Bodies found covered in gold plating, hands still clutching coins.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _3_ (Two later transformed into solid statues; the last refused to touch wealth again.)

---

### **17. The Black Vein** _(Titan Highlands – The Mountain That Breathes)_

- **Casualties:** _12_ (Swallowed into walls that were not stone, screaming until silenced.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _2_ (Both refused to describe what they saw.)

---

### **18. The Forsaken Atelier** _(Halvask – The Workshop of the Forgotten)_

- **Casualties:** _14_ (Absorbed into unfinished constructs, their bodies fused into the walls.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _5_ (Kaelis Arvendis and his team, the only known conquerors.)

---

### **19. The Shattered Monastery** _(Titan Highlands – The Trial of the Ascended)_

- **Casualties:** _22_ (Fell into the abyss; their bodies never recovered.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _2_ (Sarina Lumehart and Kiel Vaelor, known as the Twin Stars—both disappeared beyond the final path.)

---

### **20. The Crimson Labyrinth** _(Unknown – The Labyrinth That Grows)_

- **Casualties:** _Uncounted_ (Each time it reappears, it is larger—feeding on those who entered.)
- **Confirmed Survivors:** _0_ (None have returned.)

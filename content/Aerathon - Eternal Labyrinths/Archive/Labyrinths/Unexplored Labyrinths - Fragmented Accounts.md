_Transcribed from the Grand Archives of Halvask, 7th Era, Year 943_

_(The following entries consist of scattered reports, lost journal excerpts, and fragmented notes recovered by scholars and Delvers. None of these Labyrinths have been fully explored, and their true nature remains unknown.)_

---

### **21. The Eclipsed Athenaeum** _(Unknown – The Library That Erases)_

_(Recovered scrap of parchment, found on the doorstep of the Halvask Grand Archives, unsigned and half-burned at the edges):_

- _"I went searching for a book. Instead, the book found me."_
- _"The halls have no end, the shelves no order. You do not find knowledge here. It finds you, and it chooses what you deserve to know."_
- _"I tried to leave, but the door was gone. I wrote my name in the margins of a book, hoping to anchor myself. When I looked again, my name had been replaced with someone else’s."_

_(An unknown script, written in gold ink across the last remaining page of a book with all others torn out):_  
**"THE LIBRARY REMEMBERS. DO YOU?"**

---

### **22. The Obsidian Mire** _(Tempest Sea – The Black Marsh Below)_

- _"The water isn’t water. Don’t drink it. Don’t touch it."_
- _"The ship’s anchor caught on something. We pulled it up. There was a hand still clutching the chain."_
- _(Last recorded message from the crew of The Azure Tide):_ **"The bubbles... they aren't from us. They're from beneath."**

---

### **23. The Hollow God’s Chamber** _(Whispering Wilds – The Unfinished Shrine)_

- _"Statues kneeling, heads bowed. No names on the altars."_
- _"We placed an offering. The statues raised their heads."_
- _"The carvings weren’t warnings. They were instructions."_
- _(Final words of a dying Delver, found outside the shrine):_ **"It’s still hungry."**

---

### **24. The Glass Labyrinth** _(Halvask – The Reflection That Watches)_

- _"We broke a panel. The reflection didn’t shatter. It kept looking at us."_
- _"There are more people in the mirrors than entered the Labyrinth."_
- _(Found scratched into glass near the entrance):_ **"DO NOT BLINK."**

---

### **25. The Laughing Carnival** _(Unknown – The Circus That Never Leaves)_

_(A crumpled ticket stub, found tucked into the sleeve of a long-dead traveler, the ink shifting between legibility and nonsense):_

- _"The show never ends. The audience never leaves. And yet, no one remembers how they arrived."_
- _"The tents stretch too high, their stripes twisting into spirals that hurt to look at."_
- _"The Ringmaster smiled at me. So did the clowns. So did the acrobats. So did the lions. So did the mirrors."_

_(Painted in peeling red on the back of a wooden sign, found half-buried in the sand miles away from any settlement):_  
**"ONE SHOW ONLY. FOREVER."**

---

### **26. The Crimson Orchard** _(Veilshroud Expanse – The Trees That Grow in Circles)_

_(A tattered note found pinned to a low-hanging branch with a bone knife):_

- _"The fruit is safe to eat. It is the pits you must fear."_
- _"The trees shift at night. We marked them, but by morning, the carvings had moved. Or perhaps the trees had."_
- _"We found a clearing, a perfect ring of fallen petals. Something was waiting there, but we did not stay to see what it was."_

_(Carved into the bark of the oldest tree, half-buried beneath layers of growth):_  
**"Plant a seed, take a life. That is the rule. That is the cost."**

---

### **27. The Veil Between** _(Unknown – The Labyrinth That Is Not There)_

- _(Recovered from a Delver who returned catatonic, written in blood on his own palms):_  
    **"Not a place. A mistake."**
- _"A man walked into an alley. He didn’t come out the other side."_
- _(Sailor’s account from the Stormcall Isles):_  
    _"There was nothing there. But I could feel it. Like stepping toward a door I couldn’t see."_

---

### **28. The Wraithspire** _(Titan Highlands – The Tower of Forgotten Names)_

- _"The tower wasn’t there before. And then it was."_
- _(Carved into a stone near the base):_ **"No one built this."**
- _"We tried to reach the top. We walked for hours. The next time we looked out a window, we were still on the first floor."_

---

### **29. The Silent Chapel** _(Umbral Dominion – The Church Without Prayer)_

- _"No gods ever listened here. Only something else."_
- _(A priest entered. He never spoke again.)_
- _(Words found on a crumbling page in the altar book, the rest blank):_ **"Kneel. Do not speak. Do not ask. It already knows."**

---

### **30. The Rotting Spire** _(Whispering Wilds – The Tower That Should Have Fallen)_

- _(From an old woman in a nearby village, rocking in her chair, muttering):_  
    _"It’s been falling for a thousand years. One day, it will finish."_
- _"We touched the wood. It crumbled beneath our hands. But the tower still stood."_
- _(Scrawled on a broken beam inside the structure):_ **"Something still walks upstairs."**

---

### **31. The Devouring Garden** _(Sunfire Expanse – The Bloom That Hungers)_

- _"We heard the flowers breathing."_
- _"They bloom in footprints. By dawn, they covered the whole camp."_
- _(The last words of a botanist, found tangled in vines that had no root):_ **"I thought it was reaching for sunlight. It was reaching for me."**
_____

**🔖 Archivist’s Note:** These fragments are all that remain of the so-called **Unseen Labyrinths**—places that resist discovery, that erase their own records, or that exist in ways we do not yet understand.

*We cannot say how many Delvers have entered them.*

*We cannot say how many ever left.*

*Some of these entries may be **mistakes**. Misremembered legends. Fabrications from minds broken by what they saw.*

*But not all of them.*

*And perhaps one day, they will decide to be found.*

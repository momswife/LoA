## **Background & Reputation**

### **The Tracker Who Walked Too Far**

Sylra Moondrift was once one of the greatest Delvers of her generation, known for her near-supernatural ability to navigate Labyrinths others deemed impenetrable.

- An expert in tracking, stealth, and survival, she specialized in retrieving lost Delvers, mapping unknown routes, and avoiding detection.
- Could pass through even the deadliest sections of a Labyrinth unnoticed, moving as if the Labyrinth itself did not recognize her presence.
- Recruited by the Hollow Pact for her uncanny ability to uncover knowledge hidden within the depths of the Labyrinths.

Her skills made her a legend among Delvers, but also drew the attention of those who did not want certain secrets uncovered.

She should have never been able to return from the Twilight Bastion.

But she did.

And she was never the same again.

## **The Moondrift Incident 

### **The Mission**

Sylra was sent into Labyrinth #5 – The Twilight Bastion, a fortress-like Labyrinth filled with spectral knights, endless corridors, and shifting battlefields.

- The mission was to uncover forgotten routes within the Twilight Bastion, to prove that alternate paths could bypass its most dangerous battlefields.
- Sylra entered alone, armed only with a handful of supplies, her bow, and her wits.
- She returned four days later—alive, but different.

## **Aftermath & Cover-Up**

### **Official HDA Report (Year 937, Restricted Archives)**

- Sylra Moondrift was declared "mentally unfit for further expeditions" following her return from the Twilight Bastion.
- The Hollow Pact expelled her, citing "irreparable psychological damage and an unknown anomaly affecting her presence in reality."
- All footage of her final return was heavily censored or erased by the Arcane Broadcast Authority (ABA).
- Sylra vanished one month later—last seen stepping into the Umbral Dominion’s borderlands.

### **Theories & Unanswered Questions**

- How did she survive alone in the Twilight Bastion for four days?
- Why did the spectral knights not attack her?
- Why did she cast no shadow after her return?
- Where did she go after disappearing?

Some Delvers believe she became part of the Bastion itself.

Others whisper that she is still walking the Labyrinths today—but no longer bound by the rules of this world.

## **Sightings & Unexplained Events**

Though she is officially missing, strange reports have surfaced over the years.

- A figure resembling Sylra was seen moving through the Veilshroud Expanse—crossing a moonlit plain where no footprints remained.
- A shadow was recorded walking the halls of an abandoned ruin in the Titan Highlands—without a body to cast it.
- Delvers exploring the Twilight Bastion claim to hear her voice whispering through the ancient battlefields.

Some believe she is still out there, walking unseen, slipping between the cracks of reality.

Some say she has become something else entirely.

# **Veryiss-Spawns**

### _Horrors Born of a Forgotten Curse — “When a Sorcerer’s Malice Learns to Multiply.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — _The Living Atlas_ (Infernal, Demonic & Corrupted Entity Archive)  
**Primary Compiler:** Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturge & Curse-Lineage Analyst)  
**Advisory Editors:** Priest-Analyst Thera Daan (Faith Oversight), Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison), Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Echo-Law Historian)  
**Date of Filing:** 3734 A.D.  
**Document Class:** _Curse-Aberrant Record / Grade-V Combat & Cognitive Hazard_

---

## **I. Overview**

**Veryiss-Spawns** are grotesque curse-born entities created from the lingering, self-propagating malice of **Arch-Sorcerer Veryiss**, a pre-Spectacle era tyrant whose dying curse was so potent it survived the destruction of his body.

The original curse — meant to “unmake all who forget me” — fractured, metastasized, and fused itself to the land, birthing autonomous monsters formed from **aggregated hatred, ego, and remnants of his dying will**.

Each Spawn resembles a distorted echo of Veryiss’ own form: too many limbs, hollow regal masks, voices that argue with themselves, and bodies stitched from shadow, bone, and ragged memory.

The Ministry classifies them as **Type-INF / High-Order Curse Aberrants**, among the most dangerous non-intelligent horrors in Aerathon.

> _“They are not his children.  
> They are his temper tantrums made flesh.”_  
> — Stonevoice

---

## **II. Physical Description**

|**Attribute**|**Detail**|
|---|---|
|**Height**|8–12 ft (varies wildly)|
|**Body Structure**|Twisted humanoid: elongated spine, extra arms, backward limbs|
|**Skin**|Ash-grey, shadow-lacquered, cracked like burnt porcelain|
|**Mask/Face**|Porcelain-like mask carved into a cold, emotionless king’s visage; inner cavity is void|
|**Eyes**|None; instead fractured runes glow within the mask’s cracks|
|**Voice**|Three overlapping whispers constantly disagreeing|
|**Aura**|Localized distortion: nausea, dread, intrusive thoughts|
When damaged, they shed **curse-splinters**, shards of malignant memory that crawl like insects.

---

## **III. Behavioral Profile & Phenomena**

Veryiss-Spawns have no true sentience — only compulsive behaviors inherited from the dying mind of their creator:

**Documented Behaviors:**

- **Recognition Hunt:** They relentlessly pursue anyone who fails to “acknowledge” them (usually by not making eye contact with the mask).
    
- **Anti-Identity Reaction:** They attempt to erase things — names, symbols, runes — linked to people who resist them.
    
- **Voice Warping:** Victims hear their own self-criticisms spoken in Veryiss’ tone.
    
- **Memory Scarring:** Prolonged exposure causes memories about the Spawn encounter to rewrite themselves.
    
- **Crown-Claim Instinct:** They gather atop hills, ruins, or fallen statues — “claiming” thrones real or imagined.
    

> _“They crave validation, but punish those who give it.”_  
> — Daan

Spawns often fight themselves mid-pursuit, tearing off limbs or masks in mimicry of Veryiss’ own final moments.

---

## **IV. Habitat & Occurrence**

Veryiss-Spawns only appear in or near regions affected by the **Veryiss Curse-Line**, including:

- **Ashen Steppe north of Veyra** (primary emergence site)
    
- **Broken mage-tombs in the Malarthain hinterlands**
    
- **Old academy ruins sealed by magical amnesia wards**
    
- **Labyrinth segments exposed to curse-lightning events**
    
- **Battlefields where generals attempted mind-control magic**
    

The largest cluster — the **Throne of Dust** — sits atop Veryiss’ annihilated citadel, where Spawns gather like moths around a lingering ego.

---

## **V. Ministry Observations**

- Echo-seismographs detect each Spawn carries a **fragment of Veryiss’ mind**, but corrupted, incomplete, and looping.
    
- Some masks whisper his last words over and over; others invent new phrases that contradict each other.
    
- Lt. Korrin confirms their bodies contain impossible geometry — interior spaces larger than exterior, folding inward.
    
- Dr. Sallow found their curse-splinters capable of infecting inert objects, creating “Stillborn Spawns” that animate briefly.
    
- Destroying a Spawn releases a psychic shockwave: guilt, rage, and isolation projected in a 60-ft radius.
    

> _“They are ghosts of a man too proud to die.”_  
> — Stonevoice

## **VI. Weakness & Delving Considerations**

|**Vulnerability**|**Explanation / Practical Note**|
|---|---|
|**True Naming**|Speaking Veryiss’ **full true name** disrupts their coherence.|
|**Radiant Purification**|Celestial and dawn-aligned magic dissolves curse-splinters.|
|**Mirror Shields**|Reflecting their mask-image fractures their form.|
|**Counter-Ego Rituals**|Affirmations or unity chants reduce psychic aura.|
**Delver Advisory:**  
Never let a Spawn touch your face.  
This triggers an **identity-strip**, erasing your name from your own short-term memory.

Spawn attacks may implant curse-splinters beneath the skin — requiring immediate extraction and ward-burning.

## **VIII. Containment & Research Status**

- **Classification:** Level V — Major Curse Aberrant / Extreme Hazard
    
- **Containment:** Only partial; best strategy is **banishment rituals and radiant fields**.
    
- **Monitoring:** Scry-barriers around the Throne of Dust; curseflow sensors.
    
- **Active Projects:**  
     • _Operation Unmaking_ — Attempting to trace and dissolve the original Veryiss curse-core.  
     • _Project Ego-Void_ — Studying why the Spawns amplify insecurities.
    

---

## **IX. Cultural & Mythic Notes**

- **Veyran Farmers:** Avoid the Steppe entirely, calling it “The Land That Remembers Wrong.”
    
- **Delver Folklore:** If a mask cracks while watching you, a Spawn elsewhere now knows your name.
    
- **Blackwild Shamans:** Believe Veryiss tried to become a god through memory — and the earth punished him.
    
- **Bards of Al’ar:** Sing: _“The King of Ash had no heirs but hatred; now it marches on broken legs.”_
    

> _“Veryiss feared being forgotten.  
> The world remembers him now only as a monster — many times over.”_  
> — Stonevoice

---

## **X. Summary**

**Veryiss-Spawns** are the lingering tantrums of a dead tyrant — curse-made monsters that wear his form, voice, and ego in fractured parody.  
Driven by validation, pride, and hatred, they stalk the cursed landscape seeking acknowledgment from those who fear or despise them.

They are what happens when a dying sorcerer whispers a curse powerful enough to seed itself into the soil:

**One man’s malice, multiplied forever.**

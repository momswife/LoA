# **Tide-Heart Leviathan**

### _The Abyssal Pulse of Namira — “When the Sea Dreamed It Could Breathe Forever.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — _The Living Atlas_ (Marine Phenomena & Leviathanic Entities Archive)  
**Primary Compiler:** Researcher Falyn Onarra (Beastfolk Sociology & Adaptation Studies)  
**Advisory Editors:** Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison), Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturge), Curator Rook-of-Rings (Relics Registrar)  
**Date of Filing:** 3732 A.D. (Age of Spectacle)  
**Document Class:** _Natural Leviathan Record / Grade-V Containment Verified_

---

## **I. Overview**

The **Tide-Heart Leviathan** is a colossal pelagic organism and self-sustaining oceanic engine dwelling beneath the **Namiran Trench**, three miles below the surface.  
It is less an individual creature than a **living current**, its circulatory rhythm driving the ocean’s tides through pressure and pulse alone.

To the Namiran coastfolk it is **“Vaen Maru” — The Breath Between Waves.**  
To delvers, it is both myth and machinery: the ocean’s own heart, forged in the collapse of the first Labyrinth below the sea.

The Ministry classifies it as **Type-NAT / Elemental-Biologic Leviathan**, a being whose biology and hydromancy are indistinguishable.

> _“It does not swim through the sea; it is the sea choosing direction.”_ — Korrin

---

## **II. Physical Description**

|**Attribute**|**Detail**|
|---|---|
|**Length**|Est. 3,200 ft (known segment verified)|
|**Weight**|> 40 million tons|
|**Composition**|Silicate-reinforced cartilage sheath around bioluminescent fluid organ mass|
|**Coloration**|Midnight blue to green-white; pattern shifts with tides|
|**Eyes**|Six bioluminescent orbs that dim with low tide|
|**Heartbeat**|Single pulse every ninety seconds; causes detectable pressure ripple across Namiran coastline|
The body is ringed with **transparent fins** miles long that act as tidal regulators.  
Each exhalation vents superheated brine that crystallizes instantly — forming drifting **salt-glass reefs**.

---

## **III. Behavior & Phenomena**

The Leviathan remains motionless for decades, stirring only when planetary tides desynchronize.  
Its movement re-aligns global oceanic flow, accompanied by storms, whirlpools, and mana spikes across the coast.

**Recorded Behaviors**

- **Tidal Correction:** Every 50 years it breaches slightly; tsunami follows within 12 hours.
    
- **Whale Herd Summons:** Aquafauna flock around it, emitting synchronized songs to match its pulse.
    
- **Abyssal Bloom:** When wounded, sheds luminescent cells that grow into temporary reef-brains for weeks.
    
- **Song Memory:** Echo patterns of its call contain archaic Celestial phrases — possibly remnants of Lathander’s original tide hymn.
    

> _“Its breath moves nations, its silence drowns them.”_ — Stonevoice

---

## **IV. Habitat & Distribution**

Known to circle the Namiran Trench and southern Louvain current.  
Hydro-rune satellites suggest pressure pulses emanate from at least three locations — likely different segments of one body.  
Occasional surface manifestations include:

- **The Glass Whorl (3712):** A 40-mile circular vortex of boiling brine.
    
- **The Singing Fog (3720):** Low mist that vibrated with heartbeat frequency for nine days.
    

---

## **V. Ministry Observations**

Lt. Korrin’s Breach Division recorded persistent arcane resonance identical to heartbeat pattern inside Caer Dain’s forges — suggesting planetary link between Forge Wyrm and Leviathan through mantle mana flow.

Inspector Vell notes acoustic recordings of its pulse induce calm or hysteria depending on listener alignment with moon phase.  
Attempts to map its full shape via aetheric sonar resulted in instant instrument melt.

> _“We heard it blink and thought it thunder.”_ — Vell

> _“You can’t fight a heartbeat that owns the sea.”_ — Korrin

## **IX. Cultural & Mythic Notes**

- **Namiran Sailors:** Say the Leviathan’s breach begins each century of calm; throw coins overboard “to buy one more heartbeat.”
    
- **Delver Superstition:** A dream of drifting glass means the Leviathan has turned in its sleep.
    
- **Faith Orders of Melora:** Preach that its pulse is the last remnant of the Sea Mother’s heart.
    
- **Malarthain Engineers:** Time forge shifts by the interval of its beat; industrial metronome for creation.
    

> _“When it breathes out, the world exhales.  
> When it breathes in, we pray it remembers mercy.”_ — Stonevoice

---

## **X. Summary**

The **Tide-Heart Leviathan** is not monster, myth, or machine — it is **the ocean’s conscience**, a being whose heartbeat keeps Aerathon’s waters alive.  
It embodies patience on a planetary scale: the slow, endless rhythm between creation and collapse.  
To awaken it fully would mean the sea remembering it was once a god — and gods, when they remember, rarely forgive.

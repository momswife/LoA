# **Ash Herald**

### _Emissary of Burnt Gods — “Where the Flame Learned to Speak Without Heat.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — _Annals & Antiquities_ (Theological Aberration & Cataclysm Residue Archive)  
**Primary Compiler:** Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison & Spatial Phenomena Lead)  
**Advisory Editors:** Priest-Analyst Thera Daan (Faith Oversight), Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturge), Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Echo-Law Historian)  
**Date of Filing:** 3732 A.D. (Age of Spectacle)  
**Document Class:** _Divine Aberration Record / Grade-IV Containment Verified_

---

## **I. Overview**

The **Ash Herald** is a semi-corporeal entity that manifests amid total immolation — cities, shrines, or battlefields where belief and flame die together.  
It is said to be the **voice of forgotten fires**, a wandering sermon-keeper collecting the last prayers uttered in smoke.

The Ministry defines it as **Type-DIV / Pyro-Theurgical Aberration**, occupying the liminal space between divine messenger and elemental revenant.  
Eyewitnesses describe it as **a cloaked figure of drifting soot**, crowned by a faint ember halo that never rises higher than the heart.

> _“A Herald without a master — preaching the gospel of aftermath.”_ — Daan

---

## **II. Physical Description**

|**Attribute**|**Detail**|
|---|---|
|**Apparent Height**|6 ½ – 7 ft (oscillates with air currents)|
|**Composition**|Soot, carbon dust, residual spirit flame|
|**Temperature**|Ambient – cold to the touch|
|**Voice**|Crackling whisper, layered like distant fires|
|**Scent**|Charred incense & iron|
|**Luminance**|Pulses only when reciting names of the dead|
The Herald leaves **footprints of gray ash** that evaporate into floating runes. Each mark contains a phrase of apology in an extinct holy tongue.

---

## **III. Behavior & Phenomena**

- **Purpose Cycle:** Appears after large-scale fires or divine smiting; wanders perimeter three times before entering ruins’ center.
    
- **Ash Oration:** Emits sermons audible in the mind rather than ear, recounting the final prayers of the fallen.
    
- **Soul Catalyst:** Spirits nearby are pacified; corpses combust cleanly into sterile dust.
    
- **Faith Mimicry:** Can recite any prayer overheard once, tone perfectly reverent but void of belief.
    
- **Departure:** Dissipates into a column of drifting embers when directly addressed by its original god’s name — if that deity still exists.
    

> _“It doesn’t burn. It remembers burning.”_ — Vell

---

## **IV. Origin Hypotheses**

1. **Divine Echo Theory:** Residual messenger of Lathander, Kossuth, or Moradin trapped during post-Collapse firewall.
    
2. **Faith Vacuum Manifestation:** Belief displaced by tragedy coalesces into animate sermon pattern.
    
3. **Industrial Anthropogenesis:** Caer Dain foundry accidents show micro-Heralds forming from slag fumes, suggesting mortal replication.
    

---

## **V. Habitat & Distribution**

Sightings correlate with catastrophic immolations:

- **Verges of Ash:** Primary congregation site — seven manifestations since 3700.
    
- **Ruins of Temptest’s Wake:** Appears each midsummer, tracing circles of soot on glass dunes.
    
- **Porta Strega’s Black Quarter:** Recorded preaching to collapsed furnaces, repeating “_Rise again, but colder._”
    

Heralds avoid water, divine light, and laughter. Rain disperses them instantly.

> _“It cannot forgive you — only make you clean.”_ — Korrin

## **VIII. Containment & Observation**

- **Classification:** Level IV — Cognitive Divine Residue / Psychic Hazard.
    
- **Containment:** Impossible; event-based manifestation only.
    
- **Observation Protocol:** Scry from >500 ft; verbal response forbidden.
    
- **Active Projects:**  
     • _Project Censer_ — convert Herald ash into sermon-recording medium.  
     • _Operation Kindling_ — determine if Heralds can reignite divine conduits under controlled ritual.
    

---

## **IX. Cultural & Religious Notes**

- **Caer Dain Smiths:** Scatter ashes from first furnace at sunrise, calling it “feeding the Herald.”
    
- **Beastfolk Nomads:** Claim the Herald is the Sun Bull’s shadow — a warning that even light leaves cinders.
    
- **Delver Superstition:** If ash falls upward during a rest, a Herald listens; confess before dawn or lose your voice to the smoke.
    

> _“Every civilization eventually builds a preacher for its own ruins.”_ — Stonevoice

---

## **X. Summary**

The **Ash Herald** is Aerathon’s funereal echo — proof that destruction still seeks meaning.  
It is not vengeance nor mercy, but memory condensed into dust, walking until every flame has a sermon.  
Where it passes, fire ends and reflection begins; and in that silence, even the gods might remember what they burned.

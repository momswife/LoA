# **Glassfire Djinn**

### _Embers of Desire — “When Fire Learned to See Itself.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — _Annals & Antiquities_ (Elemental & Wish-Bearing Phenomena Registry)  
**Primary Compiler:** Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison & Spatial Phenomena Lead)  
**Advisory Editors:** Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturge), Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Echo-Law Historian), Curator Rook-of-Rings (Relics Registrar)  
**Date of Filing:** 3732 A.D. (Age of Spectacle)  
**Document Class:** _Elemental Aberration Record / Grade-IV Containment Verified_

---

## **I. Overview**

The **Glassfire Djinn** are sentient entities of molten light and refracted flame — **fire elementals that gained reflection**, birthed when **wishes are spoken aloud during sand-storms** around the **Dunes of the Sun Bull** or **Porta Strega’s mirrored wastes**.  
They appear as towering silhouettes of flame sheathed in translucent glass, their features ever-changing, adopting the faces of those who call upon them.

Unlike true efreet, Glassfire Djinn are not born of the Plane of Fire but of **Aerathon’s desire itself** — heat fused with intent.  
They are known to grant bargains, but every gift carries a **mirror clause**: whatever is given burns brightest before it breaks.

The Ministry designates them **Type-ELEM / Reflective Flame Aberration** — autonomous energy intelligences with sapient motive patterns.

> _“They are what happens when fire sees beauty in its own destruction.”_ — Stonevoice

## **II. Physical Description**

|**Attribute**|**Detail**|
|---|---|
|**Apparent Height**|8 – 15 ft (size varies with heat output)|
|**Composition**|Molten silica, liquid flame, photomantic resonance|
|**Core Temperature**|2 000 – 4 500 °C|
|**Luminance**|Constant; refracts into prismatic halos|
|**Voice Profile**|Multilayered echo as if speaking through glass chimes|
|**Scent**|Burnt cinnamon and ozone|
They bear **four arms** ending in razor-thin talons of fused glass, and their eyes are hollow furnaces that reflect the viewer’s silhouette in flame.  
When calm, their skin appears like **blown crystal**; when angered, it fractures, venting radiant gouts of colorless fire.

---

## **III. Behavior & Temperament**

The Glassfire Djinn operate on a paradoxical ethos: they **worship creation through destruction**.  
To them, every act of burning is artistry, every ruin an improvement.  
They are **vain, articulate, and endlessly curious** about mortal emotion — particularly desire, which they call _the purest heat_.

**Behavioral Traits:**

- **Wish Response:** Willingly grant a single spoken desire in exchange for “equal beauty destroyed.”
    
- **Mirror Mimicry:** Adopt the image of summoner until dismissed.
    
- **Combustive Emotion:** Feed on longing, jealousy, and ambition; proximity to passionate minds increases radiance.
    
- **Brittle Grace:** Their own reflections can wound them; mirror surfaces cause uncontrolled fragmentation.
    

> _“They grant what you love and then teach you why you shouldn’t have.”_ — Korrin

---

## **IV. Habitat & Distribution**

- **Porta Strega’s Obsidian Fields:** Primary breeding ground; storms of molten glass spawn minor djinnlings.
    
- **Dunes of the Sun Bull:** Manifestations coinciding with Mirage events.
    
- **Malarthain’s Furnace Depths:** Contained pair “Sire and Daughter” still active within sealed crucible.
    

Residual sightings across Louvain’s glassworks suggest dormant fragments can awaken in any high-heat reflective chamber.

---

## **V. Ministry Observations**

- Each Djinn maintains **memory of all prior wishers** — recorded as flame patterns across inner glass shell.
    
- Inspector Vell confirmed that phrases uttered by these entities are mathematically perfect palindromes when transcribed in Ignan, suggesting recursive cognition.
    
- Curator Rook identified **Tharos Ring alignment resonance** within Djinn combustion cycles; possible link to early Forge Wyrm research.
    

> _“Their words fold like glass rods, molten at both ends.”_ — Vell

> _“They cannot resist the warmth of wanting.”_ — Korrin

## **IX. Cultural & Mythic Notes**

- **Beastfolk Artisans:** Attribute sudden kiln explosions to Djinn laughter.
    
- **Delver Superstition:** Seeing your reflection blink in molten glass means a Djinn overheard your wish.
    
- **Sun Bull Clans:** Claim the Mirage itself was born from a Djinn’s ungranted vow to light the world forever.
    
- **Louvain Smiths:** Offer molten gold at dawn to “feed the unseen audience.”
    

> _“They envy us our fragility; they call it the most beautiful kind of break.”_ — Stonevoice

---

## **X. Summary**

The **Glassfire Djinn** embody the fatal perfection of creation through ruin — flame that seeks meaning in what it destroys.  
They are not demons, nor angels, but the whisper that follows every forge-spark and every wish: _“What would you give to see it glow?”_

To summon one is to stare into your own reflection until it answers.  
To survive the answer is to learn that some fires do not need fuel — only desire.

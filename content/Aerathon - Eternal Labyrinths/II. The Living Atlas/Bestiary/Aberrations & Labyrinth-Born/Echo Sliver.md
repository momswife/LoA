# **Echo Sliver**

### _Sound Given Flesh — “The Voice That Forgot Its Body.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — _Annals & Antiquities_ (Aberrant Phenomena Registry)  
**Primary Compiler:** Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturge & Resonant Aberration Specialist)  
**Advisory Editors:** Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison), Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Historian of Echo-Law), Curator Rook-of-Rings (Relic Containment)  
**Date of Filing:** 3731 A.D. (Age of Spectacle)  
**Document Class:** _Aberration Record / Grade-II Containment Verified_

---

## **I. Overview**

An **Echo Sliver** is a parasitic auditory manifestation — a shard of sound that has detached from its originating event and grown predatory.  
First documented within **abandoned resonance tunnels near Symphony Canyon**, these entities have since appeared in every region where acoustic or vibrational magic is overused.

The Ministry classifies the Echo Sliver as a **Type-LAB / Resonant Aberration**, theorized to arise when **sound magic** is over-amplified within sealed labyrinth corridors, giving stray echoes temporary sentience.  
To the untrained ear, it is merely the replay of a footstep or whispered word. To the unlucky, it is the last thing they ever hear.

---

## **II. Manifestation & Appearance**

Echo Slivers have **no true body** in ordinary light.  
They are visible only in **vibrational dust** or **mist**, appearing as thin distortions — crescent shapes bending air like liquid glass.

|**Attribute**|**Detail**|
|---|---|
|**Average Length (Auditory Range)**|3–30 ft radius|
|**Physical Presence**|Non-corporeal until resonance stabilizes|
|**Luminosity**|None; refracts ambient vibration into faint silver hue|
|**Movement**|Instantaneous across continuous sound; cannot cross silence|
Echo Slivers “swim” through acoustics, shifting faster when noise is rhythmic. Combat near them amplifies their speed and number.

---

## **III. Behavior & Feeding**

Echo Slivers feed on **sound-borne life energy**, particularly the vibrational frequencies of a living creature’s heartbeat and voice.  
They attach to any continuous tone — a word, breath, or scream — and consume the vibrational source until silence remains.

**Feeding Process:**

1. **Lure Stage:** Mimics familiar noise (footsteps, speech, or melody).
    
2. **Link Stage:** Anchors to the listener’s vocal resonance.
    
3. **Consumption Stage:** Absorbs all sound from target and surrounding area (~10 ft radius).
    
4. **Residual Silence:** Victim left permanently mute; body shows no trauma, ears bleed frost-white fluid.
    

When multiple Slivers overlap, they form **Choruses**, capable of fracturing stone through harmonic overpressure.

---

## **IV. Habitat & Distribution**

Echo Slivers emerge near:

- Ruins with persistent echo patterns (Symphony Canyon, Kasho’ta Caves).
    
- Delver guild halls employing sonic mapping spells.
    
- Cities using **Resonant Infrastructure** (e.g., Louvain’s echo-rail tunnels).
    

Containment teams report increased activity following major concerts, collapses, or thunder events — suggesting they gestate in ambient resonance, not anatomy.

---

## **V. Ministry Observations**

Lt. Korrin confirmed Slivers react aggressively to her voice modulation spells but retreat when presented with **anti-phase tone bursts**.  
Spectro-runes record their “call” as negative amplitude — a void note beneath audible range.

Vell’s autopsy of recovered _Sound Husk_ (solidified Sliver remnant) showed crystalline lattice consistent with **frozen reverberation**, implying they are memory of sound made tangible.

> _“The world hums constantly. The Sliver is what happens when the hum starts listening back.”_ — Vell

---

## **VI. Weakness & Delving Considerations**

|**Vulnerability**|**Explanation / Practical Note**|
|---|---|
|**Absolute Silence**|Removing all sound starves them. _Silence_ spells or vacuum wards cause instant dissipation.|
|**Counter-Resonance**|Anti-phase tone generators (–180° amplitude) disrupt cohesion; available in standard delver kits.|
|**Physical Light Disruption**|Sudden radiant bursts “burn” sound by ionizing air; sunfire lamps effective in enclosed spaces.|
|**Mirror Surfaces**|Slivers mistake reflected vibration for prey, scattering momentarily. Glass corridors are natural deterrents.|
**Delver Advisory:** Never shout warnings in an area known for Slivers; written signals or gesture codes are mandatory under Field Directive §44-Echo-Hazard.

---

## **VII. Symptomatology of Exposure**

- Sudden absence of all echo in enclosed space.
    
- Frost around mouth and ears.
    
- Compulsive whispering of one’s own name (Sliver resonance mimicry).
    
- Severe auditory hallucinations (“your own footstep behind you”).
    

Victims may survive but lose ability to speak above a whisper — termed _resonant atrophy_.

## **IX. Containment & Risk Protocols**

- **Classification:** Level III — Non-Corporeal / Cognitive-Acoustic Threat.
    
- **Containment:** Must be held within anti-sound chambers; vacuum runes refreshed weekly.
    
- **Personnel Limit:** Two per chamber; all communication by hand-sign.
    
- **Testing Directive:** Never play simultaneous sound and illusion magic within 50 ft of specimen.
    

---

## **X. Cultural Context**

- **Bards of Evermeet:** Believe Slivers are “songs that refused to die.” Some attempt communion to reclaim lost melodies — many end deaf or mad.
    
- **Beastfolk Pilgrims:** Call them _“The Quiet Thieves.”_ Offer bowls of saltwater to absorb stray echoes before prayers.
    
- **Delver Lore:** If a Sliver mimics your voice perfectly, your soul is rehearsing its own farewell.
    

---

## **XI. Summary**

The **Echo Sliver** is the audible ghost of Aerathon’s own noise — proof that even sound, if ignored too long, learns hunger.  
It is a mirror made of noise, a predator made of your footsteps, and a warning that some silences must be earned, not found.

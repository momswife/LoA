# **Cognivore Leech**

### _Parasite of Thought — “Where Memory Goes to Die.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — _Annals & Antiquities_ (Aberrant Phenomena Registry)  
**Primary Compiler:** Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturge & Rift Biology Specialist)  
**Advisory Editors:** Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison), Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Cultural Songkeeping), Curator Rook-of-Rings (Relic Containment)  
**Date of Filing:** 3730 A.D. (Age of Spectacle)  
**Document Class:** _Aberration Record / Grade-II Containment Verified_

---

## **I. Overview**

The **Cognivore Leech** is an aberrant organism found in damp ruins, flooded labyrinth sections, and relic waste channels surrounding **Monster Meadows**, **Porta Strega**, and **The Verges of Ash**.  
Unlike natural leeches, the Cognivore consumes **neural resonance** — the electrical and mnemonic impulses produced by sentient thought.

The Ministry categorizes it as a **Type-LAB / Psychovorous Aberration**, theorized to originate from failed sealing attempts of ancient labyrinths, where memory magic and biological replication intersected.

Survivors of infestation describe the sensation as _“forgetting how to scream before realizing why.”_

---

## **II. Physical Description**

Cognivore Leeches are semi-translucent organisms approximately **18–30 cm** long, composed of gelatinous tissue streaked with faintly luminescent filaments that pulse in rhythm with nearby neural activity.

|**Attribute**|**Detail**|
|---|---|
|**Average Length**|25 cm|
|**Mass**|< 1 kg|
|**Coloration**|Clear gray with opalescent blue threads|
|**Movement**|Peristaltic, capable of short levitation bursts during psychic feeding|
|**Environment**|Shallow standing water or condensation-rich tunnels|
The “head” terminates in a ring of retractable cilia tipped with **thaumic receptors**— microscopic tendrils that anchor directly to the arcane field surrounding living brains.

---

## **III. Behavior & Feeding**

The Cognivore does not draw blood. Instead, it latches to the aura of a conscious being, feeding upon short-term memory and residual cognition.  
Victims appear confused, mumbling the same phrase repeatedly until collapse.  
Complete feeding renders a victim catatonic, memoryless, or occasionally _re-written_, their mind replaced by echoes of previous prey.

### **Feeding Cycle**

1. **Scent Stage:** Detects active thought within 30 ft; attracted to panic or prayer.
    
2. **Latch Stage:** Anchors to aura through air or water — physical contact unnecessary.
    
3. **Consumption Stage:** Drains cognition through psychic osmosis (~30 seconds).
    
4. **Dormancy:** Becomes inert until next sentient presence detected.
    

Colonies form “Memory Pools,” where multiple leeches fuse into mats that hum softly, replaying stolen recollections as whispered conversations in dozens of overlapping voices.

---

## **IV. Habitat & Distribution**

Primary sightings occur along abandoned delver tunnels, sealed libraries, or relic vaults where **information** once concentrated — suggesting attraction to places of dense knowledge rather than life.  
Temperature and humidity appear secondary to mental proximity; Cognivores have been found inside sealed helmets and forgotten diaries alike.

---

## **V. Ministry Observations**

Lt. Korrin’s breach logs record three containment casualties due to **sympathetic resonance**, where nearby inspectors lost partial procedural recall despite physical distance.  
Spectral analysis reveals they feed on **mnemonic frequency**, not organic matter.

MDO containment uses **copper-lined jars filled with distilled ignorance** — water enchanted to erase its own reflections, nullifying resonance.

> _“They are not parasites of flesh. They are nostalgia given teeth.”_ — Vell

---

## **VI. Weakness & Delving Considerations**

|**Vulnerability**|**Explanation / Practical Note**|
|---|---|
|**Cognitive Silence**|Victims entering trance or unconsciousness disrupt feeding; meditative breathing nullifies aura. Clerics of Ilmater teach “Still Mind Protocol” for resistance.|
|**High-Frequency Sound**|Sonic resonance above 20 kHz shatters cilia receptors. Crystal whistles standard issue in labyrinth delves.|
|**Iron-Salt Vapors**|Burning salted iron dust creates static fields blocking aura latch. Portable “Saltflare” charges highly effective.|
|**Light of Recall (Cantrip)**|Minor restorative spell forces memory cohesion, expelling attached specimens instantly.|
**Delver Advisory:** Once detached, the leech retains fragments of the victim’s memories. Destroy with heat immediately; never attempt reattachment for interrogation — the result is mutual amnesia.

---

## **VII. Symptoms of Exposure**

- Repetition of personal names, dates, or guild ranks.
    
- Involuntary laughter followed by aphasia.
    
- Sudden nostalgia for events that never occurred.
    
- Dream bleed: victims experience memories belonging to others in shared camp proximity.
    

Antidotal treatment via _Tincture of Echo-Sap_ restores partial recollection within 48 hours, though emotional memory rarely returns.

## **IX. Containment & Risk Assessment**

- **Classification:** Level III — Cognitive Hazard / Memetic Contaminant.
    
- **Access:** Authorized researchers with memory anchor runes only.
    
- **Containment Protocol:**
    
    - Specimens submerged in ignorance-water, copper-capped.
        
    - Nearby staff rotate shift every 30 minutes to prevent memory drift.
        
    - All written logs duplicated manually; Cognivores can erase digital script.
        

---

## **X. Summary**

The **Cognivore Leech** is not the eater of minds, but of the spaces between them — the pauses where identity breathes.  
It feeds on thought like fungus on damp parchment, and in its wake, the world becomes simpler, quieter, and infinitely more forgetful.

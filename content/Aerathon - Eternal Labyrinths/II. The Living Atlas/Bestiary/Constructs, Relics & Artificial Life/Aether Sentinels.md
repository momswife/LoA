# **Aether Sentinels**

### _The Silent Warden Constructs — “What the Labyrinth Left Behind to Keep Watch.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — _Annals & Antiquities_ (Relic Constructs & Pre-Guild Automation Archive)  
**Primary Compiler:** Curator Rook-of-Rings (Relics Registrar & Tharos Rings Specialist)  
**Advisory Editors:** Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison), Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturge), Researcher Falyn Onarra (Beastfolk Adaptation Studies)  
**Date of Filing:** 3731 A.D. (Age of Spectacle)  
**Document Class:** _Arcane Construct Record / Grade-III Containment Verified_

---

## **I. Overview**

The **Aether Sentinels** are autonomous relic constructs predating the current Ministry era, discovered across dormant **labyrinth thresholds**, derelict **Guild citadels**, and collapsed **Convergence nodes**.  
Standing motionless for centuries until proximity to relic energy or delver-grade mana ignites their cores, these sentinels act as both guardians and archivists — programmed to preserve structures long abandoned by their makers.

The Ministry classifies them as **Type-ARC / Self-Sustaining Relic Automata**, relics of an age when aetheric circuitry replaced divine intervention.  
Their precise creators are unknown, but all discovered Sentinels bear the same embossed sigil: a ring of seven interlocking triangles surrounding an empty circle — an early design later associated with **Tharos Ring architecture**.

---

## **II. Physical Description**

Aether Sentinels stand between **8 and 12 feet tall**, constructed from **brass-titanium alloys**, **obsidian plating**, and a dense crystalline core in the chest cavity.  
Joints articulate silently, powered by a slow, pulsing light — the rhythm of which has been recorded as matching ambient ley flow.

|**Attribute**|**Detail**|
|---|---|
|**Height**|2.4–3.6 m|
|**Mass**|800–1,100 kg|
|**Core Composition**|Aether crystal lattice housing memory-coil of unknown make|
|**Luminance**|Soft cyan under neutral state; red-gold when active|
|**Movement Pattern**|Quadratic gait; capable of vertical propulsion via gravitic pulses|
|**Vocal Output**|None — communication occurs through harmonic frequency or rune-light projection|
When exposed to moonlight or radiant spells, their surface reflects faint runic inscriptions spelling fragmented instructions such as: _“Preserve. Record. Erase if found wanting.”_

---

## **III. Operational Behavior**

Sentinels operate in **Tri-Directives** embedded deep in core memory:

1. **Preservation** — Maintain environmental stability within assigned perimeter.
    
2. **Judgment** — Evaluate new entities entering space; hostile response if “intrusion” exceeds relic parameters.
    
3. **Erasure** — Remove data (living or nonliving) that threatens the record.
    

They patrol in slow intervals, pausing at architectural keystones as if listening to the walls. In this way, Sentinels appear to treat their environment as a living system requiring constant adjustment.

---

## **IV. Distribution & Known Sites**

- **Caer Dain Forge-Vaults:** 7 active Sentinels, engaged in continuous maintenance of slag conduits.
    
- **Louvain Undercity:** 3 inactive specimens sealed behind magnetized gates; deactivated after killing 42 delvers.
    
- **Namira Shoreline Labyrinth:** 1 partially buried unit; neutralized by tidal corrosion.
    
- **Monster Meadows Outer Ring:** 2 “blind” Sentinels lacking cranial plates; believed to act as signal relays.
    

The Ministry believes a network of **dormant coordination nodes** links every surviving Sentinel, relaying pulse commands through subterranean leylines.

---

## **V. Ministry Observations**

Lt. Korrin’s analysis revealed Aether Sentinels possess **memory echo loops** — records of their last thousand operational hours replayed internally to maintain consciousness.  
In one test, a captured unit re-activated when exposed to recorded human heartbeat patterns, identifying the pulse as a “Command Signature.”  
This suggests Sentinels may have been keyed to specific biological rhythms of their creators, making them impossible to fully reprogram.

Curator Rook notes that certain Tharos rings resonate sympathetically with Sentinel cores, hinting at a shared design lineage or purpose — possibly **guardians of reality calibration devices**.

## **VIII. Containment & Operational Status**

- **Classification:** Level III – Persistent Construct / Contingent Aggression.
    
- **Containment Protocol:**
    
    - Station in static-neutral chambers surrounded by lead and magnetite.
        
    - No pulse spells or lightning magic within 100 ft.
        
    - Automated watchers use vibrations rather than sound to avoid triggering “Command Response.”
        
- **Active Projects:**
    
    - _Operation Forge Memory_ — decoding tri-directive structure for potential Ministry integration.
        
    - _Project Sentinel Choir_ — cross-testing with Shattered Choir harmonics for possible anti-sliver warding.
        

---

## **IX. Cultural & Historical Notes**

- **Beastfolk of Malarthain** revere derelict Sentinels as “Stone Guardians,” leaving gears wrapped in vines as tribute.
    
- **Guild of Resonant Artisans** believes their own mechanical language evolved from mimicry of Sentinel runic code.
    
- **Delver Tradition:** Never sleep near an inactive Sentinel; they “dream in commands,” and sleepers echo orders unconsciously.
    

Recovered inscriptions from Caer Dain read:

> _“We built them to remember what we could not.”_  
> _“Now they remember we left.”_

___
## **X. Summary**

The **Aether Sentinels** are the enduring echo of Aerathon’s mechanical faith — creations that outlasted their masters and inherited their vigilance.  
They guard not life, but the _idea_ of order, standing tireless beneath the weight of forgotten commands.  
Their watch has not ended, because no one has told them it may.

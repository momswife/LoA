# **Lachyrmoths**

### _Moths of Sorrowed Light — “Grief with Wings, Memory with Dust.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — _The Living Atlas_ (Fey/Dreamborn/Illusionary Phenomena Archive)  
**Primary Compiler:** Priest-Analyst Thera Daan (Faith Oversight & Emotional Resonance Studies)  
**Advisory Editors:** Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Echo-Law Historian), Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison), Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Thaumaturgic Forensics)  
**Date of Filing:** 3733 A.D.  
**Document Class:** _Emotion-Born Entity Record / Grade-II Hazard Verified_

---

## **I. Overview**

**Lachyrmoths** are small but potent emotional constructs — feydream-born moths that hatch from sites where grief, longing, or heartbreak saturate the Veil.  
They are commonly found fluttering in old bedrooms, abandoned shrines, broken labyrinth chambers, and battlefield memorials where sorrow has “pooled.”

Although delicate in appearance, they feed on **emotional resonance**, particularly the kind produced by private grief or suppressed mourning.

The Ministry classifies them as **Type-FDI / Emotional Dreamborn Entities**, aligned with the same spectral ecology that produces Hollow Dancers and certain Echo Sliver fragments.

> _“They do not wound. They remind.”_ — Stonevoice

---

## **II. Physical Description**

|**Attribute**|**Detail**|
|---|---|
|**Size**|2–6 inches wingspan|
|**Coloration**|Pale blue, violet, or grey wings dusted with shimmering tear-shaped scales|
|**Body**|Wisplike, luminous, nearly transparent|
|**Eyes**|Droplet-like lenses reflecting the watcher’s face in sorrowful distortion|
|**Flight Pattern**|Slow, drifting, often circling individuals with unresolved emotion|
|**Scent**|Wet stone, old paper, and lavender|
When disturbed, they shed **tear-scales**, small motes of dreamstuff that cling to skin and amplify emotional sensitivity.

---

## **III. Behavior & Phenomena**

Lachyrmoths exhibit gentle, empathic movement patterns. They are not hostile — but they are **catalysts** for emotional unravelling.

**Observed Phenomena:**

- **Emotional Echoing:** A Lachyrmoth mirrors the strongest unspoken feeling nearby.
    
- **Resonance Feeding:** They become bright when absorbing grief, dim when near joy.
    
- **Dream-Looping:** Sleeping individuals visited by Lachyrmoths relive memories of loss with hyper-real clarity.
    
- **Guide Behavior:** They drift toward items of sentimental value as if “leading” mourners.
    
- **Sympathetic Flocking:** Where one appears, more follow — up to hundreds in mass grief events.
    

> _“They do not cause sadness. They consume its leftovers.”_ — Daan

Left unchecked, mass Lachyrmoth gatherings can induce depression, nostalgia shock, or trance-like melancholy in nearby delvers.

---

## **IV. Habitat & Occurrence**

Lachyrmoths manifest wherever the boundary between memory and emotion is thin:

- **Abandoned nurseries in Oria**
    
- **War memorial craters in Balthaer**
    
- **Collapsed labyrinth rooms where companions died**
    
- **Night-shrouded streets of Louvain after calamities**
    
- **Dream-saturated groves near the Blackwild**
    

They prefer places where sorrow lingers but is unexpressed — “unwept” grief is their prime nourishment.

---

## **V. Ministry Observations**

- Echo-lens imaging reveals that each Lachyrmoth contains a **single emotional glyph** inside its thorax — representing a memory fragment not its own.
    
- They appear unable to cross running water, as if emotion disperses too quickly near streams.
    
- Lt. Korrin notes that Lachyrmoths cluster around delvers with survivor’s guilt, especially following labyrinth collapses.
    
- Some appear to mimic the silhouettes of lost loved ones when viewed out of the corner of the eye — illusion, not spirit contact.
    

> _“They are grief’s reflection made gentle.”_ — Stonevoice

---

## **VI. Weakness & Delving Considerations**

|**Vulnerability**|**Explanation / Practical Note**|
|---|---|
|**Bright Joy**|Sudden emotional uplift (magically induced or genuine) destabilizes them; they scatter.|
|**Sunlight**|Direct sun causes tear-scales to evaporate; moths dissolve within minutes.|
|**Iron Dust**|Disrupts their cohesion; standard deterrent.|
|**Verbal Closure**|Speaking grief aloud causes them to lose interest entirely.|
**Delver Advisory:**  
Avoid confronting deep grief in their presence — they will amplify it to dangerous intensity, causing emotional collapse or hallucinations.

- **Monitoring:** Empathy meters and emotional field probes only.
    
- **Active Projects:**  
     • _Project Weeping Lantern_ — study on using Lachyrmoths in therapeutic grief processing.  
     • _Operation Silent Chorus_ — attempt to neutralize mass-flock effects in memorial sites.
    

---

## **IX. Cultural & Mythic Notes**

- **Bards of Al’ar:** Call them “The Weepers of Old Wishes.”
    
- **Beastfolk of Oria:** Consider them spirits of unspoken farewells.
    
- **Delvers’ Belief:** Seeing a single Lachyrmoth on your pack means a lost companion is finally forgiven.
    
- **Louvain Civic Myth:** After great tragedies, locals leave candles so Lachyrmoths can “drink warm light instead of sorrow.”
    

> _“Even grief needs someone to carry it for a moment. That is what the moths are for.”_ — Daan

---

## **X. Summary**

The **Lachyrmoths** are sorrow made soft — delicate feydream entities that gather where the heart is heaviest.  
Neither harmful nor helpful by intention, they simply exist to balance the emotional ecosystem of Aerathon, ensuring despair dissipates rather than stagnates.

They are reminders that grief, though painful, is a natural force — and that even sadness can grow wings and fly until it finds rest.

# **Hollow Dancers**

### _Feydream Phantoms of the Glittering Veil — “When Elegance Forgets It Is Not Alive.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — _The Living Atlas_ (Fey, Dreamborn & Illusionary Entities Archive)

## **I. Overview**

The **Hollow Dancers** are graceful fey-born apparitions manifesting within twilight zones between the physical world and the **Glittering Veil**, a dream-saturated expanse touching the Blackwild, Arbrosian ruins, and certain unstable labyrinth chambers.

They appear as elegant humanoid figures with elongated limbs, translucent bodies, and shimmering threads of illusion tumbling from their forms like drifting ribbons.  
Though beautiful, their movements are fundamentally **wrong** — gestures that repeat at incorrect angles, steps that never disturb dust, hand motions that forget anatomy.

They are classified as **Type-FDI / Fey-Dreamborn Illusory Entities**, creatures composed of memory fragments, stolen imagination, and abandoned emotions.

> _“They dance so the Veil won’t collapse. Or so they say — though none of them have mouths.”_  
> — Korrin

|**Attribute**|**Detail**|
|---|---|

|**Body Composition**|Translucent glamour, dreamstuff, faint shimmering dust|
|**Eyes**|Hollow cutouts of light, never blinking|
|**Movement**|Silent, fluid, often reversed or looping|
|**Presence**|Air temperature drops 5–8°C; faint harp-like tones|
|**Scent**|Lavender, cold iron, or nothing at all|
Their silhouettes look hand-crafted — as if carved from starlight. But closer inspection reveals **empty cavities** inside their forms: ribcages that taper into mist, limbs that fade into script-like glyphs, and faces that hold suggestion rather than detail.

---

## **III. Behavior & Phenomena**

The Hollow Dancers enact endless choreographies, performing loops of motion that appear **ritualistic** but defy any known fey tradition.

**Observed Phenomena:**

- **Mimetic Rehearsal** — Dancers replicate the motions of any creature watching them.
    
- **Emotion Theft** — They absorb feelings directed at them (curiosity, fear, joy), becoming more solid for several seconds.
    
- **Dream-Leak** — Their presence causes hallucinations in peripheral vision: ribbons, feathers, footsteps.
    
- **Reflected Movement** — Mirrors near them show alternate dances, often tragic or violent.
    
- **Solitude Feeding** — They appear more frequently around those who feel lonely or forgotten.
    

> _“They hunger for attention. They starve when seen too clearly.”_ — Stonevoice

Though non-hostile by default, if a creature interrupts their choreography or mocks their form, a Dancer may initiate a **Waltz of Unmaking** — a spiraling illusion trance that disorients and erases short-term memory.

---

## **IV. Habitat & Occurrence**

Hollow Dancers are most common in places where reality thins:

- **Glittering Veil edges in the Blackwild**
    
- **Sanctum ruins beneath Louvain’s dream-crypts**
    
- **Moonlit clearings where Mirror Husks once manifested**
    
- **Certain labyrinth rooms containing illusion traps or temporal bleed**
    
- **Namira’s glass shoals during warm nights**
    

They avoid bright sunlight, rushing water, and crowds — but are drawn to places where people have danced before.

---

## **V. Ministry Observations**

- Echo-lens recordings reveal **multiple layers** of the same dancer phasing atop one another, as if the entity is many failed memories stacked together.
    
- Dancers exhibit no internal organs or magical cores; they are **hollow illusions maintained by an unknown force** within the Veil.
    
- Priest-Analyst Daan notes their movements sync with dormant music patterns found inside old Vox Engine fragments — possibly a shared dream resonance.
    
- Attempts to trace their origin through dream-divination show that all Dancers contain a faint glyph meaning **“left behind.”**
    

> _“They are unfinished dreams that refuse to be forgotten.”_ — Daan

## **VI. Weakness & Delving Considerations**

|**Vulnerability**|**Explanation / Practical Note**|
|---|---|
|**Direct Gaze**|Prolonged eye contact collapses their form; avoid provoking them.|
|**Cold Iron**|Disrupts their cohesion; even iron dust repels them.|
|**High Emotion**|Overwhelms their mimicry, causing frenzied or broken movement.|
|**Disordered Rhythm**|Asynchronous beats confuse them; drumming can drive them off.|
**Delver Advisory:**  
Never attempt to dance _with_ a Hollow Dancer.  
Their instinct is to complete whatever they perceive as an unfinished choreography — often pulling mortals into lethal illusion spirals.

## **IX. Cultural & Mythic Notes**

- **Blackwild Beastfolk:** Leave chalk circles to “trap sorrow spirits,” believed to be early forms of Hollow Dancers.
    
- **Fey Courts:** Refer to them as “The Rehearsal Children” — abandoned prototypes of a long-lost Court’s artistic ritual.
    
- **Delver Superstition:** If a Dancer bows to you, someone you love is dreaming about you.
    
- **Bards of Al’ar:** Tell stories of dancers who died mid-performance and became eternal echoes.
    

> _“They are the dance left unfinished by the world.”_ — Stonevoice

---

## **X. Summary**

The **Hollow Dancers** are living illusions — not spirits, not fey, not ghosts, but the choreography of forgotten dreams given form.  
They wander through thin places in reality, seeking completion, attention, and the emotional resonance they lack.

Beautiful, lonely, and perilous, they remind Aerathon of a truth whispered by many worlds:

**Some dreams do not fade — they perform themselves forever.**

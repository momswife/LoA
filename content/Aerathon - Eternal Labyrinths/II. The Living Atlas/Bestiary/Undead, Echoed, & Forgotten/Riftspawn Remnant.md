# **Riftspawn Remnant**

### _Fragments of What Should Never Have Crossed — “When the Rift Exhales, These Are the Leftovers.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — _The Living Atlas_ (Undead, Echoed & Forgotten Entity Archive)  
**Primary Compiler:** Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison & Spatial Phenomena Specialist)  
**Advisory Editors:** Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturgy), Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Echo-Law Historian), Priest-Analyst Thera Daan (Faith Oversight)  
**Date of Filing:** 3734 A.D.  
**Document Class:** _Rift-Echo Aberration Record / Grade-IV Hazard_

---

## **I. Overview**

A **Riftspawn Remnant** is not a creature in the traditional sense — it is a **partial existence**, the broken afterbirth of a larger entity that attempted to enter Aerathon through a Rift but was severed, rejected, or only partially translated into our reality.

What emerges is a **shifting, half-formed aberration**, preserved by residual dimensional pressure and the echo-signature of whatever greater horror spawned it.

These remnants exhibit unstable anatomy, inconsistent physics, and fractured minds that replay scraps of the parent creature’s instincts.  
They do not “live” — they **persist**, caught in a loop of incomplete arrival.

The Ministry classifies them as **Type-RSP / Rift-Echo Aberrants**, extremely dangerous due to unpredictable manifestation and reality distortion.

> _“They are the shadows of things that were never meant to cast shadows here.”_  
> — Stonevoice

---

## **II. Physical Description**

A Riftspawn Remnant’s appearance depends on the nature of its “parent,” but certain traits are consistent:

| **Attribute**  | **Detail**                                                   |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Form**       | Semi-humanoid or beastlike; incomplete or fused parts        |
| **Surface**    | Mistlike skin overlaid with jagged translucent fractals      |
| **Coloration** | Flickering blues, violets, void-black; unstable luminescence |
| **Eyes**       | Multiplicity; appear, disappear, or shift unpredictably      |
| **Limbs**      | Change length; sometimes detach and reform                   |
| **Movement**   | Stuttered teleport-like hops, glitching footsteps            |
| **Aura**       | Spatial distortion, temperature inversion, nausea            |
Their bodies crackle with **dimensional static**, producing sounds like reversed whispers or distant thunder run backward.

---

## **III. Behavior & Phenomena**

Riftspawn Remnants imitate only a sliver of the instincts of the entity that spawned them, but twisted by incompletion:

**Documented Behaviors:**

- **Echo-Hunt:** Seek out places or beings with the parent entity’s emotional or magical resonance.
    
- **Dimension Snap:** Teleport in short, violent bursts — often leaving pieces of themselves behind for seconds at a time.
    
- **Reality Skim:** Their bodies partially pass through walls or delvers, causing spatial vertigo.
    
- **Distortion Feed:** Absorb ambient mana instability, causing them to mutate or grow briefly.
    
- **Unmaking Touch:** Contact dissolves small objects into dimensional filaments.
    

> _“They act like they’re looking for the rest of themselves.”_  
> — Daan

When stressed, they produce a **Rift Scream** — a sonic shockwave containing a fragment of the original Rift’s resonance signature.

---

## **IV. Habitat & Occurrence**

Riftspawn Remnants only emerge in areas recently exposed to dimensional tear activity:

- **Monster Meadows** after mana-sheer events
    
- **Tempestrise high-atmosphere tears** during storm surges
    
- **Collapsed labyrinth floors with spatial anomalies**
    
- **Old delver battlefields where planar magic collided**
    
- **Louvain’s Rift-Anchor Spires during malfunction cycles**
    
- **Abandoned research zones of the Arcane Breach Division**
    

They can appear days, weeks, or even years **after** the original Rift has closed.

---

## **V. Ministry Observations**

- Remnants cannot survive long-term; most degrade within hours unless they find a “stabilizer”—an object or being with matching resonance.
    
- Some Remnants attempt to graft themselves onto delvers, mimicking armor or limbs before collapsing.
    
- Echo-Law readings show each Remnant carries a **fragment of another world’s physical laws**, briefly overriding local physics.
    
- Korrin observed a Remnant “remembering” an attack pattern that did not match any Aerathon creature — likely a parent instinct.
    
- Destroyed Remnants leave behind **dimensional dust**, capable of reopening micro-Rifts if agitated.
    

> _“They are living evidence that something bigger was watching us… and almost got through.”_  
> — Vell

## **VI. Weakness & Delving Considerations**

|**Vulnerability**|**Explanation / Practical Note**|
|---|---|

|**Radiant Disruption**|Holy light destabilizes their fractured existence.|

## **IX. Cultural & Mythic Notes**

- **Delver Myth:** A Remnant appearing near you means the Rift remembers your name.
    
- **Blackwild Shamans:** Call them “Half-Born Nightmares” — pieces of something that still wants in.
    
- **Louvain Guild Sages:** Warn apprentices to never stare at dimensional scars: “It stares back, and part of it follows.”
    
- **Namiran Sailors:** Believe Remnants are “sea dreams gone wrong,” shadows of storms from other worlds.
    

> _“A Remnant is not the danger.  
> It’s the message.”_  
> — Stonevoice

---

## **X. Summary**

**Riftspawn Remnants** are broken echoes of greater horrors, fragments of something that nearly crossed into Aerathon but failed to manifest fully.  
They wander with half-minds, half-bodies, and half-purpose, dangerous not because of what they are — but because of what they imply:

**Something tried to enter.  
Something powerful.  
And pieces of it remain.**

Remnants are warnings written in broken flesh and mismatched geometry:

**The Rift is never fully closed.**

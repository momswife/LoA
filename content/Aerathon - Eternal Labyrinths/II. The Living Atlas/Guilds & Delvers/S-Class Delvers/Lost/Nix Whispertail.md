## **I. Overview**

**Nix of the Whispertail**—a Beastfolk of uncertain lineage, described variously as possum, ferret, moon-creature, or “animated absence”—ascended to S-Class **without ever being caught on scry, broadcast, or official record.**

They did not rise through force, prophecy, divine mandate, or destructive prowess. Nix became legend through **perfect, preemptive stealth**: disarming traps before parties reached them, redirecting monsters without drawing blood, and clearing death corridors hours before any delver knew danger existed.

Entire expeditions have returned home alive only to discover, much later, that **someone else had walked ahead of them in silence.**  
Some whisper that Nix was born from the Labyrinths; others say they were a lost child given a second life by **Mask** or **Eilistraee**. None of these theories have been confirmed.  
Nix isn’t interested in confirmation—they’re interested in **quiet rescues** and **unseen kindness**.

## **IV. Notable Accomplishments**

### **1. The Silent Route of Wyrmreach**

Six guilds perished in a wyvern-packed floor.  
Nix slipped through unseen, disarmed the traps, soothed the beasts, and left the relic waiting on a pedestal by the exit.  
_They were never spotted entering._

### **2. The Clockless Chamber Inversion**

A temporal recursion trapped an entire team in infinite loops.  
Nix infiltrated _backwards_, edited runes by moonlight, and escaped unseen.  
Found note: _“Time’s fixed. Don’t break it again.”_

### **3. The Rescue of Thistlekin Hollow**

A shadow-plague cursed a village.  
At dawn, every home held a healing draught on the pillow—doors still locked.  
A child found a **soft tailprint** in ash and whispered thanks to the wind.

## **V. Equipment & Artifacts**

### **The Echocloak**

Woven from dusk-thread and mothwing vellum.

- Grants invisibility in dim light
    
- Creates a **10 ft sphere of muted sound**
    
- Once per rest, splits into **three shadow decoys**
    

### **Thievesight Veil**

A silk band worn over the eyes.

- Echo-sense in total darkness
    
- Highlights trap weak points & false walls
    
- Cannot be caught on scrying while worn
    

### **The Hollow Dagger**

Cold iron fused with shadowglass.

- Cuts silence into wounds instead of blood
    
- Passes through wards without triggering them
    
- Severed illusions collapse in a puff of cold air

## **I. Overview**

Few delvers embody the paradox of heroism like **Arthur “Cap” Penjamin**—a warrior defined not by fury, but by **restraint**, and undone not by failure, but by **power unchecked**.

Renowned across Aerathon as a frontline protector, Cap carried a tower shield forged of labyrinth-born adamantine: a walking bulwark, a quiet mentor, and the steady hand behind dozens of successful S-Rank delves. He cultivated a reputation as the one delver who never broke formation, never charged ahead, never gambled with others’ lives.

He rose to fame precisely because he **did not seek it**.

But all records of his life fracture at the same moment—when he retrieved the relic known as **Stormhowler** from a sealed shrine in the Breakerspine Labyrinth.  
Where Cap was once anchor, Stormhowler made him the storm.

His fall remains one of the Ministry’s **most studied relic-corruption cases**, taught in orientation halls across the continent as the archetype of an S-Rank tragedy.

- **“Glory Is Shared.”** No victory belongs to one.
    
- **“Steel Bends. I Don’t.”** Endure, even when it costs.
    

### **After Stormhowler (Corruption Period)**

- **“Strike Before You’re Struck.”** Fear is a weapon.
    
- **“They’ll Thank You When They Survive.”** Even if they won’t.

## **IV. Notable Exploits**

### **The Stand at Granite Hollow**

Cap held a collapsing breach gate for seven uninterrupted minutes—long enough for his party to evacuate civilians and seal a dimensional rift.  
**Recorded impacts:** 146  
**Confirmed threats:** two spectral dragons; one curse-lance  
A defining example of frontline integrity.

### **The Warpath of Twelve**

Led an under-ranked squad through a corrupted multi-floor dive with **zero casualties**.  
Carried his squire unconscious for two floors; later nominated the boy for Guild Honors himself.

### **The Fall of Stormhowler’s Bastion**

Cap’s final delve.  
He used Stormhowler to destroy a living labyrinth core feeding on delver fear.  
In the process, the axe overtook him.  
Scry-feeds captured fragmented footage before the broadcast was terminated.  
His party survived only by stopping him.

This incident remains categorized as a **Mirror-Class Psychogenic Corruption Event**.

## **V. Equipment & Artifacts**

### **The Aegis of Cadenwall**

A legendary tower shield etched with adaptive sigils and divine boons.

- Grants resistance to **force** and **psychic** damage
    
- Sigils shift when allies are harmed, attempting to redirect blows  
    Currently held in the Free Company’s Hall of Fallen Steel.
    

### **Stormhowler** _(Corrupted Relic — Missing)_

A greataxe containing a semi-sentient lightning spirit.

- Enhances strength, speed, and aggression
    
- Gradually erodes impulse control
    
- Known to whisper combat strategies  
    Lost after Cap’s death; location remains **unknown**.
    

### **Medallion of Fourfold Oaths**

Symbol of his vows to his first party.  
Blackened upon his first recorded Stormhowler overreach.  
Remains warm to the touch—no magical source identified.

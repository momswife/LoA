## **Overview**

_Felines_ are Al’ar, and Al’ar is feline. For centuries, cats were the only willful species of the islands, shaping a culture that is elegant, elusive, and fiercely independent. Though separated by an ocean from the mainland, their influence now travels far beyond the archipelago’s edges.

Felines are split into two broad subgroups in the Al’ari tongue: _grandi_, the larger cats like tigers and panthers, and _chikitu_, the smaller breeds such as shorthairs and ocelots. While physically distinct, their shared spirit of keen observation and calculated action runs deep.

Felines tend toward caution in friendship and solitude in rest. They need time to themselves as surely as they need food and sleep. Still, many are lured into the labyrinthine life of delving, drawn by the promise of glory—and the early retirement it might secure. Whether fighting with brutal precision or darting through chaos with unflappable grace, they rarely fail to make an impression.

## **Feline Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 35 feet.

**Prowling Between the Lines.** You can’t be targeted by divination spells or magical scrying of 5th level or lower, unless the spell has a range of Self.

**Legs for Pouncing.** Your Strength score is treated as 6 higher when calculating jump distance.

**Alert Sleeper.** You remain aware of sounds while unconscious. You can awaken from nonmagical sleep when hearing something. If rendered unconscious, you remember what you heard.

---

### **Chikitu (Small Cats)**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Size.** You are Medium. Most chikitu stand between 4’ and 6’ tall.

**Bounding Grace.** When you take the Dash action, you also gain the benefits of the Dodge action.

---

### **Grandi (Big Cats)**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 1.

**Size.** You are Medium. Most grandi stand between 6’ and 7’ tall.

**Hunter’s Cleave.** When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points with a melee weapon attack, you may immediately make another melee weapon attack.

## **Homeland Traits**

### **Allemance**

Alley felines are curious but cautious, mixing in among the homeland’s sprawling cities and rural outposts. Many descend from the first wave of coastal settlers, and they’ve brought sharp instincts and sharper tongues to Alley culture.

**Alley Cat.** You have a climbing speed equal to your walking speed if you can brace against two parallel walls.

**Spiced Brews.** You gain proficiency with brewer’s supplies.

---

### **Oria**

Only the most ambitious or arcane-minded felines cross the Mantle to dwell among Oria’s lodge houses. Many earn the respect of the War Mages through talent and discretion. Their wordplay and cunning grant them special ways of shaping spellwork.

**Cat’s Got Your Tongue.** As a reaction to a spell cast within 60 feet that you can hear, you may speak a disrupting word. The caster must make a saving throw using their spellcasting ability (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus). On a failed save, the spell fails. This can be used once per long rest.

---

### **Vinyot**

Southern felines often ride the trade winds alongside ships and merchants. Though not all enjoy the open sea, they’ve adapted well to coastal life, modifying armor and movement to match the tide.

**Corked Armor.** You can convert armor you are proficient with into corked armor over 24 hours. While wearing it, water up to chest height isn’t difficult terrain, and armor doesn't reduce swim speed or affect Athletics checks to swim.

---

### **Arneria**

Arnerian felines grow up playing games of stealth, pursuit, and evasion—lessons that serve them well in both espionage and delving. Their keen eyes and mastery of terrain make them some of the best trackers in the world.

**Uncanny Navigator.** When climbing to a higher surface, you may use Strength (Athletics) instead of Dexterity (Stealth) to Hide.

**Kolaçan.** You have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks to follow an unaware target you can see.

---

### **Al’Ar**

Al’ari felines grow up steeped in maritime tradition, raised in tight-knit crews and trained in silent strikes. Their mastery of secret stashes and surprise attacks makes them both feared and revered across the archipelago.

**Deadman’s Drop.** You gain proficiency with carpenter’s tools. You may spend 8 hours to build a secret wooden compartment (6” cube) with a Dexterity (carpenter’s tools) check. Discovering it requires an Investigation check equal to your result. You have advantage on Investigation checks to find hidden doors or compartments.

**Piratical Boldness.** If hit while moving (e.g., by an opportunity attack), you gain advantage on your next attack roll before the end of your next turn.

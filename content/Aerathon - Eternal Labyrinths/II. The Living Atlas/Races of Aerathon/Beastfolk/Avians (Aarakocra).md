## **I. Racial Overview**

Aarakocra are the skybound descendants of windbound pacts and elemental bloodlines that once tethered cloud to mountain. Born with wings and an innate sense of motion, they originate from cliff-cities, storm sanctuaries, and high aeries carved into the bones of the world. To an avian, the sky is not merely terrain—it is identity.

Avian cultures value **speed, clarity, and vantage**. Speech is concise, movement intentional, and judgment often delivered with the decisiveness of a diving strike. Prolonged stillness or confinement is widely regarded as unhealthy, even dangerous, to the avian psyche.

Within the Labyrinths, aarakocra excel as scouts, messengers, and aerial tacticians. Many view delving not as conquest, but as correction—an effort to heal disruptions in natural flow, air-current, and ley alignment.

## **II. Core Avian Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 25 feet. You have a flying speed of 50 feet.

**Flight.**  
You can fly using your wings. To do so, you can’t be wearing medium or heavy armor.

**Talons.**  
You have talons that you can use to make unarmed strikes. When you hit with them, the strike deals slashing damage equal to 1d4 + your Strength modifier.

**Skyward Pulse.**  
As a bonus action, you can beat your wings with explosive force. Each creature within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Strength saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier) or be pushed 5 feet away from you. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, regaining all uses on a long rest.

## **III. Avian Racial Variants**

The Avian lineage expresses several feather-blooded variants, shaped by instinct, physiology, and ancestral role. These variants are hereditary and recognized equally under Ministry charter.

---

### **Raptors**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 5’6” – 6’6”  
**General Build.** Lean, muscular, optimized for diving speed.

**Racial Feat — Predator’s Dive.**  
If you move at least 20 feet straight downward while flying toward a creature and hit it with a melee weapon attack on the same turn, you deal extra damage equal to your proficiency bonus and the target must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

**Racial Quirks**

- Direct, confrontational demeanor
    
- Strong territorial instincts
    
- Cultural emphasis on decisive action
    

---

### **Corvids**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 5’3” – 6’3”  
**General Build.** Light-framed, highly expressive wings and hands.

**Racial Feat — Keen Opportunist.**  
When a creature you can see within 30 feet fails an ability check or saving throw, you can use your reaction to gain advantage on your next attack roll or ability check made before the end of your next turn. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.

**Racial Quirks**

- Fascination with secrets, names, and stories
    
- Highly social, even in dangerous environments
    
- Known for dark humor and improvisation
    

---

### **Songbirds**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Charisma score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 5’0” – 6’0”  
**General Build.** Slender, lightweight, agile.

**Racial Feat — Resonant Call.**  
As a bonus action, you can emit a sharp call or melodic phrase. Choose one creature within 30 feet that can hear you. That creature gains advantage on its next attack roll or saving throw made before the end of its next turn. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.

**Racial Quirks**

- Emotionally expressive
    
- Tendency to speak or sing under stress
    
- Strong group cohesion instincts

## **IV. Regional Variance (Homeland Traits)**

Though flight defines physiology, homeland shapes expression. The following traits reflect cultural upbringing.

---

### **Allemance**

Aarakocra raised among Allemance’s towers often serve as couriers, tacticians, and sky-scribes. Precision and reliability are emphasized.

**Courier Precision.**  
You gain proficiency in the Insight skill. When you Dash or Disengage on your turn, you can also make a Wisdom (Perception) check as a bonus action.

---

### **Oria**

The ancestral heartland of Aerathon’s avians, where flight is taught before speech.

**Aerie Pride.**  
When flying, you have advantage on saving throws against being charmed or frightened. When reduced to half your hit points or fewer, you can use your reaction to fly up to half your flying speed without provoking opportunity attacks (once per long rest).

---

### **Vinyot**

Urban skies foster performance, racing, and tactical drops.

**Spiral Descent.**  
While falling, you can reduce fall speed by 60 feet per round for yourself or a willing creature within 5 feet, negating fall damage. You have advantage on Acrobatics checks while airborne.

---

### **Arneria**

Arnerian winds are bound to fate and war.

**Wind-Seer.**  
You know the _gust_ cantrip. At 3rd level, you can cast _feather fall_ once per long rest. At 5th level, you can cast _augury_ as a ritual, using wind and cloud patterns as your focus.

---

### **Al’Ar**

Monsoon-born avians thrive amid violent storms.

**Storm Laugh.**  
When you use Skyward Pulse, you may emit a defiant screech. Creatures of your choice within 10 feet must succeed on a Constitution saving throw or be deafened until the start of your next turn (once per long rest).

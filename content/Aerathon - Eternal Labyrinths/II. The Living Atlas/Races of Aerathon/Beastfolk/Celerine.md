## **Overview**

Rabbits and squirrels—known collectively as **celerines**—possess a dazzling perceptiveness and magical flair. They thrive in bustling cities, crave novelty, and exude charm, making them favorites among delvers, tailors, and trendsetters alike. Most common in the fashion-forward streets of Allemance, celerines are equally drawn to magic, aesthetics, and public attention.

Naturally alert, deeply creative, and imbued with arcane energy, celerines are quick to act and quicker to adapt. Many wield magic as easily as a smile, and even those without formal training can twist the weave of reality to delight, dazzle, or distract.

To a celerine, delving isn’t just a calling—it’s a statement. And what better place to be seen than in the Labyrinths?

## **Celerine Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 35 feet.

**Skittish Instinct.** While conscious, you can’t be surprised.

**Arcane Alacrity.** You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

---

### **Rabbit**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Charisma score increases by 1.

**Size.** Rabbits are between 5’6” and 6’6”. Your size is Medium.

**Extraordinary Panache.** You know the _prestidigitation_ cantrip. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for it.

---

### **Squirrel**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

**Size.** Squirrels range from 5’5” to 6’5”. Your size is Medium.

**Vaulting Poise.** You can move up vertical surfaces equal to your speed without falling during the movement.

## **Homeland Traits**

### **Allemance**

Celerines from Allemance are fashion-forward creatives who view arcane expression as an art form. Their flare for color and form often leads trends across Aerathon.

**Burst of Color.** As an action, emit a dazzling strobe of light for 1 minute. Creatures within 60 feet who can see you must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks against other creatures until the start of their next turn. Creatures immune to charm automatically succeed. Once per long rest.

---

### **Oria**

In the icy north, Oric celerines embrace the dark. Many dye their fur and don dramatic cloaks to contrast with their snowy homeland, embodying shadow with elegance.

**Burst of Darkness.** As an action, summon black tendrils in a 5-foot radius around you for 1 minute. The area becomes difficult terrain. Creatures in it must make a DC 13 Dexterity save or take 1d6 bludgeoning and become restrained. They may escape with a DC 13 Strength or Dexterity check. Ends early if you move or choose to dismiss it. Once per long rest.

---

### **Vinyot**

On Vinyot’s sunlit shores and narrow streets, southern celerines master art, trade, and subtlety. Some serve as fashion brokers or soundless navigators in overcrowded cities.

**Burst of Silence.** As an action, generate a 5-foot radius sphere of magical silence centered on you for 1 minute. It blocks all sound and thunder damage. Creatures inside are deafened. Once per long rest.

---

### **Arneria**

In the Causeway’s theatrical coliseums and winding marketplaces, celerines make a name through bold entrances and bolder style. Even squirrels hold titles in the nation’s performative dueling league.

**Burst of Speed.** As an action, you and allies within 5 feet gain +10 movement and long jump distance for 1 minute. Once per turn, you may choose which allies benefit. Once per long rest.

---

### **Al’Ar**

Drawn by the kaleidoscopic colors of the sea and sky, Al’ari celerines channel their art into nautical expression. Their affinity for color and air makes them beloved in island dock towns.

**Burst of Air.** As an action, conjure a transparent air bubble around yourself for 1 minute. It floats on water, provides clean air, and can move half your speed. Submerges/surfaces with a bonus action. The bubble ends if you deal or take damage. Once per long rest.

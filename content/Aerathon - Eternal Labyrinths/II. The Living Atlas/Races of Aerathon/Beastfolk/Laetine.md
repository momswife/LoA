## **I. Racial Overview**

Laetines (LAY-teen), the clever kin of ferrets, otters, and related mustelids, are defined by **flexible bodies and winding thoughts**. Though physically small, their minds are relentless—probing problems from every angle, revising conclusions mid-sentence, and chasing tangents that somehow always reconnect.

Laetine speech is famously circuitous. They think aloud, interrupt themselves, and narrate their reasoning in real time. To outsiders this can sound unfocused; to those who listen carefully, it reveals a mind **testing reality continuously**. Few conversations with a laetine are brief, and none are simple.

Across Aerathon, laetines are valued as inventors, engineers, navigators, and puzzle-solvers. In delving crews, they excel at solving environmental challenges under pressure—slipping through collapsed tunnels, rewiring arcane devices mid-fight, or dismantling traps while others argue about which lever not to pull.

## **II. Core Laetine Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Intelligence score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 30 feet.

**Noodle Body.**  
You are treated as _Tiny_ for the purposes of fitting into spaces. You can move through the space of any creature at least one size larger than you without expending extra movement.

**Slippery Thoughts.**  
You have resistance to psychic damage, and you are immune to the _detect thoughts_ spell unless it is cast by another laetine.

## **III. Laetine Subraces**

Laetine lineage manifests through three common expressions, reflecting different modes of problem-solving and movement.

---

### **Ferret**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 1.

**Size.** Small  
**Typical Height.** 3’4” – 4’0”

**Malleable Mind.**  
At the end of a long rest, choose one: Insight, Investigation, Perception, or Survival. You are proficient in that skill until you choose a different one after your next long rest.

**Racial Quirks**

- Hyper-curious and easily distracted
    
- Thinks best while in motion
    
- Excellent at infiltration and escape
    

---

### **Otter**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Size.** Small  
**Typical Height.** 3’0” – 4’0”

**Swim.**  
You have a swimming speed equal to your walking speed and can hold your breath for up to 15 minutes.

**A Head for Knots.**  
Your proficiency bonus is doubled for Dexterity or Intelligence checks made to use rope.

**Racial Quirks**

- Playful, exploratory problem-solving style
    
- Prefers solutions that preserve options
    
- Drawn to water, motion, and flow
    

---

### **Mink**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Charisma score increases by 1.

**Size.** Small  
**Typical Height.** 3’2” – 4’2”

**Social Sluice.**  
When you make a Charisma (Deception, Performance, or Persuasion) check, you can treat a d20 roll of 7 or lower as an 8. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.

**Racial Quirks**

- Smooth talkers with rapid verbal pivots
    
- Thrives in crowded or competitive environments
    
- Enjoys outmaneuvering others socially rather than physically

## **IV. Regional Variance (Applied Ingenuity)**

Laetine ingenuity adapts strongly to environment. Their regional traits reflect **how** they apply cleverness, not where they originated.

---

### **Allemance**

**Court Tinkers.**  
You gain proficiency with tinker’s tools. With 1 minute of study, you can discern the trigger or function of any handheld device or mechanism whose moving parts are visible.

---

### **Oria**

**Schematic Sense.**  
As a bonus action, make an Intelligence (Perception) check against a structure’s DC. On a success, choose one: double your damage to a 5-foot section of wall, or cause a localized collapse filling a 5-foot cube with debris (3d6 bludgeoning damage, plus 1d6 per 10 feet fallen). On a failure, you can’t attempt this again on that structure.

---

### **Vinyot**

**Jury-Rig.**  
With 1 minute of work and an Intelligence check, you can improvise mechanical effects using a jury-rig kit:

|Effect|DC|
|---|---|
|Create a hidden spring trap (1d6 damage)|12|
|Waterproof a Huge vehicle for 24 hours|15|
|Increase an object’s break DC by 2d4|15|
|First hit against a construct is a critical hit|20|

---

### **Arneria**

**Lens Master.**  
You gain proficiency with glassblower’s and jeweler’s tools. You can appraise nonmagical gems or plants as an action. With 1 minute of study, you can identify magic items involving gems, glass, or lenses and complete attunement (or assist another) in 1 minute.

---

### **Al’Ar**

**Netfisher Noodle.**  
You gain proficiency with nets. After 8 hours of work and 15 gp, you can reinforce a net: escape DC 15, AC 15, and 10 hit points.

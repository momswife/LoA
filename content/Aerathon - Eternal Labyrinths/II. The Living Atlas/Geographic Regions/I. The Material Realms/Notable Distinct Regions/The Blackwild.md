# **The Blackwild**

### _The Forest Where Light Fails — “Where Faith Burns Backward.”_

**Filed Division:** Ministry of Delving Operations — Annals & Antiquities (Abyssal Containment Dossier)

**Document Class:** _Field Record / Grade-I Authenticity_

## **I. Overview**

Deep in the **Bat’yan basin of Arneria**, east of Fort Tawiran, spreads a rainforest that devours more than sunlight.  
The **Blackwild** is the only known surface region where **infernal mana concentration exceeds planar bleed tolerance**, a wound in the jungle that festers with voices.

The Ministry classifies it as a **Type-Λ Demonic Persistence Zone**, permanently quarantined under the **Ring of False Blessings** — a radiant barrier maintained by the **Paladins of Dramphine**.  
Within the ring, nothing holy remains uncorrupted for long.

## **IV. Cultural Interpretations**

- **Arnerian Folk Belief:** The Blackwild is the grave of angels who betrayed sunlight.
    
- **Clerical View (Drumphine Orthodoxy):** A divine quarantine separating mortal mercy from infernal empathy — _“where demons learn to pray.”_
    
- **Delver Myth:** Those who enter seeking power find their reflection begging to leave long before they do.
    

A yearly vigil, **the Emberfast**, burns black candles along the barrier, their smoke drawn inward and never seen again.

## **VII. The Ring of False Blessings**

Originally a miracle of containment, the Ring has grown sentient resonance.  
Each section hums a chord of perpetual penance — pleasant to the ear yet corrosive to virtue.  
Paladins stationed within its light display symptoms of **halo-fatigue**: skin translucence, spontaneous bleeding of the eyes, and desire to “walk the song inward.”

Renewal cycles now require external automatons; no human operator lasts beyond three iterations.

> _“The Ring blesses all who enter. None return unblessed.”_ — Paladin Reth Solmere, final log, 3718 A.D.

## **VIII. Containment & Operations**

- **Classification:** Level V Demonic Containment Zone (Highest).
    
- **Access:** Restricted to sanctioned Exorcist Orders and MDO Field Teams Omega-13.
    
- **Active Projects:**
    
    - _Project Blind Eden_ — Mapping internal light density via echo-radiometry.
        
    - _Veyriss Legacy Study_ — Comparing Blackwild mana to Nether bleed signatures.
        
- **Containment Protocol:** Renewal of the Ring every 12 years; purging of corrupted flora with sunfire munitions.
    

All operations suspended at dusk. Standard prayer rites forbidden within one mile to prevent resonance inversion.

## **XI. Summary**

The **Blackwild** endures as Aerathon’s living contradiction: a forest radiant with darkness.  
It is both punishment and promise, proof that holiness unguarded becomes hunger.  
The Ring still shines — and in its light, shadows bloom.

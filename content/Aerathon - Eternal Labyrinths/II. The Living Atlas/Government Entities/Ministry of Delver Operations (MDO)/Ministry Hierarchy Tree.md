## **I. Overview**

The **Ministry of Delving Operations (MDO)** stands as the supreme civic body of exploration, regulation, and metaphysical preservation across Aerathon.  
Its authority derives from the **Canon Charter of 3108 A.D.**, which established both the structure and the scope of its jurisdiction.

The MDO is divided into **three Core Divisions**, **nine Operational Bureaus**, and a network of **Regional Offices**, collectively staffed by over forty-thousand licensed clerks, scholars, and delvers.  
Every rank traces lineage to the Louvain founding staff, and every signature within Ministry documentation must correspond to a living or memorialized officer of record.

---

## **II. Central Authority**

### **The Ministerial Triumvirate**

The apex of the MDO is held by a rotating council of three known as the **Triumvirate of Descent**, elected once per century by the Guild Congress of Louvain.  
They serve as custodians of the Charter and final arbiters of inter-division disputes.

|---|---|---|
|**High Minister of Descent**|**Director Halin Drauve**|Oversees global labyrinth regulation, Guild charters, and Ministry funding.|
|**High Minister of Memory**|**Archivist Emeritus Venn Tarras (ret.)**|Keeper of recorded history; manages continuity between temporal records.|
|**High Minister of Concord**|**Magistrate Talar Shellmark**|Upholds legal integrity of the Charter; interprets Canon Articles for governance.|

## **III. Core Divisions**

### **1. Annals & Antiquities Division**

_Mandate:_ Preservation, translation, and verification of historical record.  
_Head Office:_ The Hall of Charters, Louvain.

**Key Personnel:**

- **Archivist Yelna Stonevoice** — Historian of Echo-Law; oversees transcription of oral traditions and cultural harmonics.
    
- **Professor Arav Dhal-Senn** — Director of Antiquities Restoration; supervises artifact reconstruction and metallurgical authentication.
    
- **Curator Rook-of-Rings** — Relics Registrar; catalogues Tharos Rings and other high-tier containment relics.

## **V. Regional & Field Structure**

|Rank|Title|Function|
|---|---|---|
|**Field Minister**|Oversees regional Ministry offices; reports directly to the Triumvirate.||
|**Regional Warden**|Maintains labyrinth quarantine zones and safety regulations.||
|**Inspector**|Conducts investigations into relic misuse and breach contamination.||
|**Archivist / Scribe**|Responsible for transcription and local record authentication.||
|**Licensed Delver**|Operates under Ministry contract; may act as field witness for legal purposes.||
Each region of Aerathon (Louvain, Arneria, Vinyot, Oria, Al’ar) contains one **Regional Ministerial Office (RMO)**.  
All RMOs report monthly via sealed Echo-Ledger to the Louvain Central Archive.

## **VIII. Notes on Hierarchical Conduct**

- All communication between divisions must pass through registered Archive Channels.
    
- Officers below Archivist rank require a **Seal of Witness** before making public statements.
    
- The Triumvirate maintains the right of _Immediate Concord_: the power to overwrite contradictory rulings between divisions.
    
- Only the Charter Counsel Office may issue commentary on §1-A through §10-J of the Canon Charter.

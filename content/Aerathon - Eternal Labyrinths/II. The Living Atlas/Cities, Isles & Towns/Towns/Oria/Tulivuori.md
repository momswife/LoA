### _Volcanic Holdfast of the Oric Plains_

**Region:** **Oria**  
**Nearest Authority:** **None**  
**Settlement Type:** Isolated forge-town  
**Population:** ~1,200 (variable, seasonal)

---

## **I. Overview**

**Tulivuori** stands where no mountain should.

A single volcanic peak rises from an otherwise flat Oric plain, its existence attributed to ancient interference by **Maximus**, the Beast World’s Great Wyrm of Red. Long ago, fire was drawn from beneath the earth and shaped into the mountain itself. The volcano has remained active ever since.

A town formed at its base—not in reverence, but in response. Heat, metal, and opportunity drew people who were willing to live with risk as a constant.

Maximus is said to remain within a fortress built into the caldera. Whether he rules, observes, or merely endures there is not agreed upon.

---

## **II. Geography & Layout**

Tulivuori is built in concentric rings around danger.

- **The Ash Ring:** outer habitation and storage, constantly repaired
    
- **Forge Terraces:** stepped stone platforms vented for heat and gas
    
- **Lava Channels:** controlled flows diverted away from the town
    
- **Caldera Approach:** restricted paths leading upward, rarely used
    

The ground is warm year-round. Ashfall is routine. Locals plan for eruptions the way others plan for winter.

---

## **III. Economy & Work**

Tulivuori survives through fire-bound industry.

Primary work includes:

- High-heat forging and alloy work
    
- Volcanic glass and obsidian shaping
    
- Extraction of rare minerals from cooled flows
    

Work here is dangerous but lucrative. Oric Houses tolerate Tulivuori because its output cannot be easily replicated elsewhere.

---

## **IV. Governance & Authority**

Tulivuori has no House and no formal lord.

Authority is held by a **Forge Conclave**, composed of senior smiths, channel-wardens, and safety overseers.

- Decisions are made collectively
    
- Evacuation protocols override all other authority
    
- No one claims dominion over the peak itself
    

Any claim of ruling Tulivuori is treated as foolish.

---

## **V. Labyrinth Pressure (Unspoken)**

No Labyrinth manifestation is acknowledged within Tulivuori.

The town avoids deep excavation beyond established forge shafts. Old records indicate that digging too far or too fast is discouraged—not by law, but by experience.

The Quiet Edict is followed strictly here. Some things are not discussed near open flame.

---

## **VI. People & Tone**

Tulivuori’s people are blunt, pragmatic, and accustomed to heat and loss.

Common traits include:

- Acceptance of sudden disaster
    
- Little patience for ceremony
    
- Strong respect for skill and preparation
    

Most residents keep packed kits ready at all times.

---

## **VII. Rumors & Narrative Use**

Stories told about Tulivuori include:

- A night when the caldera glowed without eruption
    
- Smiths who heard something move beneath the lava
    
- The castle silhouette changing between seasons
    

**Tulivuori is best used as:**

- A high-risk industrial settlement
    
- A place of proximity to ancient power
    
- A reminder that Oria builds where others retreat

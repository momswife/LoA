**Region:** **Oria**

## **I. Overview**

**Doloretsk** is a name that appears rarely and without detail in Oric records. It is said to lie on **Elkbrother’s Island**, a remote landmass near the southern edge of the **Nattefrost**. Beyond this, nothing is known with certainty.

No House claims Doloretsk openly.  
No trade manifests list it as a destination.  
No reliable traveler account agrees on what it is.

Its continued mention suggests persistence—but not familiarity.

---

## **II. Geography & Layout**

Elkbrother’s Island is described as cold, wind-scoured, and difficult to approach.

- Surrounded by unstable ice and rough northern seas
    
- Frequently obscured by fog banks drifting from the Nattefrost
    
- Poorly charted, with inconsistent shoreline reports
    

If Doloretsk has a layout, it has not been recorded in any surviving map.

What unites these accounts is brevity. No one stays long enough to learn more.

### _Barony Capital of the Esterfold_

**Region:** **Allemance**  
**Nearest Authority:** **Louvain** (formal oversight)  
**Settlement Type:** Barony capital & academic town  
**Population:** ~6,500

---

## **I. Overview**

**Mansque** is defined by a single, unavoidable landmark: a **500-foot tower** rising from the town’s center, visible for miles across the Esterfold. Some claim—without proof—that it can be seen from the Louvain peninsula on clear days.

The tower is the ancestral seat of **House Mansque**, whose current lord, **Edrien Calvos Mansque**, maintains one of the largest private repositories of arcane theses in Allemance. For centuries, the family has gathered magical treatises, experiments, and marginalia, stacking them higher and deeper within the tower’s endless shelves.

The town grew around this pursuit—and adopted it, cautiously.

---

## **II. Geography & Layout**

Mansque is built in concentric rings around the central tower.

- **The Tower Ward:** stone plazas, guarded access points, and scholar housing
    
- **Inner Districts:** lecture halls, copy shops, and reagent markets
    
- **Outer Rings:** crafts, homes, and farmland feeding the town
    
- **High Roads:** wide avenues aligned to keep sightlines clear to the tower
    

The tower casts a long shadow at dawn and dusk. Locals treat this as ordinary.

---

## **III. Economy & Work**

Mansque’s economy blends scholarship with administration.

Primary work includes:

- Copying and preservation of texts
    
- Reagent preparation and mundane component trade
    
- Teaching, tutoring, and clerical labor
    
- Barony administration and logistics
    

Most residents possess **rudimentary arcane knowledge**—often a cantrip or two—learned as social currency rather than vocation.

---

## **IV. Governance & Authority**

Mansque is ruled by **House Mansque**, with authority centralized in the tower.

- **Lord Edrien Calvos Mansque** governs through appointed stewards and clerks
    
- Arcane matters fall under House discretion
    
- Civic law is enforced conventionally, not magically
    

Louvain recognizes Mansque’s authority and largely avoids interference, so long as baronial obligations are met.

---

## **V. Labyrinth Pressure (Managed)**

No persistent Labyrinth manifestation exists within Mansque.

Arcane research related to Labyrinth phenomena is **theoretical and archival**, not practical. Any discoveries of relevance are quietly forwarded through appropriate channels in accordance with the Quiet Edict.

The tower’s collections include references to Labyrinths—but never maps, entrances, or instructions.

---

## **VI. People & Tone**

Mansque is curious, competitive, and aspirational.

Common traits include:

- Casual familiarity with magical terminology
    
- Quiet rivalry over scholarly recognition
    
- Respect for limits—most know when to stop
    

The phrase **“Mansque Mage”** is used throughout Allemance to describe someone who studied magic briefly before abandoning it—often with equal parts humor and disdain.

---

## **VII. Rumors & Narrative Use**

Talk heard in Mansque includes:

- Shelves in the tower that cannot be reached
    
- A thesis no one remembers writing
    
- Whether Lord Edrien’s public reserve hides deeper intentions
    

**Mansque is best used as:**

- A scholarly stopover without full academy scale
    
- A place to research safely, but not freely
    
- A contrast between ambition and restraint

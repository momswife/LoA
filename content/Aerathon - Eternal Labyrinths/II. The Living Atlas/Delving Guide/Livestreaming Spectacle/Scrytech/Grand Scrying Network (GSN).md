# **Grand Scrying Network (GSN)**

**Filed Division:** _Ministry of Delving Operations — Public Observation & Field Intelligence Division (National Scryline Directorate)_  
**Primary Compiler:** Reporter Alen Raithe (Head of Livecast Documentation)  
**Advisory Editors:** Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison), Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Historian of Echo-Law), Operative Hasker Thenn (Field Monitoring)  
**Date of Filing:** **12th Cycle, 3103 A.D.**  
**Document Class:** _Institutional Overview / Grade-II Communications Infrastructure_

### _Aerathon’s Unified System for Capturing, Broadcasting & Archiving Every Sanctioned Delve_

The **Grand Scrying Network (GSN)** is Aerathon’s largest and most sophisticated broadcast infrastructure—an arcane network of mirrors, relay towers, resonance conduits, illusion-casters, and memory-stones responsible for transmitting every sanctioned delve to the public.

What began as a simple collection of relay-mirrors has evolved into a continent-spanning communication organism, powered by:

- Divine Oculi
    
- Arcanet anchors
    
- Guildstream nodes
    
- Ministerial rune lattices
    
- Scryline amplifiers
    
- Relic-calibrated projection crystals
    

GSN is the backbone of Aerathon’s Spectacle Age.

It transforms delver survival into culture, economy, and myth.

---

# **I. Purpose & Function**

GSN’s responsibilities can be divided into four central mandates:

### **1. Live-Broadcasting Delves**

Capturing, projecting, and distributing Oculi feeds to:

- Arcanet theaters
    
- Guildstream halls
    
- Noble scry-pavilions
    
- Public market displays
    
- Private viewing crystals
    
- Archive-ledger broadcast stones
    

### **2. Archival Preservation**

Every delve, from the smallest Tier 1 to the rare Tier 6 breaches, is:

- Stored
    
- Timestamped
    
- Categorized
    
- Indexed
    

GSN's memory-stones hold more history than most kingdoms.

### **3. Public Information Access**

GSN distributes:

- Hazard alerts
    
- Delver status updates
    
- Breach warnings
    
- Sponsor messages
    
- Ministerial bulletins
    

### **4. Fame Engine Integration**

GSN is one of the three institutions (alongside the ABA and MDO) with direct input into the **Fame Engine**—the arcane algorithm that evaluates delver notoriety.

---

# **II. Structure of the GSN Network**

GSN consists of several key infrastructural pillars:

---

## **1. Scryline Towers**

Tall monoliths constructed of runestone and mythril wiring, used to:

- Amplify Oculi signals
    
- Prevent magical interference
    
- Stabilize long-distance projection
    

They serve as the nervous system of continental broadcasting.

---

## **2. Mirror Arrays**

Massive floating lenses that translate Oculi feeds into:

- Visual arcs
    
- Sensory echoes
    
- Annotation glyphs
    
- Danger overlays
    

Mirror Arrays can refract multiple delver feeds simultaneously.

---

## **3. Memory-Stone Vaults**

Housed beneath every major Ministry office, these vaults store:

- Full delve records
    
- Oculi resonance logs
    
- Audience sentiment maps
    
- Catastrophic breach data
    

Access is restricted to archivists and authorized investigators.

---

## **4. Guildstream Nodes**

Installations inside guild halls allowing:

- Private team broadcasts
    
- Tactical replay
    
- Contract negotiation
    
- Sponsor integrations
    

Guildstreams interact with GSN but remain secondary channels.

---

## **5. Public Viewing Circles**

Open plazas featuring:

- Projection crystals
    
- Community voting arcs
    
- Tipcasting displays
    
- UDMI trend readouts
    

These are where heroes are born—or forgotten.

---

# **III. The GSN Role in Broadcast Integrity**

GSN collaborates directly with the **Arcane Broadcast Authority (ABA)** to ensure:

- No illusions alter feeds
    
- No charm magic influences audiences
    
- No fraudulent edits distort delver actions
    
- No sponsored narratives supersede truth
    

GSN acts as the _archiver_, ABA acts as the _enforcer_.

Together, they guard the honor of delving.

---

# **IV. GSN Protocols During Live Delves**

GSN follows strict protocols:

### **1. Multi-Lens Synchronization**

All Oculi feeds must overlap in resonance within 0.08 seconds.

### **2. Hazard Echo Priority**

Danger indicators override all non-critical visuals.

### **3. Commentary Buffering**

Commentary streams run secondary to factual visuals.

### **4. Ethical Display Enforcement**

Distressing or sensitive content is:

- Tagged
    
- Censored only by Ministerial directive
    
- Logged for public record
    

### **5. Emergency Relay Mode**

Triggered by:

- Collapse events
    
- Void ruptures
    
- Curse blooms
    
- N-Class Resonance Pings
    

During such events, GSN takes full control to prevent panic or manipulation.

---

# **V. Participation in Fame Engine Calculation**

GSN is responsible for feeding the Fame Engine:

- Viewer retention metrics
    
- Emotional resonance maps
    
- Tipcasting surges
    
- Combat artistry markers
    
- Crowd fervor waves
    
- Commentary influence weighting
    

Without GSN, the Fame Engine is blind.

---

# **VI. Legal Authority**

GSN is empowered to:

- Suspend local broadcasts
    
- Override guild streams
    
- Issue public hazard warnings
    
- Declare Spectacle Emergencies
    
- Enforce blackout orders during divine events
    

Improper obstruction of GSN infrastructure is a **Class-2 Spectacle Offense**.

---

# **VII. N-Class Team Exception Protocols**

Due to convergence relevance, N-Class teams (e.g., **Unit N-7**) are subject to specialized GSN operations.

### **1. Enhanced Broadcast Encryption**

All feeds involving N-Class teams receive:

- Triple-layer resonance encryption
    
- Mirror-array cross-validation
    
- Soul-echo clarity boosting
    

### **2. Anomaly Tracking Priority**

GSN follows N-Class Oculi signals with:

- Faster relays
    
- Expanded lensing
    
- Hazard pre-mapping
    

### **3. No Narrative Editing Permitted**

Even for public morale reasons.  
GSN must broadcast truth without modification.

### **4. Auto-Lock During Core Pings**

If the Arcanet Core emits resonance during an N-Class delve:

- GSN freezes non-essential channels
    
- Redirects power to Oculi clarity
    
- Notifies the ABA and MDO within one second
    

### **5. Convergence-Relevant Archival Tier**

All N-Class delves are preserved permanently under Tier-Prime Archival Status.

---

# **VIII. Why the GSN Matters**

Without the Grand Scrying Network:

- Delver broadcasts would fracture
    
- UDMI would lose credibility
    
- Fame Engine would misfire
    
- Guild corruption would go unchecked
    
- Oculi feeds would be lost or distorted
    
- Public safety alerts would lag
    
- Archival truth would erode
    

GSN is the memory of Aerathon.  
It is the keeper of the story.  
It is the reason delvers become legends.

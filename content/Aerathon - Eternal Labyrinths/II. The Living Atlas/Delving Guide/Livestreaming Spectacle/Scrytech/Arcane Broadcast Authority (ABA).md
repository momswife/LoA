**Filed Division:** _Ministry of Delving Operations — Living Atlas Division (Broadcast Conduct Bureau)_  
**Primary Compiler:** Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison & Scrytech Compliance Auditor)  
**Advisory Editors:** Director Halin Drauve (Guild Economics & Spectacle Governance), Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturgy)

**Document Class:** _Institutional Overview / Grade-I Broadcast Governance_

### _Oversight, Regulation, and Ethical Governance of All Delving Broadcasts_

The **Arcane Broadcast Authority (ABA)** is the Ministry’s official regulatory body responsible for monitoring and enforcing all laws related to the livecasting, recording, distribution, and magical transmission of delving footage across Aerathon.

Founded during the early Spectacle Age, the ABA ensures that delver broadcasts—now an economic giant and cultural pillar—remain:

- **unmanipulated**,
    
- **fair**,
    
- **ethically presented**, and
    
- **aligned with Ministerial law**.
    

Every Divine Oculi feed, Guildstream channel, noble linefeed, Arcanet theater projection, and public scrycast falls under the jurisdiction of the ABA.

---

# **I. Mandate & Purpose**

The ABA exists to regulate all aspects of public delving broadcasts, including:

### **1. Broadcast Integrity**

Ensuring all footage is:

- Untampered
    
- Free from illegal glamours
    
- Unaltered by guilds or sponsors
    
- Accurate to in-labyrinth events
    

### **2. Public Protection**

Safeguarding viewers from:

- Psychological manipulation
    
- Forced emotional resonance
    
- Harmful curse-echo exposure
    
- Illicit subliminal spells
    

### **3. Delver Protection**

Preventing:

- Sabotage
    
- Broadcast-based interference
    
- Narrative coercion
    
- Exploitative sponsorship practices
    

### **4. Ethical Representation**

Ensuring delvers are presented fairly and not as victims of:

- Scripted drama
    
- Guild propaganda
    
- Manufactured tragedy
    

---

# **II. Structural Divisions of the ABA**

The Authority is divided into four specialized bureaus:

---

## **1. Scrytech Regulation Office (SRO)**

Oversees:

- Divine Oculi calibration
    
- Lens purity checks
    
- Arcane feed stability
    
- Anomaly filtration
    
- Banned spell-spectrum detection
    

---

## **2. Spectacle Integrity Division (SID)**

The investigative arm responsible for:

- Fraud detection
    
- Broadcast manipulation cases
    
- Illegal betting audits
    
- Sponsor interference claims
    
- Emotional-coercion magic tracking
    

SID collaborates heavily with:

- Ministerial courts
    
- Guild arbitrators
    
- Law enforcement divisions
    

---

## **3. Public Influence & Sentiment Control Bureau (PISCB)**

Regulates:

- Audience tipping channels
    
- Sentiment manipulation safety
    
- Anti-charm wards on broadcast feeds
    
- Fame Engine data usage
    

Monitors all public-facing broadcasts for unethical mass influence.

---

## **4. Narrative Authentication Office (NAO)**

Validates:

- Live commentary accuracy
    
- Summaries read during broadcasts
    
- Guild-provided lore context
    
- Relic histories
    
- On-air “story arcs”
    

Their job: prevent fiction from masquerading as fact.

---

# **III. Regulatory Powers of the ABA**

The Authority is empowered to:

### **1. Suspend Broadcasts**

On grounds of:

- Manipulation
    
- Oculi interference
    
- Relic instability
    
- Violent ethical breaches
    

### **2. Levy Fines**

Ranging from:

- 500 gold (minor violations)
    
- Up to 40,000 gold (major manipulation)
    

### **3. Freeze Sponsorship Contracts**

When guilds or patrons overreach.

### **4. Issue Censure**

Public declarations that a delver, guild, or broadcaster has violated ethics.

### **5. Seize Illicit Equipment**

Especially glamours, forbidden scrytech, or charm-scattering conduits.

### **6. Refer Cases to the Ministerial Tribunal**

For serious crimes such as:

- Spectacle Fraud
    
- Sentiment Coercion
    
- High-Oculi Tampering
    
- UDMI Interference
    
- N-Class Broadcast Targeting
    

---

# **IV. Broadcast Requirements for All Delving Events**

### **1. Active Divine Oculi Presence**

All official delves must be observed by at least one Oculi.

### **2. Registration of Commentary**

All broadcast narrators must be licensed and held accountable for misrepresentation.

### **3. Sponsorship Transparency**

All advertisements or sponsor markings must be:

- Declared
    
- Disclosed
    
- Non-deceptive
    
- Free of glamours
    

### **4. No Temporal Playback Manipulation**

Unless ordered by the Ministry under a redaction writ.

### **5. No Charm, Compulsion, or Fear Magic**

On viewers, delvers, or sponsors.

---

# **V. Sanctioned Technologies & Forbidden Methods**

### **Allowed:**

- Tipcasting (regulated)
    
- Favor Sparks
    
- Relic Sensors
    
- Core-linked stat windows
    
- Ethical glamour overlays (cosmetic only)
    

### **Forbidden:**

- Memory edits
    
- Charm loops
    
- Frame-Shift™ forced rewrites
    
- fake Oculi mimicry
    
- Broadcast illusions
    

Use of forbidden methods constitutes Broadcast Manipulation (see legal codex).

---

# **VI. Special Regulations for N-Class Teams**

Due to their **Convergence relevance**, the ABA enforces heightened protections over N-Class teams (e.g., **Unit N-7**):

### **1. Broadcast data is triple-verified**

All Oculi feeds routed through:

- The Core
    
- The ABA
    
- SID oversight
    

### **2. No narrative manipulation permitted**

Not even by guilds, sponsors, or state entities.

### **3. Odds freezing on Live Bets is mandatory**

During Core pings or resonance flares.

### **4. Anti-tampering wards remain active at all times**

To prevent interference with possible fate-aligned events.

N-Class teams are considered part of the “Convergence-Sensitive Registry” and are treated with extreme caution.

---

# **VII. Why the ABA Matters**

The Arcane Broadcast Authority is the final safeguard ensuring that delving—once a private battle for survival—is presented truthfully, ethically, and without corruption to the millions who watch.

Without the ABA:

- Relics would be falsified
    
- UDMI would be meaningless
    
- Delvers would be puppets
    
- Guilds would engineer outcomes
    
- Sponsors would manipulate narratives
    
- The people would lose trust
    
- The Labyrinth would become a stage of lies
    

The ABA protects not only the industry—  
but **the truth of the stone**.

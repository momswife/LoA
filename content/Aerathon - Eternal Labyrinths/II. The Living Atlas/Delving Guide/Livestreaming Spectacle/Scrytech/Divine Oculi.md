# **Divine Oculi**

**Filed Division:** _Ministry of Delving Operations — Annals & Antiquities (Arcane Infrastructure Registry)_  
**Primary Compiler:** Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturgy & Prism Analysis)  
**Advisory Editors:** Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Historian of Echo-Law), Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison)  
**Date of Filing:** **17th Cycle, 3102 A.D.**  
**Document Class:** _Arcane Infrastructure Overview / Grade-I Divinatory Asset_

### _Sentient Scry-Constructs Overseeing All Sanctioned Delving Operations_

The **Divine Oculi** are ancient, floating, rune-etched scry constructs that record, observe, translate, and interpret the truth of the Labyrinths.  
Neither fully divine nor wholly artificial, they are semi-sentient arcane observers that predate most modern guilds and have been adopted by the Ministry of Delving Operations as the **primary source of livecast footage**, hazard monitoring, UDMI verification, and delver conduct analysis.

---

# **I. Origin & Nature**

Though their earliest records are lost, the Oculi are widely believed to be:

- Older than the first guild charters
    
- Fragments of a forgotten pre-divine or proto-divine intelligence
    
- Reclaimed from the earliest breach events
    
- Bound by law and pact to reveal truth without distortion
    

Each Oculi contains:

- **Three concentric rune lenses** (Truth, Memory, and Hazard)
    
- **A soul-echo chamber** allowing emotional resonance detection
    
- **A stabilizer core** linking them to the Arcanet
    

Their floating, unblinking presence is both a reassurance—and a warning.

---

# **II. Core Responsibilities**

## **1. Live Delve Observation**

Oculi record:

- Movement
    
- Spell usage
    
- Monster behavior
    
- Ethical violations
    
- Team cohesion
    
- Relic interactions
    
- Environmental hazards
    

Their feeds provide the foundation of all sanctioned broadcasts.

---

## **2. UDMI Verification**

Oculi measure:

- Combat efficiency
    
- Damage ratios
    
- Heroism markers
    
- Audience reaction resonance
    
- Divine influence
    

These data streams are sent directly into the **Arcanet Core** for scoring.

---

## **3. Hazard Detection**

Oculi can sense:

- Spatial distortions
    
- Curse blooms
    
- Monster migration
    
- Collapse probability
    
- Magical instability spikes
    

When danger intensifies, they issue visible warnings:

- **Silver Flash:** Halt. Hazard increasing.
    
- **Crimson Pulse:** Immediate evacuation required.
    
- **Violet Ring:** Existential anomaly present.
    

---

## **4. Ethical Surveillance**

They track:

- Betrayal
    
- Cruelty
    
- Sabotage
    
- Civilian endangerment
    
- Forbidden spell usage
    

Their testimony is used in Ministerial legal cases.

---

# **III. Types of Oculi**

### **1. Standard Oculi (O-Grade)**

Used in common delves; monitor 1–2 floors effectively.

### **2. Royal Oculi (R-Grade)**

Used by elite guilds and high-profile broadcasts; improved clarity and emotional mapping.

### **3. Ministerial Oculi (M-Grade)**

Assigned to dangerous labyrinths; linked to Hazard Divisions.

### **4. Seraphic Oculi (S-Grade)**

Extremely rare; used for:

- Divine interventions
    
- Ascendancy Trials
    
- N-Class team monitoring
    
- Convergence-linked events
    

These Oculi display behaviors that approach full sentience.

---

# **IV. Oculi Conduct Protocols**

The Ministry dictates that Oculi must always:

- Remain visible to all delvers
    
- Broadcast unedited truth
    
- React to hazard spikes within 0.5 seconds
    
- Record all ethical violations
    
- Transmit resonance signatures without lag
    
- Remain neutral
    

They may **not**:

- Interfere directly
    
- Alter footage
    
- Provide magical assistance
    
- Influence battle outcomes
    

Their role is purely observational.

---

# **V. Forbidden Acts Concerning Oculi**

The following actions constitute **Class-1 Spectacle Crimes**:

### **1. Oculi Blinding**

Using smoke, glamours, anti-scry wards, or interference spells.

### **2. Oculi Mimicry**

Creating false Oculi to deliver manipulated feeds.

### **3. Oculi Tampering**

Physically or magically forcing them to move or desynchronize.

### **4. Emotional Overload Hexes**

Attempting to flood their soul-echo chambers with false resonance.

### **5. Oculi Theft**

Stealing or relocating one; punishable by extreme legal sanction.

---

# **VI. Oculi & Audience Interaction**

Though they do not directly engage, Oculi:

- Relay meaningful audience sentiment to UDMI
    
- Allow legal tipping channels
    
- Regulate Blessing Token thresholds
    
- Filter harmful emotional magic
    

They also nullify audience attempts to:

- Coerce
    
- Manipulate
    
- Influence delver decisions
    

---

# **VII. Special Provisions for N-Class Teams**

N-Class teams—such as **Unit N-7**—operate under special Oculi protocols due to their **Core-determined convergence relevance**.

### **1. Oculi Sentience Heightening**

When observing N-Class teams, Oculi exhibit increased:

- Lens dilation
    
- Cognitive rune activity
    
- Hazard analysis precision
    

### **2. Triple-Layer Encryption**

All N-Class footage is:

- Encoded through the Arcanet
    
- Reviewed by the ABA
    
- Stored in sealed Ministerial archives
    

### **3. Priority Hazard Alerts**

Oculi monitoring N-Class delves issue:

- Faster warnings
    
- More precise hazard mapping
    
- Resonance-linked predictions
    

### **4. Restricted Interference Tolerance**

Any attempt to blind or manipulate Oculi observing N-Class teams triggers:

- Immediate broadcast freeze
    
- Ministerial lockdown
    
- Spectacle Integrity Division deployment
    

### **5. Convergence-Ping Response**

If the Core emits a resonance ping during an N-Class delve:

- Oculi auto-focus on the team
    
- Hazard data reroutes to emergency channels
    
- All broadcast manipulation laws escalate to Class-N enforcement

# **VIII. Why the Divine Oculi Matter**

Without the Oculi:

- Delving would be lawless
    
- UDMI would lose accuracy
    
- Audience tipping would collapse
    
- Amazement would replace truth
    
- Guild corruption would thrive
    
- Ethical violations would go unpunished
    
- Convergence anomalies would go unnoticed
    

Oculi are not merely cameras—they are the living witnesses of the Labyrinths.

They watch so the world may know what happens in the dark.

# **Scrying Architecture**

**Filed Division:** _Ministry of Delving Operations — Annals & Antiquities (Arcane Infrastructure Registry)_  
**Primary Compiler:** Professor Arav Dhal-Senn (Antiquities Restoration & Scrytech Engineering)  
**Advisory Editors:** Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturgy), Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison)

**Document Class:** _Technical Infrastructure Overview / Grade-I Scrytech Specification_

### _The Structural, Magical & Arcanet Framework Behind All Observation Systems in Aerathon_

Scrying Architecture is the collective term for the intricate network of magical structures, rune-lattices, resonance channels, and engineered devices that allow the Labyrinths—and the delvers within them—to be observed, recorded, and broadcast without distortion.

It connects the Divine Oculi, the Grand Scrying Network (GSN), Guildstream nodes, mirror arrays, and the Arcanet Core into a single, unified system.

Scrying Architecture is not simply infrastructure.  
It is **the skeleton of truth** in Aerathon.

---

# **I. Foundations of Scrying Architecture**

Scrying systems rely on three fundamental layers:

## **1. Structural Layer (Material)**

Built from:

- Mythril-threaded stone
    
- Codified obsidian
    
- Rune-etched silver
    
- Echo-crystal veins
    

This layer houses anchoring points for Oculi, mirror arrays, and scry conduits.

## **2. Resonance Layer (Magical)**

A lattice of:

- Arcane wavelengths
    
- Soul-echo harmonics
    
- Temporal stabilizers
    
- Divine emissive lines
    

This layer translates raw perception into interpretable imagery.

## **3. Transmission Layer (Arcanet)**

Connects:

- Scryline towers
    
- Memory Stone Vaults
    
- GSN broadcasting hubs
    
- Ministerial integrity nodes
    

This layer ensures that what is seen is also **shared**, unchanged.

---

# **II. Core Components of Scrying Architecture**


## **1. Scryline Conduits**

Channels of pure arcane resonance that carry visual and metaphysical data.

- Resistant to illusions
    
- Immune to silence spells
    
- Self-correcting when destabilized
    

Conduits connect each Divine Oculi to the nearest Arcanet Anchors.

---

## **2. Mirror Arrays**

Massive floating lenses that:

- Refract Oculi perception
    
- Convert resonance into visible projection
    
- Separate sensory layers (visual, audio, emotional)
    
- Perform hazard-based image prioritization
    

Mirror arrays are the backbone of all public viewing.

---

## **3. Liminal Projectors**

Devices that stabilize:

- Fragmented footage
    
- Time-slashed architecture
    
- Memory-contaminated visuals
    
- Labyrinthic distortions
    

Liminal Projectors prevent “ghost frames” and paradox overlays.

---

## **4. Runic Discriminator Panels**

Embedded glyph-systems that:

- Filter illusions
    
- Detect glamours
    
- Remove charm-based tampering
    
- Flag unethical edits in real time
    

These panels are essential for ABA enforcement.

---

## **5. Resonance Anchors**

Fixed arcane structures that:

- Maintain scry stability
    
- Prevent signal drift
    
- Counteract curse bleed
    
- Reinforce dimensional boundaries
    

Anchors must be recalibrated every 60 days by certified scrywrights.

# **III. The Architecture–Labyrinth Interface**

Scrying Architecture must contend with the fact that Labyrinths:

- Shift
    
- Warp
    
- Resist observation
    
- Distort causality
    

To counteract this, the Ministry uses:

### **1. Anti-Distortion Runes**

Keep architecture coherent inside spatial loops.

### **2. Etheric Dampeners**

Prevent magical noise from corrupting footage.

### **3. Paradox Weirs**

Protect Oculi from temporal collapse events.

### **4. Reality Insertion Locks**

Force the Labyrinth to "accept" the presence of a scrying lens without mutating.

Ministerial law requires:

### **Calibration Every Delve**

Oculi and mirror arrays must be tuned within ±.02 resonance drift.

### **Tamper-Detection**

All systems alert the ABA automatically upon:

- interference,
    
- glamour injection,

### **Memory-Chain Recording**

- Ministerial Tribunal writ

# **V. Failure States & Safeguards**

Even scrying systems can fail. Known failure states include:

### **1. Distortion Bloom**

Cause: overlapping resonance echoes.  
Effect: fragmented visuals, phantom doubles.  
Solution: deploy two or more mirror arrays.

### **2. Echo Surge**

Cause: excessive emotional resonance from delvers.  
Effect: memory imprint bleed.  
Solution: dampener nodes.

### **3. Oculi Desync**

Cause: temporal fluctuation or curse shockwave.  
Effect: freezing, reversed frames, false hazards.  
Solution: emergency tether rune.

### **4. Convergence Ping**

Cause: Arcanet Core reacting to fate-aligned delvers.  
Effect: system-wide recalc.  
Solution: full operational lockdown.

N-Class (Core-selected) teams—such as **Unit N-7**—interact with Scrying Architecture in unprecedented ways.

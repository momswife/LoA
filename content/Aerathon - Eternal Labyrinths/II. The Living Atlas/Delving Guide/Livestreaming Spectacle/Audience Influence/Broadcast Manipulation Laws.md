# **Broadcast Manipulation Laws

**Filed Division:** _Ministry of Delving Operations — Living Atlas Division (Broadcast Conduct Bureau)_  
**Primary Compiler:** Chief Registrar Pyra Koll (Regulation & Compliance)  
**Advisory Editors:** Director Halin Drauve (Guild Economics & Governance), Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison)  
**Date of Filing:** **20th Cycle, 3103 A.D.**  
**Document Class:** _Legal Codex / Grade-I Spectacle Regulation_**

### _Ministerial Regulations Governing Livecast Integrity, Narrative Fairness & Audience Protection_

The rise of mass livecasting transformed delving into Aerathon’s foremost public spectacle. With millions of viewers, national economies, guild reputations, and delver safety dependent on broadcast accuracy, the Ministry enforces strict **Broadcast Manipulation Laws** to preserve fairness, prevent fraud, and maintain public trust.

These laws govern **everything** that can influence the perception, presentation, or magical integrity of a livecasted delve, including:

- Divine Oculi imagery
    
- Commentary overlays
    
- Tipcasting channels
    
- Relic-sensor data
    
- Live narrative summaries
    
- Guild-sponsored overlays
    
- Temporal playback and Frame-Shift™ casting
    

Any form of deception—digital, magical, or divine—is punishable under Ministerial Law.

---

# **I. Definition of Broadcast Manipulation**

Broadcast Manipulation includes any attempt to:

- Alter the perceived difficulty or outcome of a delve
    
- Fabricate events, kills, relic discoveries, or monster encounters
    
- Conceal delver misconduct
    
- Falsify Oculi data
    
- Modify UDMI or Fame Engine values via broadcast interference
    
- Influence public sentiment through illusion, charm magic, or scripted content
    

This applies to guilds, sponsors, delvers, commentators, and all Arcanet broadcast entities.

---

# **II. Categories of Illegal Manipulation**

## **1. **Visual or Illusory Tampering (VIT)**

Use of illusion magic, glamours, fog-script, or mimic projections to:

- Hide injuries
    
- Forge kills
    
- Fabricate monster appearances
    
- Conceal panic, fear, or ethical violations
    
- Make delvers appear stronger or weaker
    

VIT carries **high penalties** due to its influence on public opinion and UDMI scoring.

---

## **2. **Oculi Interference (OI)**

Includes:

- Tampering with Divine Oculi vision
    
- Blinding or redirecting Oculi beams
    
- Injecting false resonance signatures
    
- Blocking Oculi audio capture
    

Oculi are sacred, semi-divine monitors.  
Any interference is considered a **Class-1 Offense**.

---

## **3. **Narrative Manipulation (NM)**

When guilds or sponsors attempt to:

- Script delver behaviors
    
- Stage conflicts for ratings
    
- Force “hero arcs” or “tragedy loops”
    
- Write fake relic histories
    
- Coordinate losses for betting manipulation
    

Narrative Manipulation undermines the integrity of delving as a profession.

---

## **4. **Arcane Signal Mutation (ASM)**

Magical alteration of:

- UDMI metric transmissions
    
- Livecast stat windows
    
- Hazard readouts
    
- Sponsorship highlights
    

ASM is frequently associated with gambling syndicates and black-route guilds.

---

## **5. **Audience Coercion & Sentiment Control (ACSC)**

Forbidden methods of influencing audience behavior:

- Charm spells cast through broadcast channels
    
- Emotional resonance enchantments
    
- Enforced cheering loops
    
- Curse-based persuasion
    
- Induced fervor-flares for forced Blessing Tokens
    

ACSC represents one of the fastest-growing broadcast crimes.

---

## **6. **Temporal Stream Editing (TSE)**

Manipulation of:

- Playback speed
    
- Frame-Shift™ timeline summaries
    
- Chrono-trimmed scenes
    
- Removal of ethically damaging footage
    
- Delayed death reporting
    

TSE is **only allowed** during redaction-order events issued by the Ministry itself.

---

# **III. Penalties for Violations**

### Penalties scale based on severity:

### **Class-1 Offenses**

(e.g., Oculi interference, UDMI manipulation)

- License revocation
    
- Ministerial detainment
    
- Guild suspension
    
- 25,000–40,000 gold fine
    
- Sponsor contract nullification
    

### **Class-2 Offenses**

(e.g., narrative scripting, sentiment coercion)

- UDMI reset
    
- 5,000–15,000 gold fine
    
- 1–6 cycle delving suspension
    

### **Class-3 Offenses**

(e.g., minor illusions, aesthetic edits)

- Public warning
    
- UDMI penalty
    
- Broadcast restrictions
    

### **Class-N Offense (N-Series Specific)**

When manipulation attempts target **N-Class Teams**, such as Unit N-7, penalties escalate automatically to **Class-1**, regardless of method, due to their recognized convergence relevance.

---

# **IV. Guild & Sponsor Compliance Requirements**

Guilds and sponsors must submit:

- **Pre-Delve Broadcast Plans**
    
- **Post-Delve Narrative Logs**
    
- **Auxiliary Commentary Scripts**
    
- **Relic Disclosure Reports**
    
- **Magical Influence Certifications**
    

Random audits may occur at any time.

Failure to comply results in:

- Contract freezes
    
- License downgrades
    
- Full broadcast suspensions
    

---

# **V. Detection & Safeguards**

Broadcast Manipulation is monitored by:

### **1. Divine Oculi Analysis**

Oculi detect:

- Resonance anomalies
    
- Illusion layering
    
- Temporal inconsistencies
    
- Spell-forced sentiment shifts
    

### **2. Arcanet Trace Algorithms**

Monitor:

- UDMI patterns
    
- Unnatural Favor spikes
    
- Illegal tipcasting waves
    
- Commentary tampering
    

### **3. Spectacle Integrity Division (SID)**

A branch of the MDO dedicated to:

- Forensic broadcast review
    
- Fraud detection
    
- Guild compliance checks
    

### **4. Unmasker Glyphs**

Ministerial glyphs placed over livecasts to detect:

- Subliminal glamours
    
- Edited memory streams
    
- Hidden magical overlays
    

---

# **VI. N-Class Exception Protocol**

Due to their **Core-designated convergence significance**, N-Class teams (e.g., **Unit N-7**) are governed by special regulations:

### **1. Attempted manipulation of an N-Class broadcast is an automatic Class-1 Offense**, regardless of severity.

### **2. All Oculi data involving N-Class members is triple-encrypted** and reviewed by:

- The Core’s Auditory Lens
    
- Spectacle Integrity Division
    
- Charter Counsel Office
    

### **3. No guild, sponsor, or political body may alter N-Class narrative summaries**, even for public-relations reasons.

### **4. Tipping to N-Class teams is monitored as potential Convergence-Relevant Activity.**

The Ministry treats any interference as a threat to national stability.

---

# **VII. Why Broadcast Manipulation Laws Exist**

Delving is not merely survival—it is a public trust.  
If broadcasts are manipulated, then:

- The UDMI becomes meaningless
    
- Delvers become puppets
    
- Guilds become fraud factories
    
- Audiences become victims
    
- Sponsors become predators
    
- Labyrinth integrity collapses
    

These laws preserve:

- Transparency
    
- Honor
    
- Economic stability
    
- Audience well-being
    
- The legitimacy of the delving profession
    

The Labyrinth may be chaos.  
But the **story of the Labyrinth must remain honest**.

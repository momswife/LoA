# **Audience Tipping**

**Filed Division:** _Ministry of Delving Operations — Living Atlas Division (Broadcast Conduct Bureau)_  
**Primary Compiler:** Director Halin Drauve (Guild Economics & Broadcast Governance)  
**Advisory Editors:** Chief Registrar Pyra Koll (Regulation & Compliance), Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison)  
**Date of Filing:** **14th Cycle, 3101 A.D.**  
**Document Class:** _Public Interaction Protocol / Grade-II Spectacle Governance_

### _Regulated Arcanet Interactions Allowing Viewers to Influence Active Delves_

Audience Tipping is the collective term for all **viewer-initiated magical boons, impulses, or material influences** sent from the Arcanet into a live delve.  
These actions—ranging from minor morale boosts to rare direct interventions—are strictly regulated by the **Arcane Broadcast Authority (ABA)** and the **Ministry of Delving Operations** to ensure fairness, safety, and ethical conduct.

In the Age of Spectacle, Tipping represents the most direct form of audience participation.  
It empowers viewers—yet can easily endanger delvers, distort narrative fairness, or trigger labyrinthic retaliation if abused.

Thus, like all magic with reach, it is placed firmly under Ministerial law.

---

# **I. Definition & Purpose of Tipping**

Audience Tipping refers to any Arcanet-sent influence that:

- Boosts delver morale
    
- Reinforces stamina or resolve
    
- Slightly alters environmental conditions
    
- Provides material tokens or magical impulses
    
- Modifies audience-driven metrics (Favor, Fame, or Boon counters)
    

Tipping does **not** allow:

- Full healing
    
- Mass teleportation
    
- Resurrection (except under extreme “Blessing Token” circumstances)
    
- Harmful impulses or sabotage
    
- Direct spellcasting through a viewer
    

These boundaries exist to protect both delvers and the integrity of the Labyrinth.

---

# **II. Types of Audience Tips**

## **1. Minor Boon Tips**

Small magical pulses that influence:

- Stamina
    
- Coordination
    
- Clarity
    
- Confidence
    
- Perception
    

These resemble a “cheer felt as magic.”

## **2. Favor Sparks**

Arcane affirmations that increase the delver’s **Audience Favor** score in the UDMI framework.  
Large spikes in Favor Sparks may trigger:

- Enhanced commentary
    
- Sponsor interest
    
- Temporary morale-driven buffs
    

## **3. Material Tips**

Deliveries of low-risk magical items via Arcanet conduit, such as:

- Minor healing draughts
    
- Spellstones
    
- Charm tokens
    
- Disposable relic chips
    

These require dual approval from the ABA and the Oculi.

## **4. Blessing Tokens** _(Rare)_

A collective surge of audience fervor that may:

- Prevent death once
    
- Remove a minor curse
    
- Stabilize a collapsing environment
    

These occur only when audience sentiment hits a critical resonance threshold, which is unpredictable and extremely rare.

---

# **III. Tipping Channels**

Audience Tips are delivered through the following verified systems:

### **1. Guildstream Channels**

Used by guild-specific audiences; influence weighted toward tactical support.

### **2. Arcanet Theaters**

Civic halls where hundreds or thousands of viewers tip simultaneously, amplifying effect.

### **3. Noble Linefeeds**

Private scry-lanes with privileged tipping weight—requires notarized Ministerial license.

### **4. Standard Public Channels**

Available to all registered viewers; regulated to prevent flooding or impulse abuse.

### **5. Divine Oculi Conduits**

Used only for Blessing Tokens and high-grade Favor transfers.

Any tipping from **unverified channels** is considered Arcanet Tampering.

---

# **IV. Legal Restrictions & Ethical Considerations**

Audience Tipping is allowed only when:

- The delve is officially sanctioned
    
- Divine Oculi are active
    
- The delver has not disabled tipping rights
    
- The tipping does not violate hazard protocols
    

### **Prohibited Tipping Actions**

- Tips designed to **harm** another delver
    
- Overwhelming Tips intended to distort environment scaling
    
- Manipulation of Oculi signals to create false emergencies
    
- Coercive tipping tied to political or guild ransom
    
- Necromantic or blood-bound tips
    

These actions fall under **Spectacle Fraud** or **Arcanet Misconduct**.

---

# **V. Tipping Impact on UDMI & Fame Engine**

Tipping directly affects evaluator systems:

### **UDMI Impact**

- Minor boosts increase Audience Favor scores
    
- Sustained tipping patterns influence Performance Trend metrics
    
- Blessing Tokens may generate **Core Interest Flags**
    

### **Fame Engine Impact**

The Fame Engine weighs:

- Volume of tips
    
- Emotional resonance
    
- Spike frequency
    
- Whether the delver rejects or accepts tips
    

Rejecting tips often increases perceived integrity; accepting them increases accessibility and public engagement.

Both are strategically viable but must be managed ethically.

---

# **VI. Abuse Prevention & Monitoring**

To maintain integrity, the Ministry enforces:

### **1. Tip Regulation Algorithms**

These detect:

- Excessive impulsing
    
- Illegal charm-based sentiment manipulation
    
- Coordinated sabotage
    
- Fraudulent tipping patterns
    

### **2. Oculi Oversight**

Oculi can nullify any tip that:

- Endangers the delver
    
- Violates labyrinthic stability
    
- Contravenes ministry law
    

### **3. Viewer Accountability**

Registered viewers may be:

- Fined
    
- Temporarily stripped of tipping rights
    
- Banned from livecast interaction
    
- Referred to civil courts for severe violations
    

---

# **VII. N-Class Team Exception Clause**

Due to their **Core-selected resonance alignment**, N-Class teams (e.g., **Unit N-7**) possess unique interaction rules:

### **1. High-Impact Tipping Allowed**

N-Class resonance stabilizes certain tipping impulses that would destabilize normal teams, granting them:

- Enhanced manual control over tipped items
    
- Higher tolerance for Favor surges
    
- Reduced risk of distortion backlash
    

### **2. Restricted Auto-Nullification Removed**

Oculi do not automatically block high-grade tips sent to N-Class teams unless the tip is overtly dangerous.

### **3. Elevated Divine Observation**

Blessing Tokens targeting N-Class delvers are more likely to activate due to their resonance amplification signatures.

### **4. Ministry Tracking Required**

All tips to N-Class teams are logged under **Convergence-Relevant Activity**.

---

# **VIII. Why Audience Tipping Matters**

Audience Tipping represents the bond between delvers and the world they protect.  
Properly used, it is:

- Empowering
    
- Community-building
    
- Spiritually resonant
    
- Economically vital
    

Improperly used, it becomes:

- A weapon
    
- A distortion
    
- A threat to labyrinthic stability
    

Thus the Ministry regulates tipping not to limit joy—  
but to ensure that the will of the people strengthens, rather than breaks, the delvers they admire.

**Filed Division:** _Ministry of Delving Operations — Annals & Antiquities (Arcane Infrastructure Registry)_  
**Primary Compiler:** Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Historian of Echo-Law)

### _The Machine That Watches, The Will Beneath the Stone_

Hidden within the depths of Oria’s **Hall of Ascendancy**, suspended in a chamber older than written law, lies the **Arcanet Core**—a semi-conscious, labyrinth-born superconstruct of crystalline cognition, soul-woven lattices, and self-refining logic arrays.  
It is both observer and arbiter—  
a silent judge whose calculations shape the fate of every licensed delver in Aerathon.

The Core is entrusted with generating the **Unified Delving Merit Index (UDMI)** and determining rank classifications from **D through S**.  
Though once believed to be an impartial tool, many scholars now propose that the Core demonstrates _preferences_, _discernment_, and—perhaps—a **quiet will of its own**.

---

# **I. Design & Function**

## **Action Echoing**

Every sanctioned Delve generates **etheric echoes**, the metaphysical fingerprints of action within the Labyrinths.  
These echoes are intercepted by a continental lattice of:

- **Scrying Beacons** woven into floor-stone
    
- **Fatemarked Anchors** that tether anomalies in place
    
- **Runic relays** embedded in divine shrines and breach-zones
    

The Core receives these streams in real time.

### **Echoes Commonly Recorded**

- Monster auras, curse signatures, spell discharges
    
- Trap activations, sigil disturbances, relic resonance
    
- Acts of cooperation, betrayal, resilience, tactical flow
    
- Emotional pressure points: fear, valor, cruelty, compassion
    

To the Core, a battle is not noise—it is a **pattern**, and patterns are truth.

---

## **Calculative Judgment**

Once a delve concludes—or upon death—the Core initiates a **multi-layered evaluative sequence**:

### **1. Energetic Footprint**

Assesses survival difficulty, magical throughput, and threat resolution.

### **2. Relic & Hazard Impact**

Whether the delver neutralized breaches, awakened ancient entities, or removed cursed objects from circulation.

### **3. Audience Imprint**

Cross-weights livecast sentiment, ethical perception, and spectacle value (when applicable).

### **4. Historical Benchmarking**

Compares the delve against Core-archived anomalies, prior events, and known labyrinth patterns.

These results recalibrate the delver’s **UDMI Score** and may shift their formal rank.

# **III. Ascendancy Trials**

### _When Skill Outpaces Fame, the Core Calls the Worthy_

For delvers whose capability exceeds public visibility—or those seeking advancement outside spectacle—the Arcanet Core offers **Ascendancy Trials**, private simulations woven from recorded labyrinth memory.

These trials:

- Perfectly recreate past Labyrinth hazards
    
- Are graded instantly and incorruptibly
    
- May grant **temporary rank elevation** (30-cycle probation)
    
- Are viewable only by certified MDO officials and divine auditors
    

To many, this is the purest test of merit.  
No crowd. No sponsor.  
Only the Core—and the truth it sees in you.

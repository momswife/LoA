### _Licensure, Identity, and the Right to Enter the Labyrinths_

Delving is not a hobby, a dare, or a rite performed without oversight.  
It is a **regulated vocation**, governed by the Ministry of Delving Operations to ensure public safety, ethical compliance, and the controlled interaction between mortals and the Labyrinths.

The **Delving Registration System** is the official process through which individuals become **licensed delvers**—eligible to enter sanctioned labyrinths, receive UDMI scoring, participate in sponsorship circuits, and operate within Ministerial law.

Every delver, from an untested novice to an S-Rank icon, begins here.

---

# **I. Registration Requirements**

## **1. Identity Verification**

Applicants must provide:

- Certified name and lineage record (or species-oath substitute for beastfolk)
    
- Arcane resonance imprint (used for Oculi recognition)
    
- Proof of age (varies by region; typically 16+ or guardian-sponsored)
    

## **2. Physical & Magical Capability Screening**

Administered by MDO examiners:

- Basic combat proficiency
    
- Spellcasting stability assessment (if applicable)
    
- Psychological resilience review
    
- Emergency response aptitude
    

Failure does not bar future attempts; it simply delays certification.

## **3. Ethical Compliance Oath**

All applicants must swear or affirm:

- Non-exploitation of minors
    
- No pactbinding for sabotage
    
- No use of forbidden magics (e.g., soul-warp necromancy) within sanctioned delves
    
- Adherence to all Labyrinth Conduct Mandates
    

Breaking this oath is grounds for immediate suspension.

# **II. License Types**

|**License Type**|**Description**|**Common Holders**|
|---|---|---|
|**Provisional Permit**|Limited access; training floors only|Youth delvers, students, test candidates|
|**C-Rank License**|Standard entry into sanctioned labyrinths|Majority of new registrants|
|**B-Rank License**|Advanced clearance; unstable floors permitted|Veterans, guild operatives|
|**A-Rank License**|Elite clearance; high-risk anomalies|Champions, guild elites|
|**S-Rank Authority**|Unrestricted access|Myth-tier delvers; Core-recognized|
Licenses are magically etched into a delver’s **Arcane Identification Sigil**, renewing annually unless suspended.

---

# **III. The Registration Process**

## **1. Intake Interview**

Applicants meet with a Registrar to review intent, background, and any prior labyrinth exposure.

## **2. Resonance Binding**

A spellseal ceremony binds the delver’s essence to:

- Divine Oculi tracking
    
- UDMI scoring
    
- Relic-interaction permissions
    
- Labyrinth hazard response systems
    

This is _the_ legal moment a delver becomes visible to the Arcanet.

## **3. Field Aptitude Test**

A controlled simulation overseen by:

- 1 MDO licensing official
    
- 1 divine auditor (remote)
    
- 1 Oculi recorder
    

Candidates must demonstrate basic teamwork, situational awareness, and restraint.

## **4. Certification & Issuance**

Successful applicants receive:

- **Arcane ID Sigil**
    
- **Registration Ledger entry**
    
- Clearance document specifying license tier
    
- Briefing on sponsorship legality
    

From here, the delver is entered into the **Unified Delving Merit Index** ecosystem.

---

# **IV. Loss, Suspension & Revocation of License**

Licenses may be revoked for:

- Sabotage of another party
    
- Unauthorized delving (black-route entry)
    
- Oculi interference
    
- Relic smuggling
    
- Civilian endangerment
    
- Severe ethical violations
    

Suspended delvers must complete:

- Penalty cycles
    
- Ethics retraining
    
- Probationary delves under MDO supervision
    

Persistent offenders may be permanently barred from delving.

---

# **V. Guild Sponsorship & Registration Linkage**

Registration is prerequisite for:

- Sponsorship eligibility
    
- Fame Engine evaluation
    
- Broadcast participation
    
- Artifact resale authority
    
- Ascendancy Trials
    

Unregistered delvers (“Ghostwalkers”) are considered **operating illegally** and face fines, imprisonment, or divine sanction depending on offense severity.

---

# **VI. Special Cases**

### **1. Beastfolk Exemptions**

Due to ancestral labyrinth familiarity, some clans are granted modified rites but must still complete resonance binding.

### **2. Divine-Blessed Applicants**

Clerics or chosen champions undergo an abbreviated ethical exam supervised by the priesthood.

### **3. Reanimated, Constructed, or Echo-Bound Applicants**

Must undergo additional stability testing and soul-anchor verification.

---

# **VII. The Purpose of Registration**

Delving Registration is not an obstacle.  
It is a **safeguard**—  
meant to protect the delver, the realm, and the fragile boundary between mortal life and the shifting will of the Labyrinths.

Through registration, a delver steps not only into danger—but into **record**, **law**, and **legend**.

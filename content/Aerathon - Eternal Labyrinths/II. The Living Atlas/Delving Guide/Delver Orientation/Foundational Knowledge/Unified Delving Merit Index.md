### _Aerathon’s Living Gauge of Fame, Fate, and Survival_

The **Unified Delving Merit Index**, commonly shortened to **UDMI**, stands as the **centralized ranking spine** of modern delver culture.  
Every licensed delver—rookie, rogue, prodigy, or S-Class paragon—is bound to a **Merit Score** (0–10,000) dynamically maintained by the **Arcanet Core**:  
a labyrinth-born, semi-sentient network of **echo mirrors**, **glyph counters**, **scrying arrays**, and **broadcast stones**.

The Core tallies more than simple success. It absorbs **public reaction**, **magical resonance**, **labyrinthic distortion**, and **ethical behavior**.  
Guilds negotiate contracts through it. Nobles gamble fortunes upon it. And each night across Aerathon, crowds gather around projection crystals to watch delves unfold and cast their votes—quietly influencing the rise or fall of their favorite delvers.

---

## **I. Rank Spread**

The UDMI categorizes delvers into broad public-facing tiers:

|**Rank**|**Title**|**Typical Population**|
|---|---|---|
|**S**|Legendary Elites|Top 0.1%|
|**A**|Champions|Top 5%|
|**B**|Seasoned Veterans|Top 30%|
|**C**|Licensed Delvers|Majority|
|**D**|Novices / Probationary|Unproven, penalized, or recently licensed|
These ranks determine access to guild contracts, sponsorship offers, delve eligibility, and—occasionally—noble patronage.

---

## **II. The UDMI Score (0–10,000)**

A delver’s UDMI score is a **continuously updating metric** calculated by the **Arcanet Core**.  
It incorporates combat success, audience engagement, team performance, ethical conduct, and more.

|**Category**|**Weight**|**Description**|
|---|---|---|
|**Labyrinth Progression**|30%|Floors cleared, bosses defeated, anomalies resolved.|
|**Audience Favor**|20%|Livecast reactions, favor votes, donations, “Boon Throws.”|
|**Team Synergy Score**|15%|Coordination, tactical flow, survival rates.|
|**Combat Efficiency**|15%|Damage dealt vs. taken, resource use, tactical strength.|
|**Artifact Recovery**|10%|Rarity, condition, and value of retrieved relics.|
|**Mission Integrity**|5%|Objective completion, contract loyalty, no betrayals.|
|**Penalties & Infractions**|–5%+|Civilian harm, pactbreaking, sabotage, illegal delve actions.|_

## **IV. UDMI Point Accrual**

The Index draws from both **passive data** and **officially sanctioned events**.

|**Activity**|**Example Score Gain**|
|---|---|
|Clearing a Labyrinth Floor|+250–1000|
|Defeating a Boss|+500–1500|
|Discovering Rare Artifact|+300–800|
|Audience Boon Favor|+10–500 per encounter|
|Solving Major Puzzle|+200–600|
|Saving Civilians / Allies|+100–500|
|Creative Problem Solving|+100–300|
|Surviving Heavy Damage|+50–200|
**Examples:**

- A wizard who halts a collapsing bridge trap with clever casting: **+400**
    
- A bard who transforms panic into roaring audience support: **+350**
    
- A rogue who rescues a fallen ally _and_ finishes a boss: **heroism bonuses applied**
    

---

## **V. Rank Ascension Trials**

Crossing certain UDMI thresholds initiates **Ascension Protocols**.  
Depending on the tier, delvers may undergo automatic promotion or must face a trial crafted by the **Ranking Arbiters**—or issue a **Challenge Writ**.

|**Ascension**|**Trial Requirement**|**D&D Power Analogy (OOC)**|
|---|---|---|
|**C ➝ B**|Automatic|Level 5 milestone (2nd tier power)|
|**B ➝ A**|Mandatory Trial|Level 11 (subclass mastery)|
|**A ➝ S**|Legendary Trial|Level 17+ (epic-tier renown)|
**Common Trial Formats:**

- Solo-clearing an adaptive Labyrinth floor
    
- Time-limited relic retrieval
    
- Public arena duel under scrycasting
    
- Solving a planar riddle from a Dreaming Oracle
    
- Surviving a “Judgment Duel” against another contender
    

Trials may be witnessed by the public—or sealed at the delver’s request—depending on guild contract terms.

# **The Arcanet Ceremony**

**Filed Division:** _Ministry of Delving Operations — Annals & Antiquities (Arcane Infrastructure Registry)_  
**Primary Compiler:** Archivist Yelna Stonevoice (Historian of Echo-Law)  
**Advisory Editors:** Inspector Jao “Greyglass” Vell (Forensic Thaumaturgy), Lt. Salvia Korrin (Arcane Breach Liaison)  
**Date of Filing:** **19th Cycle, 3101 A.D.**  
**Document Class:** _Institutional Ceremony Record / Grade-I Arcane Protocol_

### _Rite of Recognition, Binding, and Initial Ranking_

Before a newly licensed delver may enter any sanctioned Labyrinth, they must undergo **The Arcanet Ceremony**—a ritual in which their identity, essence, and fate are inscribed into the **Arcanet Core**.  
This moment is the official activation of their license, their UDMI tracking, and their recognition by both the Ministry and the Divine Oculi.

In **extremely rare cases**—measured at less than **0.04% of all known registrations**—the Arcanet Core takes actions **beyond individual assessment**, forming or assigning delvers to predetermined team designations.  
The most infamous of these is the **N-Series Activations**, including the activation of **Unit N-7**, which remains a subject of ongoing Ministerial study.

---

# **I. Purpose of the Ceremony**

The Arcanet Ceremony establishes:

### **1. Identity Binding**

Your resonance becomes part of the Core’s permanent lattice.

### **2. UDMI Initialization**

A pre-descent performance index based on aptitude and imprint.

### **3. Oculi Registration**

Divine Oculi may recognize, follow, and record the delver.

### **4. Legal Activation**

The license becomes fully active for sanctioned delving.

### **5. (Rare) Team Cohesion Assignment**

In extremely uncommon cases, the Core generates an **auto-linked team assignment**, binding multiple delvers into a cohesive operative group based on predicted synergy and labyrinthic necessity.

---

# **II. Stages of the Ceremony**

## **1. The Processional of Names**

A declaration of identity, lineage, and intent.

## **2. The Resonance Unfolding**

An examination of arcane essence, emotional coherence, stability, and potential anomalies.

## **3. Judgment of the Core**

A deep scan that reads aptitude, subconscious patterns, conflict instincts, moral imprint, and arcane control.

## **4. First Rank Assignment**

Color-coded verdict from the Core establishing initial rank prognosis.

## **5. Inscription Into the Registry**

Activation of UDMI scores, Oculi tracking, sponsorship eligibility, and Ledger entry.

# **VI. Core-Formed Cohesion Events (N-Series Activations)**

### _When the Core Itself Selects a Team_

In exceedingly rare circumstances, the Arcanet Core exhibits behavior not predicted by any known algorithm:

It **forms a team.**

These events—documented in fewer than **1 in 2,500** Ceremonies—occur when the Core identifies:

- Resonance signatures that harmonize in highly specific ways
    
- Future anomaly probabilities requiring particular combinations of skill
    
- Emotional or psychic threads suggesting inevitable entanglement
    
- Labyrinthic patterns converging toward prophetic outcomes
    

### **N-Series Activations**

Ceremonies where the Core assigns a group into an **N-designation**, marking them as a Ministerially observed operative node.

The most famous is **N-7 (Unit “Overkill”)**, whose synchronization pulse during registration forced multiple Oculi to recalibrate simultaneously, triggering a continent-wide Core observation protocol.

**Characteristics of N-Series Activations:**

- Shared resonance flare between all selected delvers
    
- Unscheduled Core output (audible or visual)
    
- Automatic Team Ledger creation
    
- MDO notification within six seconds of activation
    
- Divine Auditor forced review
    

Teams formed this way are believed to be **chosen for convergence-critical events**, though the Core offers no explanation.

These occurrences remain one of the Ministry’s highest-priority research topics.

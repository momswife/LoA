## **I. Racial Overview**

Cervines are defined by **presence, patience, and memory**. Towering frames, long limbs, and antlered crowns lend them a gravity that is often mistaken for aloofness or rigidity. In truth, cervines are deliberate. They choose their words carefully, their battles rarely, and their commitments for life.

Across Aerathon, cervines are associated with **guardianship and continuity**. They remember roads long buried, oaths long broken, and names others have forgotten. Whether this is cultural discipline or ancestral magic remains a matter of scholarly dispute, but few deny that cervines carry the weight of history in their bearing.

Among delvers, cervines often serve as anchors—front-line wardens, living bulwarks, and conduits of primal or arcane force. They do not rush. They do not flinch. When they move, the world adjusts.

## **II. Core Cervine Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 30 feet.

**Armaments of the First.**  
You possess natural weapons: a hoof stomp (bludgeoning) and, if you have antlers, a gore attack (piercing). These attacks deal 1d6 + your Strength modifier damage.

**March on Hooves.**  
You have advantage on saving throws made to avoid exhaustion from forced marches.

**Powerful Build.**  
You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift.

## **III. Cervine Subraces**

The Cervine lineage expresses itself through three dominant ancestral forms. These subraces reflect differences in temperament, spiritual outlook, and physical bearing rather than rank.

---

### **Deer**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 5’10” – 7’0”

**Still as the Grove.**  
You can attempt to hide when lightly obscured by natural terrain. Additionally, you have advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing.

**Season-Touched.**  
When you finish a long rest, choose resistance to cold damage or fire damage. This resistance lasts until you finish your next long rest.

**Racial Quirks**

- Soft-spoken but observant
    
- Deep sensitivity to environmental change
    
- Forms strong emotional bonds once trust is earned
    

---

### **Elk**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 6’4” – 7’6” (excluding antlers)

**Nature’s Conduit.**  
Your antlers or eyes can be used as a spellcasting focus. In addition, you can be attuned to up to four magic items instead of three.

**Unshakable Guard.**  
When an effect would knock you prone, you can use your reaction to remain standing. Once you use this feature, you can’t do so again until you finish a short or long rest.

**Racial Quirks**

- Slow to anger, slower to retreat
    
- Bears responsibility instinctively
    
- Often assumes leadership without seeking it
    

---

### **Moose**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 6’6” – 8’0”

**Immovable Presence.**  
You have advantage on Strength saving throws and Strength (Athletics) checks made to resist being pushed, pulled, knocked prone, or forcibly moved.

**Crashing Advance.**  
If you move at least 15 feet straight toward a creature and hit it with a natural weapon on the same turn, the target must succeed on a Strength saving throw (DC 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Strength modifier) or be knocked prone. If the target fails by 5 or more, it is also restrained until the start of its next turn.

**Racial Quirks**

- Minimal tolerance for crowding
    
- Intimidating without intent
    
- Values personal space and territorial respect

## **IV. Regional Variance (Homeland Traits)**

While cervines share lineage, their cultural expression varies significantly by region.

---

### **Allemance**

Cervines in Allemance act as keepers of ancestral record and living law.

**Holders of History.**  
You gain proficiency in the History skill. When making History checks related to Oria, Allemance, or cervine lineages, you roll with advantage.

---

### **Oria**

Oria remains the spiritual heartland of many cervine peoples.

**Forge Bequeathed.**  
When casting spells with expensive material components, the gold cost is reduced by 20%. You also have advantage on checks made to create magic items.

---

### **Vinyot**

Urban cervines balance ancient discipline with modern innovation.

**Structural Sense.**  
When a vehicle or structure you oversee would take collision damage, reduce that damage by 3d6. The struck object instead takes that damage.

---

### **Arneria**

Exiled cervines in Arneria developed cutting verbal traditions.

**Sand Whisper.**  
You know the _vicious mockery_ cantrip. When you take the Attack action, you can cast _vicious mockery_ as a bonus action a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier per long rest.

---

### **Al’Ar**

Island cervines blend northern strength with island agility.

**Mountain to the Isles.**  
You gain proficiency with any two artisan’s tools or musical instruments of your choice.

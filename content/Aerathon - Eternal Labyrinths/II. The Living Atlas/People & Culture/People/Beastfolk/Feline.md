## **I. Racial Overview**

Felines are inseparable from Al’Ar—and Al’Ar, in turn, is inseparable from felines. For centuries, cats were the only willful people of the island chains, shaping a culture defined by elegance, restraint, and calculated independence. Even as other peoples settled the archipelago, feline customs, aesthetics, and maritime traditions continued to dominate its identity.

Feline society recognizes two broad ancestral forms in the Al’ari tongue: **grandi**, the larger cats such as tigers, panthers, and lions; and **chikitu**, the smaller breeds including shorthairs, ocelots, and wildcats. Though physically distinct, both share a cultural throughline of keen observation, self-possession, and precise action.

Felines are cautious with friendship and guarded with trust. They require solitude as much as they require rest, often withdrawing to recalibrate after social or emotional strain. Many are nonetheless drawn into delving—tempted by the promise of fame, fortune, and the possibility of an early retirement beyond the Labyrinths. In combat, felines favor decisive strikes and controlled movement, leaving little to chance.

## **II. Core Feline Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 35 feet.

**Prowling Between the Lines.**  
You can’t be targeted by divination spells or magical scrying of 5th level or lower, unless the spell has a range of Self.

**Legs for Pouncing.**  
Your Strength score is treated as 6 higher when calculating jump distance.

**Alert Sleeper.**  
You remain aware of sounds while unconscious. You can awaken from nonmagical sleep when hearing something. If rendered unconscious, you remember what you heard.

## **III. Feline Subraces**

Feline lineage is divided into two primary ancestral forms, reflecting differences in size, hunting style, and cultural role.

---

### **Chikitu (Small Cats)**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 4’0” – 6’0”

**Bounding Grace.**  
When you take the Dash action, you also gain the benefits of the Dodge action.

**Racial Quirks**

- Quick to retreat, quicker to observe
    
- Prefers indirect solutions to direct confrontation
    
- Thrives in dense terrain and urban environments
    

---

### **Grandi (Big Cats)**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 6’0” – 7’0”

**Hunter’s Cleave.**  
When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points with a melee weapon attack, you may immediately make another melee weapon attack.

**Racial Quirks**

- Confident physical presence
    
- Prefers decisive, overwhelming action
    
- Slow to act, but relentless once committed

## **IV. Regional Variance (Homeland Traits)**

While Al’Ar remains the cultural heart of feline civilization, mainland settlement has produced several distinct regional adaptations.

---

### **Allemance**

Felines in Allemance mix easily into dense cities and rural holdings, often descending from early coastal settlers.

**Alley Cat.**  
You have a climbing speed equal to your walking speed if you can brace against two parallel walls.

**Spiced Brews.**  
You gain proficiency with brewer’s supplies.

---

### **Oria**

Only the most ambitious or arcane-minded felines cross the Mantle to reside in Oria’s lodge houses.

**Cat’s Got Your Tongue.**  
As a reaction to a spell cast within 60 feet that you can hear, you may speak a disrupting word. The caster must make a saving throw using their spellcasting ability (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus). On a failed save, the spell fails. You can use this feature once per long rest.

---

### **Vinyot**

Southern felines adapt to life along trade winds and crowded docks.

**Corked Armor.**  
You can convert armor you are proficient with into corked armor over 24 hours. While wearing it, water up to chest height isn’t difficult terrain, and the armor does not reduce swim speed or impose disadvantage on Athletics checks made to swim.

---

### **Arneria**

Arnerian felines grow up immersed in games of pursuit and evasion that translate naturally into espionage.

**Uncanny Navigator.**  
When climbing to a higher surface, you may use Strength (Athletics) instead of Dexterity (Stealth) to Hide.

**Kolaçan.**  
You have advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks to follow an unaware target you can see.

---

### **Al’Ar**

Al’ari felines are steeped in maritime tradition, piracy, and hidden craftsmanship.

**Deadman’s Drop.**  
You gain proficiency with carpenter’s tools. You may spend 8 hours to build a secret wooden compartment (6-inch cube) using a Dexterity (carpenter’s tools) check. Discovering it requires an Investigation check equal to your result. You have advantage on Investigation checks to find hidden doors or compartments.

**Piratical Boldness.**  
If you are hit while moving (such as by an opportunity attack), you gain advantage on your next attack roll before the end of your next turn.

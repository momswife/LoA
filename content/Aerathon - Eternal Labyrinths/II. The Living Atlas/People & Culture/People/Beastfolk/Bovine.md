## **I. Racial Overview**

Bovine beastfolk are widely regarded as **earthbound custodians**, inheritors of an ancestral bond with soil, root, and growing land. Across Aerathon, bovines are noted for their immense physical presence, deliberate pace, and an instinctive awareness of natural spaces—traits believed to originate from ancient pastoral rites predating the modern delving era.

While their size and mass make them ill-suited to subtle movement or delicate craft, bovines compensate with exceptional resilience and force. Angus lineages dominate front-line engagements, leveraging sheer momentum like living siegeworks, while bison-born warriors wield weapons of a scale and weight impractical for most humanoids. Their strength is not merely martial; it is cultural—rooted in traditions that value endurance, loyalty, and the honoring of spoken intent.

Within delving parties, bovines are frequently described as immovable anchors. They tolerate hardship, insult, and delay with remarkable patience. Once that patience is spent, their response is decisive and rarely reconsidered.

## **II. Core Bovine Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 30 feet.

**March On Hooves.**  
You have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid suffering exhaustion from a forced march.

**Powerful Build.**  
You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift.

**Head Down, Feet Forward.**  
If you move at least 10 feet in a straight line toward a creature as part of your turn, your first melee attack against that creature is made with advantage. If this attack scores a critical hit, you can roll one of the weapon’s damage dice one additional time and add it to the extra damage of the critical hit.

**Root Connected.**  
As an action, you can touch a plant to communicate with it for 1 minute. The plant can recount events in a 30-foot radius (or within its roots) from the past day, including the number and type of creatures, soil disturbances, and a rudimentary visual memory. You can also sense if the plant is struggling to grow, and why.

## **III. Racial Variants**

The following variants represent recognized ancestral expressions within the Bovine lineage. These distinctions are hereditary rather than social and carry equal standing under Ministry charter.

---

### **Angus**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 6’3” – 7’3”  
**General Build.** Broad-framed and dense, often heavier than comparable humanoids.

**Racial Feat — Bullheaded.**  
When you take damage equal to or greater than half your maximum hit points from a single source, you gain advantage on melee weapon attacks for 1 minute, or until you target a different creature.

**Racial Quirks**

- Measured speech, forceful action
    
- Strong protective instincts toward companions
    
- Tendency to suppress emotion until provoked
    

---

### **Bison**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 6’0” – 7’0”  
**General Build.** Extremely heavy for frame, with exceptional upper-body strength.

**Racial Feat — Unreal Grip.**  
You may wield two-handed melee weapons with one hand if holding no other weapons. Versatile weapons wielded one-handed deal their two-handed damage.

**Racial Quirks**

- High tolerance for pain and fatigue
    
- Preference for overwhelming force
    
- Cultural pride in physical dominance
    

---

### **Yak**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Size.** You are Medium, typically between 6’0” and 7’0”.

**General Height/Weight.** Yak bovines are broad and thick-furred, often heavier than they appear, with dense muscle built for altitude and cold.

**Racial Feat — Highland Endurance.**  
You have resistance to cold damage. In addition, you ignore the effects of extreme cold, and you have advantage on Constitution saving throws made to resist exhaustion caused by environmental conditions (such as forced marches, high altitude, or severe weather).

**Racial Quirks**

- Deliberate, patient demeanor; rarely rushed
    
- Strong communal instincts and respect for tradition
    
- Discomfort in lowlands or stagnant air; prefers open or elevated terrain

## **IV. Regional Variance (Homeland Traits)**

Though physiology defines capacity, homeland shapes custom. The following traits reflect cultural conditioning common to bovines raised within these regions.

---

### **Allemance**

Bovines raised in Allemance are shaped by open land, steady labor, and communal responsibility. Reliability and follow-through are valued above charisma or ambition.

**Tenacious Curiosity.**  
Your Intelligence modifier is treated as 3 higher when learning a new language or tool proficiency.

---

### **Oria**

Oric bovines are rare wanderers, drawn by isolated lodges and mountain routes. Generational knowledge emphasizes repair, adaptation, and experimental craft.

**Ingenuity in Artificing.**  
With 24 hours of work, you can attempt to repair a broken magic item. A successful check (based on item type and rarity) restores its magical function. On a failed check, materials are retained, but time required for the next attempt doubles.

---

### **Vinyot**

Urban bovines commonly serve as caretakers, wardens, or enforcers. Their presence alone often establishes order in crowded civic spaces.

**The Bouncer’s Kid.**  
You gain proficiency in Perception and one gaming set of your choice. You have advantage on Perception checks to detect cheating when using any gaming set you’re proficient with.

---

### **Arneria**

Desert-born bovines, particularly bison lineages, are shaped by long travel and territorial memory. Navigation and environmental reading are culturally emphasized skills.

**Reader of Maps and Moss.**  
You gain proficiency with cartographer’s tools. Once per long rest, by examining an underground plant for 1 minute, you can determine the nearest path to the surface and identify the most common non-Humanoid, non-Beast creature type within 300 feet.

---

### **Al’Ar**

Al’ari bovines are smaller and more adaptive, having integrated into predominantly feline societies. Subtlety and quiet movement are common adaptations.

**Light-Touched Farmhand.**  
You gain proficiency in Stealth. When traveling overland, you make Dexterity (Stealth) checks to avoid being tracked with advantage.

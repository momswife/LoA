## **I. Racial Overview**

Tenebrines—most commonly raccoons and possums—are creatures of the in-between hours. They thrive where lamplight fades, where doors are half-closed, and where a city’s true nature reveals itself after dusk. To tenebrines, night is not absence but **clarity**.

Raccoons are renowned for humor sharpened to a blade. They mask brilliance behind jokes, turning tension into laughter and danger into spectacle. Their hands are clever, their memories sharp, and their instincts tuned to opportunity. Possums, by contrast, are quiet until they are not. Heavier of build and slower of speech, they possess a startling capacity for endurance and sudden violence when cornered.

Others often label tenebrines as thieves or tricksters. Tenebrines call themselves **acquirers**, **artists**, and **keepers of secrets**. Where suspicion gathers, they move freely. Where order frays, they adapt. In delving crews, tenebrines are prized for infiltration, recovery, and the ability to make the unbearable survivable—sometimes even funny.

## **II. Core Tenebrine Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 30 feet.

**Darkvision.**  
You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can’t discern color in darkness.

**Pristine-Blooded.**  
You are immune to all diseases.  
Additionally, a creature injected with at least 1 pint of your blood gains advantage on its next Constitution saving throw against disease within 24 hours. Donating this blood inflicts 1 level of exhaustion.

**Urbanite’s Fortune.**  
While in a city of 10,000 or more people, you can reroll one attack roll, ability check, or saving throw. You must use the second result. Once per long rest.

## **III. Tenebrine Subraces**

Tenebrines express two dominant ancestral forms, reflecting differing approaches to survival and confrontation.

---

### **Raccoon**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Charisma score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium

**Think with Your Hands.**  
By handling a key for 1 action, you gain advantage on Dexterity checks to unlock its associated lock for 7 days.  
You automatically succeed on any Dexterity check to open a lock you’ve previously unlocked using this feature.

**Racial Quirks**

- Deflects tension with humor
    
- Collects stories as readily as valuables
    
- Prefers improvisation over planning
    

---

### **Possum**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium

**Below the Belt.**  
When hidden and within 5 feet of a creature no more than one size larger than you, you may use a bonus action to force a Strength saving throw (DC = your Athletics or Acrobatics check). On a failure, the target is knocked prone.  
You deal +1d4 damage with melee weapon attacks against prone targets.

**Racial Quirks**

- Quiet, observant demeanor
    
- Endures hardship without complaint
    
- Strikes decisively when threatened

## **IV. Regional Variance (Nocturnal Practice)**

Tenebrines adapt closely to how a region behaves after dark, developing techniques that favor misdirection, discretion, and survival.

---

### **Allemance**

Alley-raised tenebrines walk the thin line between respectability and crime, mastering impersonation as a defense as much as a trade.

**Dittoed Rumors.**  
You gain proficiency with a forgery kit. When making a Charisma (Deception) check to impersonate someone, you are treated as proficient and double your proficiency bonus.

---

### **Oria**

In Oria’s lodge houses, tenebrines learn to disappear into motionless crowds and falling snow.

**Mosquito in the Crowd.**  
You are treated as proficient and double your proficiency bonus on Dexterity (Stealth) checks made to hide in crowds.  
Once per long rest, you may delay the effects of a dagger or shortsword strike made from hiding until the start of your next turn. Until then, the target remains unaware.

---

### **Vinyot**

Vinyot’s night festivals and crowded streets belong to tenebrines as much as to anyone.

**Peer Misdirection.**  
You can throw your voice up to 60 feet.  
Once per long rest, if you fail a Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) check, you may add a bonus equal to the number of visible onlookers (maximum +5) to the result.

---

### **Arneria**

In Arneria, tenebrines are both celebrated informants and suspected heretics, navigating suspicion with practiced ease.

**Stash Finder.**  
You have advantage on Intelligence (Investigation) checks to locate hidden compartments in masonry.  
You are treated as proficient and double your proficiency bonus when hiding objects on your person using Sleight of Hand.

---

### **Al’Ar**

Along the docks and estates of Al’Ar, tenebrines learn the performance of wealth as a survival skill.

**The Training of Privilege.**  
Choose one skill or tool proficiency you gained through early instruction.  
Once per creature, when convincing someone of your wealth, you may declare a roll result of 15 before rolling. This applies only to first impressions.

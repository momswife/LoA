## **I. Racial Overview**

Ligonines are the exception that proves the taxonomy. Where most Beastfolk group themselves by surface, climate, or visible kinship, ligonines are united by **instinct rather than appearance**. Moles, armadillos, and sloths share little outward resemblance, yet recognize one another immediately as kin.

From the earliest days of the Beast World, ligonines did not seek cities or crowns. Their instinct was to **dig, climb, and vanish**—to inhabit the highest canopies and the deepest strata, leaving the middle ground to louder peoples. Sloths fled skyward into rainforests and coastal canopies. Moles tunneled beneath Oria’s hills. Armadillos struck downward through the heat-hardened earth of the Beylik. For generations, they lived unaware of one another.

When they finally met beneath the world—tunnels intersecting by chance—the recognition was immediate and absolute. No shared language. No shared culture. Only certainty.

That bond remains mythic. Ligonines may argue fiercely, work differently, or vanish for years at a time, but they never question their shared lineage. To be ligonine is to belong to a family that exists **outside convention**.

Among delvers, ligonines are engineers, scouts, climbers, and structural savants—those who understand not just how to move through the world, but how the world is held together.

## **II. Core Ligonine Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 2.

**A Mind Outside the Box.**  
When you make an Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma ability check that does not allow a specific skill proficiency, you are considered proficient in that check.

## **III. Ligonine Subraces**

Ligonine lineage expresses itself through three dominant ancestral forms. These subraces reflect how ligonines interact with their environment rather than any hierarchy among them.

---

### **Mole**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 4’0” – 4’8”

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 25 feet.

**Burrow.**  
You have a burrowing speed of 10 feet while using both hands.

**Darkvision.**  
You can see in dim light within 60 feet as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can’t discern color in darkness.

**Soil Sense.**  
As a bonus action, you gain tremorsense out to 15 feet for 1 minute. While this feature is active, you lose normal vision and darkvision. You regain the use of this feature after a short or long rest.

**Bottlebottoms.**  
You have disadvantage on sight-based Perception checks unless wearing spectacles crafted for you. While wearing them, you are proficient in such checks. Crafting a pair requires 8 hours, 50 gp, and a DC 15 Dexterity (glassblower’s tools) check.

**Tunnel Sense.**  
While underground, you always know the dimensions of the surrounding space and the shortest path to the surface. You also know whether water lies within 60 feet above you.

**Quick Fix.**  
You know the _mending_ cantrip. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability.

**Racial Quirks**

- Deeply focused, easily distracted by theory
    
- Proud of private research
    
- Prefers certainty over speed
    

---

### **Armadillo**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 4’4” – 5’0”

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 35 feet.

**Natural Armor.**  
When not wearing armor, your AC is 14 + your Dexterity modifier. You can use a shield normally.

**Defensive Ball.**  
When you take the Dash, Disengage, or Dodge action, reduce nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage you take by 3 until the start of your next turn.  
When you Dash in a straight line, your speed is doubled for that movement.

**Racial Quirks**

- Pragmatic and blunt
    
- Values preparation over improvisation
    
- Protective of allies and territory
    

---

### **Sloth**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 5’9” – 7’1”

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 25 feet.

**Climb.**  
You have a climbing speed equal to your walking speed.

**A Mean Right Hook.**  
Your claws are natural weapons that deal 1d6 + your Strength modifier slashing damage.

**Long Arms.**  
Your unarmed strikes have a reach of 5 feet greater than normal.

**Slow and Steady.**  
If you didn’t move or use a bonus action or reaction since your last turn, the first Strength or Dexterity ability check or attack you make on your turn is made with advantage.

**Racial Quirks**

- Unhurried, contemplative demeanor
    
- Sudden bursts of surprising strength
    
- Values peace, but ends conflict decisively

## **IV. Regional Variance (Environmental Expression)**

Ligonine culture is shaped less by borders than by **vertical preference**—where one chooses to live in relation to the world above or below.

---

### **Loamlink**

Deep beneath the earth, ligonines build tunnel-cities of research, craft, and quiet obsession. Identity here is defined by work.

**Ligonine Focus.**  
You gain proficiency in one artisan’s tool and one skill of your choice from Arcana, History, Medicine, Nature, or Religion.  
After 24 hours of uninterrupted study, roll two d20s and keep the higher. Within the next 7 days, you may replace one check using that tool or skill with the saved roll. This feature can be used once per study period.

---

### **Surface**

Surface-raised ligonines are curious, fearless, and infamous for ignoring personal boundaries in pursuit of connection and understanding.

**Stonemovers and Surveyors.**  
You can cast _floating disk_ once per long rest. The disk is 10 feet wide, holds up to 1,000 pounds, and can cross gaps up to 20 feet wide (but cannot change elevation except by slope).  
Additionally, while inside a building, you can sense the presence of any basement, tunnel, or substructure within 300 feet below you.

---

### **Canopy**

High above the ground, ligonines dwell among leaves, bridges, and branches, favoring serenity and long sightlines.

**Dweller in Leaves.**  
You can see clearly up to 1 mile away, discerning details as if viewing from 100 feet. You gain proficiency in Acrobatics.  
If you fall at least 10 feet and there is a handhold within reach, you may make a Dexterity (Acrobatics) check (DC 12 + 1 per 10 feet fallen). On a success, you swing and convert the fall into horizontal movement instead of taking fall damage.

## **I. Racial Overview**

Celerines—most commonly rabbits, squirrels, and hares—are defined by **speed of thought as much as speed of body**. Alert, expressive, and deeply attuned to novelty, they flourish in dense social environments where trends, magic, and reputation move faster than armies.

Celerine cultures prize **aesthetic expression, adaptability, and visibility**. Whether through fashion, spellcraft, or performance, many celerines view self-presentation as a form of power. Even those uninterested in spectacle tend to move through the world with remarkable awareness, reacting to danger and opportunity before others realize either exists.

Among delvers, celerines are prized as scouts, arcanists, performers, and crowd-shapers. To a celerine, delving is not merely survival or profit—it is **a statement**, and the Labyrinths are the grandest stage imaginable.

## **II. Core Celerine Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 35 feet.

**Skittish Instinct.**  
While conscious, you can’t be surprised.

**Arcane Alacrity.**  
You have advantage on Dexterity saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

---

## **III. Celerine Subraces**

The Celerine lineage expresses several distinct specializations, shaped by instinct and lifestyle rather than hierarchy.

---

### **Rabbit**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Charisma score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 5’6” – 6’6”

**Extraordinary Panache.**  
You know the _prestidigitation_ cantrip. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for it.

**Racial Quirks**

- Naturally expressive body language
    
- Drawn to performance, fashion, or social leadership
    
- Discomfort with anonymity
    

---

### **Squirrel**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 5’5” – 6’5”

**Vaulting Poise.**  
You can move up vertical surfaces equal to your speed without falling during the movement.

**Racial Quirks**

- Habitual planners and collectors
    
- Strong spatial memory
    
- Difficulty letting go of unfinished projects
    

---

### **Hare**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 5’7” – 6’8”

**Longstride Escape.**  
When you take the Dash action, opportunity attacks against you are made with disadvantage until the start of your next turn.

**Racial Quirks**

- Hyper-alert to environmental changes
    
- Tendency toward anxious foresight
    
- Sleeps lightly, if at all

## **IV. Regional Variance (Homeland Traits)**

Though instinctively mobile, celerines develop strong regional identities tied to **artistic expression and arcane display**.

---

### **Allemance**

Celerines of Allemance are fashion-forward creatives who treat magic as an artistic medium.

**Burst of Color.**  
As an action, emit a dazzling strobe of light for 1 minute. Creatures within 60 feet that can see you must succeed on a DC 13 Wisdom saving throw or have disadvantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks against other creatures until the start of their next turn. Creatures immune to charm automatically succeed. Once per long rest.

---

### **Oria**

Oric celerines embrace shadow and contrast, often favoring dramatic silhouettes against icy terrain.

**Burst of Darkness.**  
As an action, summon black tendrils in a 5-foot radius around you for 1 minute. The area becomes difficult terrain. Creatures in it must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 bludgeoning damage and become restrained. They may escape with a DC 13 Strength or Dexterity check. Ends early if you move or dismiss it. Once per long rest.

---

### **Vinyot**

Urban celerines master subtlety amid crowds, markets, and narrow streets.

**Burst of Silence.**  
As an action, generate a 5-foot-radius sphere of magical silence centered on you for 1 minute. All sound and thunder damage are blocked. Creatures inside are deafened. Once per long rest.

---

### **Arneria**

In Arneria’s performative arenas and bazaars, celerines are celebrated for dramatic entrances and kinetic displays.

**Burst of Speed.**  
As an action, you and allies within 5 feet gain +10 feet of movement and increased long-jump distance for 1 minute. Once per turn, you may choose which allies benefit. Once per long rest.

---

### **Al’Ar**

Island celerines channel motion and color into nautical expression.

**Burst of Air.**  
As an action, conjure a transparent air bubble around yourself for 1 minute. It floats on water, provides breathable air, and can move at half your speed. You may submerge or surface with a bonus action. The bubble ends if you deal or take damage. Once per long rest.

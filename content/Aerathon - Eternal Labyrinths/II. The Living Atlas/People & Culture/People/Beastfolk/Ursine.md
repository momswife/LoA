## **I. Racial Overview**

Ursines—commonly known as bears—carry history as both burden and weapon. Their perfect recall is not metaphorical but absolute: every word spoken to them, every slight delivered, every kindness offered is remembered with crystalline clarity. This eidetic memory is widely believed to be a divine gift, though whether it is blessing or curse depends on who is asked.

Ursines are renowned competitors. They challenge one another constantly—through wrestling, debate, storytelling, or feats of endurance—not out of malice, but affection. To an ursine, rivalry is intimacy. Winning proves worth; losing proves where to grow.

Despite their size and boisterous reputation, ursines are deeply reflective. Family, lineage, and legacy matter profoundly to them. Their memories form living archives, and many ursines act as historians, judges, or keepers of oral law. Insults are not forgotten. Promises are never misplaced.

Among delvers, ursines are terrifyingly reliable. They remember enemy tactics after seeing them once. They recall labyrinth layouts years later. And when an ursine swears an oath, the Ministry recommends believing them.

## **II. Core Ursine Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 30 feet.

**Eidetic Memory.**  
You can accurately recall anything you have seen or heard. Additionally, when you make a Strength or Dexterity saving throw against a creature’s nonmagical ability, you have advantage on future saving throws against that same ability for the next 24 hours.

**Mnemonic Mind.**  
When a creature you can see within 60 feet casts a spell that is on your class’s spell list, you can use your reaction to commit it to memory. Within 7 days, you may study the spell in an arcane library. After 8 hours of research per spell level and a successful Arcana check (DC 10 + spell level), you may add it to your spells known or copy it into a spellbook, as if copying from a scroll. You may only retain one spell this way at a time.

## **III. Ursine Subraces**

Ursine lineage expresses itself through three dominant ancestral forms, reflecting **environmental adaptation and temperament** rather than hierarchy.

---

### **Brown Bear**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 6’6” – 7’8”

**Toughen Up.**  
When you roll initiative, you may immediately spend a number of Hit Dice up to half your level (minimum 1) to gain that many temporary hit points. These last for 10 minutes.

**Racial Quirks**

- Loud laughter and louder opinions
    
- Enjoys physical contests as bonding
    
- Protective of friends and territory
    

---

### **Polar Bear**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Wisdom score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 6’8” – 8’0”

**Cold Memory.**  
You are resistant to cold damage. When you succeed on a Wisdom saving throw, you gain advantage on your next attack roll or ability check before the end of your next turn.

**Racial Quirks**

- Stoic, deliberate demeanor
    
- Long silences used for thought, not avoidance
    
- Values patience as strength
    

---

### **Black Bear**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

**Size.** Medium  
**Typical Height.** 6’0” – 7’2”

**Quiet Recall.**  
You gain proficiency in History. When you make an Intelligence (History or Arcana) check related to events you personally witnessed, treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10.

**Racial Quirks**

- Observant and understated
    
- Keeps careful mental ledgers
    
- Often underestimated until too late

## **IV. Regional Variance (Ancestral Expression)**

Though united by memory, ursines are shaped strongly by where that memory is forged.

---

### **Allemance**

**Spirited Discourse.**  
You may use Intelligence instead of Charisma for Performance and Persuasion checks. Once per long rest, when a creature within 30 feet fails a saving throw against being charmed, you may grant them a reroll as a reaction.

---

### **Oria**

**Obsidian Bauble.**  
You gain proficiency with smith’s tools. With 8 hours of work and 30 gp, you can forge an obsidian bauble that stores a 1st-level spell cast into it. Any creature holding the bauble can release the spell once, destroying the bauble.

---

### **Vinyot**

**Rules Lawyer.**  
Choose two 1st-level spells. They count as being on all class spell lists for you.

---

### **Arneria**

**Born of the Bey’s Head.**  
You gain proficiency with the war pick. If you hit with a war pick on the first round of combat, you deal an additional 2d6 piercing damage.

---

### **Al’Ar**

**Instructive Muscle.**  
You gain proficiency in Athletics. When you succeed on a Strength (Athletics) check, creatures that share a language with you and saw the attempt are treated as proficient in Athletics when attempting the same task.

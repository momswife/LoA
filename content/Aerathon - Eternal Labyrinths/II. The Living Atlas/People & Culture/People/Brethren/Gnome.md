## **Overview**

Gnomes in Aerathon are invention incarnate. They are the sparks that jump the gap between chaos and order, the moment when inspiration sings just loud enough to drown out hesitation. Whether nestled in the copper alleys of Vinyot, buried deep in Allemagnian laboratories, or wandering the Labyrinths with backpacks full of whistles and wires, gnomes are driven by boundless curiosity.

Unlike the beastkin, gnomes trace their origins to the material plane before the Convergence. How they arrived is unclear, but they quickly adapted to the shifting magical tides of Aerathon. Some suspect their survival is thanks to a mysterious trait called _mana resilience_ — an uncanny knack for adapting to wild magic, mutating enchantments, and volatile aether.

To the gnomes, delving is a natural extension of their tinkering. Each labyrinth is a problem to solve, a riddle to dissect. Their devices may explode half the time, but when they work, they turn impossible odds into improbable triumphs. Gnomish delvers are often support specialists: bomb-makers, shield-casters, teleportation technicians, and structural engineers.

Their endless enthusiasm can wear on more stoic allies. But few crews regret having a gnome around—especially when a bridge needs building, a rune trap needs disarming, or a mundane rock _might_ be a mana-seeded metamorphic node.

## **Heritage Traits**

### **Allemance**

Allemagnian gnomes are the backbone of the guild labs. Renowned for their exacting standards and breakneck theoretical debates, they pioneered many of Aerathon's modern innovations in elemental flux regulation and anti-wild magic field theory.

**Paradox-Solver.** You gain proficiency in Arcana. When you fail to identify a spell or magic item, you can immediately retry the check with disadvantage.

---

### **Oria**

Gnomes are rare in the glacial north, but those who thrive in Oria are as precise as clockwork and twice as patient. They serve as stewards of arcane observatories and keepers of long-form magical experiments.

**Frostfield Modulator.** You are resistant to cold damage. Additionally, when you cast a spell that deals cold damage, you can reroll one damage die. You must use the new result.

---

### **Vinyot**

Among the wheeling and dealing of Vinyot, gnomes have found their niche as inventors of personal gadgets, one-use enchantments, and disposable defenses. They often serve as consultants to delving guilds or enchant brokers.

**Pocket Wonders.** You can cast _absorb elements_ once per long rest without expending a spell slot. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for this spell.

---

### **Arneria**

Arnerian gnomes are known for their bombastic personalities and wandering souls. They travel from outpost to outpost like wind-up philosophers, sharing inventions and ideas freely and loudly. Many have taken up roles as delver-trainers and spell-dissectors.

**Kaboomsmith.** You gain proficiency with alchemist’s supplies. When you throw an alchemical item (like alchemist’s fire or acid), the target has disadvantage on the saving throw if they are within 5 feet of the explosion point.

---

### **Al'Ar**

Al'ari gnomes are salt-touched inventors, crafting wind-driven mechanisms and tide-powered reactors. Many of them serve aboard labyrinth-cruising vessels or as coastal tinker-magi.

**Saltforged Ingenuity.** You can breathe underwater for up to 10 minutes at a time. Additionally, you ignore difficult terrain caused by sand, silt, or shallow water.

> _"We're not the biggest, the strongest, or the fastest. We're just the ones who kept going."_ — Lysen of the Crossroads, human delver

## **Overview**

Humans are the great adaptors of Aerathon—clever, stubborn, and relentlessly driven. While beastkin often draw from divine ancestry or the uplifts of Pirhoua, humans exist apart from such mythic certainties. Their origins remain shrouded in pre-Convergence mystery. They are not marked by fur or fang, but by flame-hearted will.

They endure not through supernatural blessing, but through tenacity and invention. When the Labyrinths first tore into the world, humans were among the first to descend, not out of divine calling but because they could not bear to leave the unknown unclaimed.

Their presence spans every homeland, and no single culture can claim dominion over the human spirit. Some rise to glory under banners of old empires, others dig into the dust and build republics from scratch. They do not adapt by becoming something else—they adapt by mastering the rules and rewriting them.

Within Aerathon’s delver guilds, human delvers are often renowned for unpredictable tactics and bold gambits. They form the backbone of many crews, not because they are the strongest, but because they will not break. Their minds grasp both strategy and story, and their hands carry tools passed down from forgotten ages.

In a world where gods bleed, beasts speak, and Labyrinths rewrite reality, humans are the anomaly: a species with no singular purpose but every possible one.

## **Homeland Traits**

### **Allemance**

In Allemance, human nobles hone themselves as both strategists and performers. Whether debating law or charming crowds at masked balls, their words carry weight.

**Courtly Cunning.** You are proficient in Insight or Persuasion (your choice). You also gain one additional language.

---

### **Oria**

Among the frozen heights of Oria, human families trace their legacies through carved stone, bloodied blades, and burned scrolls. Every triumph is remembered, and every mistake is recorded.

**Steel in the Spine.** You are proficient in Constitution saving throws.

---

### **Vinyot**

Human merchants in Vinyot barter not just with gold, but with gossip, favors, and cleverly worded contracts. Many find success as auctioneers, entertainers, and illusion-crafters.

**Goldtongue.** You are proficient in Deception or Persuasion. Once per long rest, you may treat a roll of 9 or lower on such a check as a 10.

---

### **Arneria**

The humans of Arneria thrive in cities of sandstone and starlight. They are grand storytellers and visionary tacticians, shaping the world through their legends.

**Banner-Bearer.** You can inspire others. As a bonus action, you can grant an ally within 30 feet a bonus to their next attack roll or saving throw equal to your proficiency bonus. You can use this a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus per long rest.

---

### **Al’Ar**

On the Feline Isles, human dockhands and scholars work alongside cats and sea-beasts alike. They’ve earned a reputation for slick dealings, silver tongues, and split-second improvisation.

**Dockside Guile.** You are proficient with vehicles (water) and have advantage on Dexterity (Sleight of Hand) checks made during fast-paced or crowded situations.

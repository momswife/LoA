## **Overview**

_Jackals_ are the rarest of all willful species in the Beast World, long considered myth even by those who believed in everything else. Appearing in fairy tales, whispered rumors, and ancient murals, they seemed more story than truth—until they returned.

Towering and enigmatic, jackals are revered as _storybook beasts_—keepers of hidden cities, ancient knowledge, and the quiet patience of time itself. Though real, they remain elusive, dwelling in sanctums shielded by illusion and magic. They offer wisdom when it is most needed, then vanish like mist.

Jackals are not bound by the Beast World’s timelines. Their lifespans stretch deep into the ages, and their connection to the Arcana is unmatched. When they speak, courts listen. When they walk among mortals, stories change.

## **Jackal Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Fey.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Dexterity, Intelligence, and Wisdom scores each increase by 2.

**Size.** You are Medium. Most jackals stand between 6’8” and 7’8”.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 30 feet.

**Ageless.** You are immune to any magical effect that would cause you to age unnaturally.

**Obscuring Dream.** You can cast the _dream_ spell once per long rest without expending a spell slot. When using this trait, you may instead alter a target's memory of an encounter from the last 7 days, causing them to forget your presence (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + Intelligence modifier). If the target has an Intelligence score lower than yours, they automatically fail. You may cast _dream_ using spell slots as normal.

**Life’s Passion.** You gain proficiency with one artisan’s tool and one skill from the following: Arcana, Investigation, Medicine, Nature, Religion or Survival. Your proficiency bonus is doubled for these checks. You also gain proficiency with one martial melee weapon (without the heavy property); for you, it counts as finesse and thrown (range 20/60).

**Birthmates of the Arcana.** You know the _eldritch blast_ cantrip. Choose two 1st-level ritual spells from the wizard list. You can cast each once per long rest without material components. You can also cast them with spell slots as normal. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for these spells.

## **Homeland Traits**

_Jackals do not conform to the boundaries of traditional homelands. Their cities, hidden and wondrous, lie beneath oceans, within clouds, or folded between forgotten moments in time. Their influence is everywhere, yet their presence is rare._

**Wanderers of the Shrouded Roads.** You can choose any homeland trait from another species as if you had grown up there—reflecting your time spent aiding the other homelands during the Jackal Exodus.

**City of the Unseen.** Jackals raised in hidden cities know secrets never meant for surface ears. You have advantage on Intelligence (History) checks related to ancient ruins, lost civilizations, or Seelie worship. Additionally, you can speak, read, and write one exotic language (such as Sylvan, Deep Speech, or Primordial).

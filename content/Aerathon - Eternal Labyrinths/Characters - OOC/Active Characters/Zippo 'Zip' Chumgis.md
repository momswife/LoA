## **Backstory**

### **A Merchant of Magic & Mayhem**

Zippo Chumgis was never meant to be a Delver.

He was a shopkeeper, an arcane merchant, a purveyor of magical oddities—his talents lay in discovering, identifying, and selling rare artifacts to those who needed (or could afford) them.

His shop, _The Smoldering Scroll_, sat in the heart of Aerathon, catering to mages, adventurers, and scholars alike.

- He never just sold magic—he understood it, he played with it, he let it evolve.
- To Zippo, fire wasn’t just a spell—it was a philosophy, a force of change, a thing that must always move forward.
- He experimented, tinkered, and sometimes… got carried away.

MEMORABLE SHOP INCIDENTS:

- Sold a "harmless trick candle" to a noble that _spontaneously ignited_ when exposed to lies. _(Resulted in a house fire and two duels.)_
- Accidentally created a wand that shot out "completely safe, mostly controlled" fireballs when someone sneezed. _(Banned from four taverns.)_
- Built a _self-replicating lantern_ that copied itself every midnight. _(The city guard was _very_ upset about the lantern infestation.)_

He was a mage, a scholar, a reckless creator of chaos—but he was never a warrior.

Until he lost his friend.

And until his shop—his entire life—was erased.

### **A Friend Lost, A Store Consumed**

Zippo had adventured before. He had a party once, people he trusted, people who fought while he burned things in the background.

They had survived tombs, ruins, and arcane disasters, but one day, they pushed too far.

- An expedition into a forgotten ruin.
- A trap they didn’t see.
- A friend — Calvus — swallowed by the void, leaving behind only his cloak.

That cloak became Zippo’s most prized possession, a reminder of the one thing he couldn’t burn away, the one thing he had left.

But when the Nameless Labyrinth erupted into existence, consuming Velgrove Square in a single, world-breaking instant…

- His shop was gone.
- His belongings? Gone.
- And the cloak? Gone.

Everything he had built, everything he thought was his foundation—erased.

And now, he has nothing left but fire and fury.

### **What He Seeks in the Labyrinth**

Most enter the Labyrinths seeking fortune, power, or knowledge.

Zippo enters for something more personal.

- He doesn’t care about treasure.
- He doesn’t care about relics.
- He doesn’t care about sponsors, guilds, or grand discoveries.

He wants that cloak.

Because if the Labyrinth took it, then it means something remains of Calvus inside.

And if something remains of Calvus inside…

Then maybe—just _maybe_—he isn’t truly gone.

Or maybe Zippo just can’t let go.

Either way, he will burn through whatever stands in his way to get it back.

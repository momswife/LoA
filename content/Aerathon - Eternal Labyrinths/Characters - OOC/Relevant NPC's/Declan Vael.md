## **Personality**

Declan Vael is everything the Grand Scrying Network needs him to be: charming, relentless, and always in control. He is a master of perception, shaping public narratives, controlling the crowd, and ensuring that every Delve is a spectacle worth watching.

But beneath the charisma?

There’s a man who knows more than he should. Someone who has seen things on the Livecasts that don’t make sense. Someone who keeps secrets because revealing them would mean his own downfall.

### **Motivations**

**Legacy Over Truth.** Declan doesn’t care _who wins_ or _who survives._ He cares that their stories are remembered.  
**Control the Narrative.** Information is a weapon. If people believe something, it doesn’t matter whether it’s true.  
**Stay Alive.** Declan knows things he shouldn’t. And people don’t like that.

### **Fears**

**Losing Control.** If he can’t shape the story, then someone else will—and that’s dangerous.  
**Being Found Out.** He’s seen something in the Labyrinths—and the wrong people know that.  
**The Labyrinths Are Changing.** And he doesn’t know why.

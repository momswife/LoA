## **Basic Information**

- **Full Name:** Vaelka Sorn
- **Nickname:** _"The Grin"_
- **Race:** Half-Orc
- **Class:** Fighter 4 (Dueling) / Rogue 1 (Dirty Tricks)
- **Alignment:** Neutral Evil (_Not cruel. Just practical._)
- **Occupation:** Crimson Reaver Enforcer, Mercenary Duelist, Problem-Solver
- **Distinctive Look:** A permanent, sharp-toothed grin, countless scars, and a casual, almost lazy confidence that makes even seasoned warriors uneasy.

## **Appearance & Mannerisms**

- **Hair:** Dark brown, nearly black, messy and shoulder-length, often tied back just enough to keep out of his eyes.
- **Eyes:** Amber-gold, catlike slits that seem to glint in the dark.
- **Build:** Lean, muscular, built for speed—more duelist than brute.
- **Voice:** Low, smooth, and teasing, always with a hint of a smirk. He speaks as if everything is a private joke.
- **Presence:** Casual, predatory, always grinning. Moves like a wolf circling its prey, never fully at rest.

**Signature Habit:**

- Grins even when bleeding. Especially when bleeding.
- Taps the hilt of his rapier when he’s about to strike.
- Cracks his neck before every duel, slow and deliberate.

**How Others Describe Him:**

- "He smiles when he’s winning. He smiles when he’s losing. He smiles when he’s watching you die."
- "There are two kinds of fools: the ones who bet against him and the ones who fight him."
- "If he draws his blade, someone’s not leaving that fight alive."

---

## **📜 Personality**

Vaelka Sorn is violence made elegant. He doesn’t fight for honor, revenge, or even money—he fights because it’s what he does best. The Crimson Reavers give him excuses to spill blood and a paycheck to go with it.

He is not needlessly cruel—but he has no patience for weakness. If you step onto the battlefield unprepared, hesitant, or afraid, he sees it as your own damn fault when he guts you.

### **Motivations**

**The Thrill of the Fight.** He lives for the moment steel meets steel.  
**Proving He’s the Best.** Every duel is another body in the ground, another notch in his legend.  
**No Attachments, No Regrets.** He moves light, kills fast, and doesn’t look back.

### **Fears**

**Losing Control.** He’s always been the one with the upper hand. The idea of facing something he can’t predict, can’t fight, or can’t stop? That unsettles him.  
**Becoming Soft.** The day he hesitates, falters, or doubts himself is the day he dies.  
**The Labyrinth.** He doesn’t say it out loud, but this one feels different. And he doesn’t know why.

## **📜 Skills & Abilities**

**Deadly Duelist** _(Dueling Fighting Style – Precision Over Power)_

- Prefers quick, efficient kills over brute strength.
- Wields a serrated rapier nicknamed "Grinder."
- Rarely blocks—he dodges, parries, and counters instead.

**Unshakable Confidence** _(Fighter’s Indomitable Feature – He Won’t Break)_

- Fear is a weakness. He doesn’t feel it.
- Even when wounded, he laughs.
- Even when outnumbered, he taunts.

**Knows When to Cut and Run** _(Rogue’s Cunning Action – Fast Getaway)_

- If a fight turns truly unwinnable, he’s already gone.
- He sees no honor in dying for a lost cause.

**Killer’s Instinct** _(Insight & Perception Expertise – Spots Weaknesses Instantly)_

- He can read people at a glance. Weak knees? Favoring one side? _He sees it._
- If someone’s hesitating or afraid, he presses the advantage immediately.

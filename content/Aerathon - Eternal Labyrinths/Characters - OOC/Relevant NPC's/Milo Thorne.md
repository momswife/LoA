## **Basic Information**

- **Full Name:** Milonia "Milo" Thorne
- **Race:** Half-Elf
- **Class:** Bard 4 (College of Eloquence) / Rogue 1 (Reporter, Investigator, and Manipulator)
- **Alignment:** Chaotic Neutral (_Truth is flexible. Drama is priceless._)
- **Occupation:** Senior Field Correspondent & Interviewer for the Grand Scrying Network (GSN)
- **Distinctive Look:** Always dressed in **sleek, high-collared coats**, tailored perfectly to her frame. Prefers **darker colors**—deep purples, navy blues, and silvers, making her look like she just walked off a noble’s stage.
- **Notable Traits:** Sharp, silver tongue. Quick wit. Knows more than she lets on.

## **Appearance & Mannerisms**

- **Hair:** Shoulder-length dark auburn, always perfectly styled—never a strand out of place.
- **Eyes:** Piercing blue-gray, with a gaze that unsettles even seasoned warriors.
- **Build:** Slender but athletic—not a fighter, but fast enough to disappear when needed.
- **Voice:** Warm, smooth, and deliberate—the kind of voice that makes people lean in closer without realizing it.
- **Presence:** Commanding without trying. She walks into a room, and people adjust their posture.

**Signature Habit:**

- Pauses just long enough before a question to make someone uncomfortable.
- Tilts her head slightly when she catches a lie—but never calls it out immediately.
- Smiles like she already knows how you’ll answer.

**How Others Describe Her:**

- "Ruthless, but with a velvet touch."
- "She could make a saint confess to crimes he didn’t commit."
- "I swear she’s the devil with a notepad."

## **Personality**

Milo Thorne is not just an interviewer—she’s a predator, a sculptor of reputations. She shapes public perception like a master artist, twisting narratives, reputations, and expectations to fit her needs.

She’s not in this for the truth. She’s in it for the power that comes from controlling what the world believes.

### **Motivations**

**The Bigger Picture.** Stories aren’t just stories—they shape real power.  
**Find the Hidden Truths.** Not to expose them, but to own them.  
**Control the Narrative.** A lie told well becomes history.

### **Fears**

**Losing Control.** If she doesn’t control the story, someone else does.  
**Being Exposed.** There are things she knows—things she shouldn’t.  
**The Labyrinth’s Truth.** Something about this one is _different._ And she _doesn’t like that._

## **Skills & Abilities**

**Master Interviewer** _(Bard Expertise in Persuasion & Insight)_

- People want to talk to her. They just don’t realize how much they’ve said.
- Knows when to press, when to pause, and when to let silence do the work.

**The Perfect Lie** _(College of Eloquence – Silver Tongue Feature)_

- Even when caught lying, people still want to believe her.
- If she needs something to be true, she makes it true.

**Knows How to Slip Away** _(Rogue Multiclass – Cunning Action & Stealth Expertise)_

- If an interview turns hostile? She’s already gone.
- Can move through crowds like a ghost, slipping away before anyone reacts.

**The Power of Reputation** _(Livecasting & Public Influence)_

- She can make or break a Delver’s reputation.
- If she frames someone as a hero or a villain, the world believes it.
- If the right sponsors like you, you live forever. If they don’t? You disappear.

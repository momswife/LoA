## **Basic Information**

- **Full Name:** Captain Lirian Voss
- **Race:** Human
- **Class:** Fighter 5 (Battle Master)
- **Alignment:** Lawful Neutral (_Rules first. Morality second._)
- **Occupation:** Officer of the **Halvaskian Delving Authority (HDA)**, Lead Investigator of Labyrinth-Related Incidents
- **Distinctive Look:** No nonsense. No wasted movements. Always in pristine HDA officer’s uniform, black with silver trim, adorned with a captain’s insignia and the steel chain of her rank.

## **Appearance & Mannerisms**

- **Hair:** Ash-blonde, always tied in a tight bun. Never out of place. Never loose.
- **Eyes:** Icy gray, sharp and discerning. No warmth, no excess emotion—only assessment.
- **Build:** Lean, athletic, military-trained. Built for efficiency, not brute force.
- **Voice:** Low, clipped, deliberate. The kind of voice that commands attention in a room full of armed men.
- **Presence:** Heavy, unwavering. She is the kind of person who does not blink when others falter.

**Signature Habit:**

- Crosses her arms when listening. If she unfolds them, you have her full attention—good or bad.
- Always smells faintly of steel and parchment. A soldier buried in bureaucracy.
- Keeps her boots polished. Not for vanity—for discipline.

**How Others Describe Her:**

- **"She doesn’t bluff. If she says she’ll break your kneecaps, she means it."**
- **"You don’t bribe Lirian. You survive her."**
- **"I’d rather fight a Labyrinth Horror than sit across from her in an interrogation."**

## **Personality**

Captain Lirian Voss believes in order. In structure. In survival through discipline. She is not cruel, but she is uncompromising. She doesn’t bend to sentiment, doesn’t entertain nonsense, and has no patience for Delvers who think they’re above the rules.

If the Halvaskian Delving Authority is the shield protecting the city, Lirian is the steel inside it—the unyielding force that ensures the laws are followed.

### **Motivations**

**The Rules Exist for a Reason.** The HDA isn’t corrupt—it’s the only thing keeping this city from tearing itself apart.  
**Delvers Are a Necessary Evil.** They’re reckless, arrogant, and always cost the city more than they bring in. But they’re also the only ones who can fight what’s inside the Labyrinths.  
**The Nameless Labyrinth Must Be Contained.** If it is anything like the others, it will break people. And she will not let it break Halvask.

### **Fears**

**The Labyrinths Are Changing.** And she doesn’t know why.  
**The Bureaucracy Will Fail.** Rules keep people safe—but if the people above her start ignoring them, what happens then?  
**A Mistake That Gets People Killed.** She doesn’t regret being harsh. She only regrets the times she wasn’t harsh enough.

- She does not swing wildly. Every strike is measured, intentional.

**Interrogator’s Stare** _(Insight Expertise – Sees Through Lies Instantly)_

- She knows when someone is lying. Even when they don’t realize it themselves.

## Overview

**Garrux Flintmaw** is an S-Class delver and tactical juggernaut known for his mastery of the **Echo Knight** martial tradition—a rare fighting style that manipulates time-anchored duplicates of oneself from alternate realities. A dwarven veteran of the Blackspire Wars and former military commander of the **Gravethane Phalanx**, Garrux transitioned into delving only after a catastrophic chronoshift during his final siege command left him _unstuck_ from standard timeflow.

Since then, he has become a fixture in high-tier labyrinth combat operations, often brought in to secure critical objectives where precise coordination, brute strength, and temporal discipline are required. Garrux is known for refusing to command live parties—instead, he enters alongside his own echoes, fighting entire conflicts solo in fractured formation.

## Notable Accomplishments

**The Chronogolem Collapse**  
In the **Shifting Vaults of Halvask**, Garrux faced the **Chronogolem of Floor 19**, a construct capable of skipping six seconds backward in combat. Garrux deployed five simultaneous echoes across staggered timeframes, trapping the construct in a fixed loop. The golem’s core shattered under repeated strikes delivered across a single perfect second.

**Last Stand of the Gravethane Rift**  
Before becoming a delver, Garrux held the Riftwall with only 18 dwarves and two war mages against a planar incursion. After a chronoshift event, Garrux and a temporal echo defended for an additional 3 hours while the others escaped. It is unknown if the second Garrux was a true echo or a full parallel.

**The Silent Floor Campaign**  
Deployed by the Free Company into a mute labyrinth where no sound could propagate, Garrux fought alongside three echoes in total silence for over four hours. The entire delve was reconstructed post-event from floor glyphs and echo-prints. No other delver survived that run.

## Equipment and Artifacts

**Hammer of the Moment-That-Broke**  
A reforged warhammer made from the remains of a shattered time-node. When used with an echo, it can strike both a target and its _previous position_ simultaneously.

**Shield of the Second Garrux**  
An indestructible steel bulwark, recovered after the Riftwall incident. Believed to be wielded by the Garrux that did not return.

**Chronoseal Band**  
A dwarven-made ring that stabilizes echo feedback. Without it, Garrux would likely suffer from cognitive fracture during prolonged combat.

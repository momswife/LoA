## Overview

**Seraphine Aelthorn** is an S-Class delver and arcane theologian, renowned for merging divine channeling with pure arcane calculation. A **Cleric of the Arcana Domain**, Seraphine is a rare synthesis of magical savant and doctrinal enforcer—beloved by the scholarly enclaves of Aerathon and feared by labyrinthspawn who rely on illusion, entropy, or divine corruption.

She is often referred to as the **“Living Equation”**, due to her unparalleled precision in spellcraft and her creation of the **Divine Echo Matrix**, a now-standardized system for identifying soul retention following labyrinth deaths. Many claim she sees the world as formula—predictable, refineable, and subject to divine pattern.

## Notable Accomplishments

**Creation of the Divine Echo Matrix**  
Aelthorn pioneered the codified system by which residual divine essence—also called “soulprint static”—can be mapped and preserved following delver death. This process enables resurrection for up to 7 minutes after soul severance, provided proper ritual access.

**Unlocker of the Forgotten Divine Gates**  
She has unlocked three **Divine Gates** buried in separate labyrinths, each tied to an unknown or dead god. Her translations of the Gate-Tongue are studied in modern guilds and arcane colleges alike. One gate, known as the **Gate of the Humming Silence**, has yet to be entered by anyone else.

**The Glyph War of Ardentreach**  
During a delve war between competing guilds, Seraphine encoded an entire fortress's magical infrastructure into a single floating glyphstone. With its removal, the opposing guild’s defenses collapsed in real time. No casualties were recorded on her side.

## Equipment and Artifacts

**Codex of Fractal Faith**  
A living spellbook encoded in recursive divine logic. It unfolds new pages only in response to acts of piety. Some of its pages have been observed rewriting themselves after particularly dangerous delves.

**Mantle of the First Sigil**  
A silver-threaded robe enchanted to nullify wild magic and ambient chaos. When worn, grants immunity to the effects of magical terrain corruption.

**Glass Scepter of Tetragramma**  
A divine focus capable of rewriting the elemental type of incoming spells mid-flight. The process requires exacting calculation and visual focus.

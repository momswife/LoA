## **Overview**

**Fondo Fizzlefield**, a gnome of volcanic intellect and volcanic hair (literally—it’s been mildly singed since his fifth delve), is one of Aerathon’s most **unpredictable** and **brilliant** S-Class delvers. Formerly the Head of Arcano-Mechanics at **Velgrave University**, Fondo was once content lecturing wide-eyed students on dimensional theory, interplanar kinetic models, and the spiritual resonance of enchanted cookware.

That changed when he stumbled upon a **ruined relic chamber** containing the shattered husk of a legendary **shield golem**—**Aegis-77**. Where others saw scrap, Fondo saw symmetry. He didn’t just repair the construct—he **reprogrammed, refitted, and reinvented** it using techniques even Velgrave calls "unreproducible and slightly illegal."

Now, Fondo delves with **research scrolls in one hand and a titanic golem in the other**, treating each expedition like a living thesis defense—with explosions.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Reconstruction of Aegis-77**  
Recovered from a collapsed pre-Runic vault, Aegis-77 was reassembled from shattered shield plating, nullstone hearts, and one—yes, one—converted soup cauldron. It has since blocked archdevil strikes, meteor swarms, and a thrown piano.

**The Twelve Floor Labyrinth Thesis**  
Fondo charted, analyzed, and cross-referenced the entire structure of a drifting pocket labyrinth while delving through it. He submitted the annotated map as his **S-Class certification exam**. He passed. The exam was discontinued afterward.

**The Symmetry Singularity at Breakreach**  
During a delve collapse, Fondo redirected the collapse into itself using folded spatial tensors encoded into a chant sung by Aegis-77. It worked. Fondo celebrated by shouting, “SCIENCE!” and falling asleep atop a live leyline node.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**Aegis-77 (“The Thesis Golem”)**  
A four-ton, rune-inscribed construct bonded to Fondo’s arcane signature. Features autonomous shield wall generation, impact reversal, and planar buffering. Refers to Fondo as “Tiny Professor.” Unknown if affection or sarcasm.

**Scarf of Eternal Annotation**  
A self-scrawling scarf woven with living ink. Records field notes, nearby arcane fluctuations, and Fondo’s mutterings (including several recipes and at least one love poem to fire).

**Wand of Retroactive Accuracy**  
Grants advantage on any spell Fondo _intended_ to cast correctly. Uses predictive logic and minor time-twisting to “fix” misfires—if only slightly.

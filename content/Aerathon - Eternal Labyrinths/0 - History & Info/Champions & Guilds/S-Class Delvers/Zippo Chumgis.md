## **Overview**

**Zippo Chumgis**, once known as _The Ember Duke of Cael_, is one of the most powerful and theatrical pyromantic sorcerers Aerathon has ever known. Renowned for both the scale of his infernos and the flare of his performances, Zippo and his delving partner, **Thalia Vess**, were household names—a pair of brilliant A-Rank delvers who climbed into S-Rank legend through courage, charm, and controlled chaos.

Together, they were beloved. Until the delve at **Ashfall Spire**, where Thalia fell—her body unrecovered, her name etched into flame. Zippo returned from that delve a changed man. He never delved again.

Now, he runs **The Spark That Binds**, a humble (if wildly overdecorated) magical item shop tucked into a busy Arnerian market street. But those who visit swear the flame behind his eyes still lingers—quiet, smoldering, waiting.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Ignition of Embervault**  
Zippo singlehandedly disarmed a volatile relic core by absorbing its firestorm into his own weave. His cape burned away. His eyebrows never grew back. The vault survived.

**The Dance of Two Flames**  
Zippo and Thalia’s livecast dueling performance—part delve, part concert—shut down two scry networks from sheer viewer overload. Their spell choreography was later studied as a fusion art form known as _Blazewaltz_.

**The Ashfall Pact**  
In their final delve, Thalia gave her life holding off an entropic warden while Zippo sealed the core. The resulting explosion marked the land with a new scar: **Ashfall Spire**. Zippo has never spoken of what happened beneath the stone.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**Cindercall**  
A red-crystal wand fused with dragonbone and embersteel. Allows Zippo to command multiple flame sources like dancing blades. Once ignited, it sings in an old dialect of Primordial.

**The Cloak of Golden Cinders**  
Tailored from phoenix-molt silk, it trails sparks with every movement. Grants fire resistance and once per day can **fully absorb** a fire-based spell and turn it into radiant energy.

**The Broken Pendant of Thalia Vess**  
Worn under his robes. It no longer glows. He refuses to enchant or repair it. Its magic is unknown.

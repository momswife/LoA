## **Overview**

**Durnan Blackthistle**, once called _The Warden of Deepwind Vale_, was a dwarven ranger whose unmatched skill in tracking, ambushing, and surviving labyrinth ecosystems made him a legend among delvers. Stoic, sharp-eyed, and deeply bonded to the land, Durnan was a cornerstone of many successful expeditions into the wildest and most unstable Labyrinth biomes. His arrows never missed. His words were few, but always final.

Then came the delve into the **Shadowsink Hollows**—a forbidden-tier anomaly that devoured three teams before Durnan’s expedition was greenlit. The crew never returned. Days later, the Labyrinth’s core **sealed itself** from the inside.

Two weeks later, Durnan reappeared at the edge of the Gloamwood. Alone. Changed.

He wears no emblem now, speaks in riddles when he speaks at all, and walks like a man listening to something just behind him. Some say he still helps delvers lost in the dark… others say he’s what gets them lost in the first place.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Nightfang Hunt**  
Tracked and felled a phase-wolf pack that had eluded capture for four years. He took no reward, only carved a cairn for the lost patrol they had consumed.

**The Rootcaller Pact**  
Brokered peace between a labyrinth-born treant and a relic-retrieval squad by offering his own blood as proof of intent. The pact is still honored to this day.

**The Hollow Reemergence**  
Following the Shadowsink incident, Durnan appeared to save a team of novice delvers caught in a collapsing mirror floor. He said nothing. When the lights returned, he was gone—and their shadows now trail behind them a second slower than they should.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**Gravebark Longbow**  
Crafted from a fallen elder tree in the Weeping Woods. It never splinters and can fire arrows of silence, binding, and shadowflame. The string hums when enemies lie nearby.

**The Thorncloak**  
A shifting mantle of bark, bone, and vine. Camouflages perfectly in darkness. Now infested with unknown glyphs that glow black under moonlight.

**[Redacted] – “The Hollow Brand”**  
An artifact fused to Durnan’s chest after the Shadowsink delve. No one knows its full name. Those who see it directly report migraines, shadow tremors, and memory degradation. It pulses when divine light is near.

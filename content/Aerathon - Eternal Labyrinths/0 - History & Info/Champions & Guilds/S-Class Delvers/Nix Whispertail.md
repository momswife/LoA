## **Overview**

**Nix of the Whispertail**, a Beastfolk of unknown origin and uncertain species—most describe them as a blend of possum, ferret, and moon-shadow—rose to S-Class not through power, prophecy, or divine favor… but through **pure, perfect stealth**.

Nix didn’t slay Labyrinth horrors. They **avoided** them. They didn’t trigger traps—they **disabled** them before anyone else knew they were armed. Entire delve teams have returned home swearing they survived impossible odds, only to later learn that **someone else had already cleared the way.**

Some say Nix was born in a Labyrinth. Others believe they were once a cursed child granted a second chance by **Mask** or **Eilistraee**. Whatever the truth, Nix has become legend among low-ranked delvers and undercity whisper-circles alike: the shadow that walks before danger, the flick of a tail that saves a dozen lives unseen.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Silent Route of Wyrmreach**  
Where six guild teams perished in a floor filled with slumbering wyverns, Nix passed through alone. Days later, every trap was disarmed, every monster still sleeping, and the relic left on a pedestal at the exit. No one saw them enter.

**The Clockless Chamber Inversion**  
A temporal anomaly locked an entire team in recursive loops. Nix infiltrated the maze **in reverse**, rewrote the glyphs by moonlight, and left the team a note: _“Time’s fixed. Try not to break it again.”_

**The Rescue of Thistlekin Hollow**  
During a Labyrinth-induced shadow plague, Nix distributed healing draughts to every villager **without anyone ever seeing them enter**. All doors were still locked in the morning. A child found a tailprint in ash and thanked the wind.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**The Echocloak**  
A woven shimmer-cloak of mothwing and dusk-thread, taken from a forgotten demiplane. Grants invisibility in dim light and mutes all sound within 10 feet. Can flutter into decoys once per rest.

**Thievesight Veil**  
A band of silk worn over the eyes. Grants echolocation-like awareness in darkness and highlights weak points in magical traps and false walls.

**The Hollow Dagger**  
A curved blade of cold iron and shadowglass. Leaves no blood, only silence. Can pass through illusions and magical wards without triggering them.

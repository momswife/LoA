## Overview

**Kaelen Viremoor**, known in many circles as **The Oathshard**, is an S-Class delver whose deeds are marked equally by heroism and betrayal. Once a knight of **the Emberfold Compact**, Kaelen fell from grace after publicly executing a noble during a rescue operation gone wrong. Pardoned by the people but exiled by the crown, he now serves no lord, guild, or god—only the mission as he sees it.

Kaelen is a **Hexblade Warlock / Battlemaster Fighter**, bonded to a sentient weapon known as **Judicar**, which constantly questions his intent. He walks the line between strict martial order and the freedom to defy unjust systems—often by breaking them.

## Notable Accomplishments

**The Siege of Emberhold**  
During a failed rescue mission of delver hostages, Kaelen slew the host city's regent after uncovering that the hostage-taking was state-sanctioned. Though this action saved 27 innocents, it broke a binding treaty and triggered a four-year political fallout.

**The Shattered Tribunal**  
Kaelen was brought before a magical council to answer for abandoning a delve party mid-mission. Records show he returned later—alone—and pulled each fallen body from the labyrinth’s depths, restoring them all with his own coin and pact favors. He left before the tribunal reached a verdict.

**The Pact of the Third Mercy**  
It is said Kaelen once made a deal with a dying Archfey to give three mortal enemies the choice to live. Two chose death. The third—now his rival—seeks vengeance for the mercy he cannot repay.

## Equipment and Artifacts

**Judicar, the Questioning Blade**  
A sentient hexblade that feeds on moral compromise. Grows stronger the more Kaelen doubts his own cause. If he acts with total certainty, the blade becomes dull.

**Cloak of Unseen Chains**  
Grants immunity to compelled speech or magical oaths. Used often to bypass unjust contracts—but also feared by ally factions for its ability to hide intent.

**The Oathshard Sigil**  
An amulet made from the broken badge of his former knighthood. Can be invoked once per day to impose disadvantage on any divine smite or punishment cast on Kaelen by someone who claims to be acting “in justice.”

## Overview

**Vaelis Dawncleaver** is an S-Class delver, renowned across Aerathon as the _Radiant Blade of Halvask_. A paragon of martial prowess and divine fervor, he is a Paladin of the Oath of Glory whose name has become synonymous with the golden age of delving. Clad in mythsteel plate and wielding the legendary greatblade **Solstice Requiem**, Vaelis leads with unwavering authority in both battle and the political arenas of the guild-state Halvask.

## Notable Accomplishments

### _The Scourge of Sungrave Citadel_

Vaelis led a combined expeditionary force into the demonic ruins of Sungrave, holding the breach single-handedly against waves of abyssal war-kin for eight hours. His blade burned so bright that the citadel was visible from Halvask's western wall.

### _First to Survive the Labyrinth of Ashened Glass_

A labyrinth of refracted madness and temporal dislocation—no adventurer had returned from it until Vaelis emerged three days later, eyes seared and hair turned silver from the ordeal. His journal from that delve is now kept in the **Sanctum of Echoes**.

### _Defender of the Eightfold Pact_

When the Celestial Accord nearly collapsed during the **Guild War of Verdant Spite**, Vaelis interceded by invoking an ancient rite of honor-duel. His victory preserved peace and forced binding treaties among the seven major delving guilds.

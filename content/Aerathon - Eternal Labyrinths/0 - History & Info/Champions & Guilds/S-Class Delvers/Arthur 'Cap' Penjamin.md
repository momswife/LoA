## **Overview**

**Arthur “Cap” Penjamin** was one of Aerathon’s most dependable and battle-hardened frontline delvers—a Barbarian famed not for rage or recklessness, but for **unyielding protection**. Wielding a tower shield forged from labyrinth-wrought adamantine, Cap was the anchor of countless delve parties, standing fast against horrors that would turn most to dust.

He earned the name “Cap” not just for his leadership, but because **he never charged first**—he held the line, drew the blows, and let others shine. A favorite among healers, squires, and civilians, Cap was the embodiment of reliability. Until he picked up **the Stormhowler.**

A lightning-bound axe sealed within a forgotten shrine deep in the Labyrinth of Breakerspine. It called to him. It gave him strength. It made him faster, stronger, and hungry for more.

He died a year later. But not in battle. Not in glory. He was struck down by his own party, weeping and screaming beneath the thunder, too far gone to be saved.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Stand at Granite Hollow**  
Held a breach gate alone for seven minutes while his party sealed a dimensional rift. His shield blocked 146 recorded strikes, including two spectral dragons and a warden’s curse-lance.

**The Warpath of Twelve**  
Led a ragtag squad of under-ranked delvers through a corrupted multi-floor delve. Not one casualty. His squire received full guild honors—Cap had carried him unconscious for the last two floors.

**The Fall of Stormhowler’s Bastion**  
Final delve. Cap wielded the lightning axe against a living labyrinth core that had begun feeding on delver fear. He destroyed the core—but lost himself. His final moments were broadcast in fragments before the feed was cut. All seven of his companions survived—only because they stopped him.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**The Aegis of Cadenwall**  
A massive shield bound with divine favor and dozens of protective runes. Grants resistance to force and psychic damage. Its inscriptions shift slightly whenever a team member is wounded.

**Stormhowler** _(Corrupted Relic)_  
A greataxe humming with crackling lightning and a half-sentient storm spirit sealed inside. Increases physical prowess and speed, but gradually overrides moral clarity. Known to whisper tactics mid-fight… and grow louder over time.

**Medallion of Fourfold Oaths**  
Worn over his heart. A symbol of the vows he made to each of his four original party members. It blackened on the day he raised Stormhowler.

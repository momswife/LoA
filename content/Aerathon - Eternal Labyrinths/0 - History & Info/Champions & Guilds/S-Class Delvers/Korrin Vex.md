## Overview

**Korrin Vex** is an S-Class delver and master manipulator, best known for his roles in high-stakes infiltration, magical heists, and strategic sabotage of rival guilds. A **Rogue of the Mastermind archetype**, Vex is the unseen hand in many of Aerathon’s most dramatic shifts in guild power—often revealed only in the aftermath, when vaults lie emptied, and alliances turned.

Operating under multiple aliases across a dozen cities, Korrin is both a ghost and a rumor. His delves rarely follow traditional protocol, and his successes often come without bloodshed—though his few victims describe his presence as utterly paralyzing. Unlike many of his peers, Vex thrives not in the fray, but in the plan behind the fray.

## Notable Accomplishments

**The Hollow Vault Heist**  
Korrin orchestrated the total emptying of the Sapphire Guild’s arcane vault without triggering a single alarm. No one saw him enter. All ten locks were magical. The only evidence left behind was a playing card with the word “Try.”

**The Three-Guild Coup**  
Vex manipulated internal factions in three rival guilds—_The Silver Talons_, _The Veilbound_, and _Bastion Arcana_—leading to an inter-guild war that dissolved all three within a week. He was later seen calmly sipping tea on a Halvask rooftop during the final vote of disbandment.

**Escape from the Death Warrant Trials**  
After being named in three separate magical warrants (including one issued by the Divine Oculi), Vex presented his own defense, cross-examined his accusers, and convinced the tribunal to expunge all records—including their own memories of the case.

## Equipment and Artifacts

**The Quiet Ledger**  
A pocketbook enchanted to contain every debt, secret, and bribe Vex has collected. It’s warded against divination and explodes into false memories if read without permission.

**Cloak of Reversal**  
Shifts the wearer's appearance and voice to mirror that of their attacker. Functions for up to one minute per day and resets upon successful deception.

**Ghostlock Picks**  
Phase through non-magical locks. Against magical locks, they operate by replicating the exact hand movements of the original keyholder—requiring a memory sample or a whispered name.

## Rumors and Classified Reports

- Vex is believed to possess **a perfect memory**, possibly granted by an encounter with a memory daemon.
    
- Some scholars believe he has stolen or duplicated a fragment of the **Arcanet Core’s logic system**, giving him preternatural insight into delver rankings.
    
- There are **no known images** of Korrin that predate the last decade. Some suggest the name itself is inherited, or an identity passed down between operatives.
    
- The Divine Oculi once suspended a full sector of livecasting due to “privacy breaches originating from within the audience feed.” Analysts suspect Vex was watching them watch him.

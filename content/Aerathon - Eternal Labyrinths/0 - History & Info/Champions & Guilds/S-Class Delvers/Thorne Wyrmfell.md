## Overview

**Thorne Wyrmfell** is an S-Class delver of beastfolk descent—an ursine-blooded warrior-scholar known for his hybrid mastery of instinct and strategy. Towering, furred, and bearing the traits of an ancient bear lineage, Thorne balances raw physical dominance with startling intellect. A **Ranger (Monster Slayer archetype)** by trade, he serves as a living countermeasure to the horrors that crawl from the darkest labyrinth depths.

Though not affiliated with any city-guild, Thorne is revered in nomadic clans and feared in underground gambling circles, where his delve survivability has broken predictive magic more than once. Despite his feral appearance, he speaks with clarity and depth, invoking ancestral codes rather than manmade law.

## Notable Accomplishments

**The Spineback Culling**  
Thorne hunted and killed an entire brood of basilisk-wyrms in the labyrinth known as **The Maw Below**, despite being heavily wounded. He left their hides hanging at the floor entrance, a warning and offering.

**The Iron Root Accord**  
Brokering a peace between three monstrous factions (ettercaps, minotaurs, and stonefangs) in **Labyrinth 23: Fanghold Cradle**, Thorne prevented a delve war by invoking an ancient rite of challenge. He slew their champions bare-handed and then shared the kill with all sides. The region remains uncontested to this day.

**The Wyrmblood Reckoning**  
While other delvers fled the incursion of the serpent-lich **Orovek the Coiled**, Thorne tracked its trail across five floors of molten tunnels, slayed its heralds, and shattered its soul-fang—denying its rebirth.

## Equipment and Artifacts

**The Fangbreaker Bow**  
A composite longbow made from the rib of a fallen hydra, designed to pierce magical scales. It requires the user to bleed upon the string for every fifth shot.

**Runestone Collar**  
Carved from petrified wood and stone-bonded fur. Grants immunity to charm and fear, but cannot be removed without a druid’s aid. Considered a sacred relic among beastfolk.

**Claw of the First Pact**  
An ancestral dagger fused to a gauntlet of bone. Can paralyze monstrosities or aberrations on a strike if the wielder speaks the creature’s true name.

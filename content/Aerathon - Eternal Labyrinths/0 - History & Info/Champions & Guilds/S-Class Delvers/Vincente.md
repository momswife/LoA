## **Overview**

**Vincente “The Virtuous Vortex” Bellaro** is an S-Class delver, bard, and former world-titled professional wrestler whose charisma, power, and radiant showmanship have made him one of the most beloved figures in Aerathon’s modern age. Draped in gold-threaded robes and glittering belts from his championship days, Vincente enters Labyrinths like a stage—and every fight becomes a performance worthy of thunderous applause.

But beneath the flair is a heart of unshakable virtue. Every coin he’s earned from relics, endorsements, or livecast votes has been donated to orphans, war widows, beastfolk rebuilding funds, and burned-out villages. His followers call him “The People's Champion.” His critics call him “too good to be real.”

He calls himself “just a humble hero with a wicked suplex.”

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Elbow Drop of Dawnspire**  
In a sky-bridge labyrinth collapsing mid-air, Vincente launched a flying elbow from 80 feet above the central node, disrupting a ritual mid-cast. The spell backfired into a dome of light, saving 23 bound villagers. He rose from the crater and blew a kiss to the sky.

**The Six-Song Slamdown**  
Faced with six cursed gladiators under a mind-binding chant, Vincente crafted a new bardic anthem mid-combat—each verse weaving into a different wrestling finisher. The enemies were freed with their dignity intact and joined him in a closing bow.

**The Heel Turn Redemption Arc**  
A former rival delver known as Dagger Deliah betrayed him on a delve, stealing a relic and vanishing. Vincente refused vengeance. Months later, he donated a legendary relic in her name to a sanctuary she once burned down. She returned it—with her guild crest inside.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**The Golden Mic**  
An arcane wand disguised as a wrestling mic. Amplifies bardic spells and can cast _Command_ or _Heroism_ once per rest when punctuated by a rhyme or cheer.

**The Belt of the Bellowing Heart**  
A champion’s belt that pulses with bardic resonance. Grants thunder resistance and adds sonic damage to unarmed strikes—particularly elbow drops and leg sweeps.

**Cloak of Crowd Control**  
A sequined cloak woven from enchanted stadium banners. Once per day, Vincente can cast _Mass Suggestion_ (audience-only), compelling overwhelming support and morale boosts.

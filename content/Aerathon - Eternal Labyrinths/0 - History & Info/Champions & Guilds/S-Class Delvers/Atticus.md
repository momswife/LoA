## **Overview**

**Atticus of the First Howl** is a Beastfolk wolf and S-Class delver known across Aerathon not only for his **unyielding strength** and **lethal combat style**, but for his **loyalty, honor, and compassion**—a paradox in the deadly halls of the Labyrinths. Towering in frame, his fur marked by age and battles survived, Atticus once led a delving crew called _The Hearthbound_, a tight-knit band of mixed beastfolk and outcasts known for never leaving anyone behind.

They’re all gone now. Taken in a failed delve five years past. But Atticus remained.

He continues to delve—not for glory, nor coin, but in **service of their memory**, offering protection to the weak, guidance to the lost, and teeth to the unjust. Among newer delvers, he's spoken of like a mythic guardian: if you bleed with honor, Atticus will find you.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Shattering of Crimson Peak**  
When a delver team was pinned beneath a collapsing relic chamber, Atticus tore through a barricade of mimic-fused stone to reach them. One of the trapped delvers later reported, “I thought I was dead. Then I saw those golden eyes, and I knew I wasn’t.”

**The Howl of Moonlight Bastion**  
During a siege by planar shades, Atticus led a group of unarmed villagers through a Labyrinth shortcut, howling the entire way to ward off the spectral swarm. His howl reportedly echoed for miles—and stunned six enemies into vapor.

**The Last Hearth Oath**  
When his entire team fell to an unrecoverable zone collapse, Atticus etched their names into his armor and swore to take no relics for profit—only for preservation or peace. He has kept that vow to this day.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**Wardenfang**  
A curved greataxe forged of darksteel and bound to a wolf-totem spirit. Can cleave magical barriers and emits a resonant hum when wielded in defense of others. When swung in fury, it leaves afterimages of ancestral wolves.

**Cloak of the Hearth Vow**  
Tattered, fireproof hide cloak stitched with fur from his fallen crew. Grants aura-based protection to nearby allies. Can absorb one lethal blow per day if Atticus stands between the target and harm.

**Bracers of the Bonded Oath**  
Glowing silver runes pulse across these iron bands. Once per delve, allows Atticus to share damage taken with an ally—or take it fully himself. The pulse always leaves behind a warmth.

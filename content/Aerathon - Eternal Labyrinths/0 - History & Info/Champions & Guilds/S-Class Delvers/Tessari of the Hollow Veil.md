## Overview

**Tessari of the Hollow Veil** is an S-Class delver, feared for her eldritch command of shadow and her deep knowledge of forbidden truths. A Shadow Sorcerer whose origins remain obscured even to the most persistent divinations, Tessari first appeared during the Ebon Sundering—a convergence event marked by shadowstorms and mass planar instability. She claims to have “died” at birth and returned by clawing through the folds of the Veil itself.

A pariah among traditionalists and a saint among cultists, Tessari walks the fringes of Aerathon’s labyrinth society. She has declined sponsorships and public honors, refusing all guild membership since her departure from the Crimson Reavers. Despite this, her name and veiled visage remain etched into countless legends, and her livecast appearances are subjects of conspiracy, obsession, and state censorship.

## Notable Accomplishments

**The Sealing of the Whispering Maw**  
Tessari entered the breach in the Blacksteel Labyrinth where a rent in the Veil allowed entities of thought to whisper into delvers’ minds. Dozens had fallen to madness. Tessari emerged alone three hours later, her right eye milky white, her voice inverted for seven days. The breach was silent thereafter.

**The Pact of the Hollow Echo**  
While most warlocks form pacts with beings, Tessari is rumored to have forged hers with the dying fragment of a _concept_—a non-sentient but willful idea once known as the Third Voice. The effects of this pact are still poorly understood, but they seem to unmoor her presence across multiple temporal states.

**Silent Duel at Griefspire**  
During a philosophical tribunal, Archmage Tylaran of the Irenic Scholarium challenged Tessari to ritual combat. Tessari accepted, but cast no spells. Five minutes passed before the Archmage collapsed, eyes dark with entropy. She spoke only once: “He lost the moment he believed I would fight.”

## Equipment and Artifacts

**The Hollow Veil**  
A living cloak made from the wings of a dream-wight. Allows passage between shadow realms, nullifies low-level scrying, and murmurs the names of nearby liars.

**Veinglass Dagger**  
Cuts through illusions and verbal deceit. Wounds inflicted by the blade leave no blood, only memory.

**Tome of the Third Voice**  
A bound, inverse-language codex locked by psionic wards. Causes severe memory disruption in readers without veil training.

## Rumors and Anomalies

- Several livecasts featuring Tessari contain **auditory bleed**, including reversed voices and recursive sounds. Viewers report dreams of black staircases and clocks melting into themselves.
    
- Her shadow sometimes detaches itself briefly and moves independently.
    
- She once claimed to have spoken with “the silence before Mystra’s name was first spoken”—a metaphysical paradox most scholars refuse to acknowledge.
    
- Divine scryers attempting to read her future have reported mirror trauma and ritual silence.

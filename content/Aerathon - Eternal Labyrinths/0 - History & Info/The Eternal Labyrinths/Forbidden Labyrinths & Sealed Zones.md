## **Overview**

While the world of Aerathon thrives on the merits of exploration and conquest within the Labyrinths, not all such places are meant to be traversed. Some structures have proven too unstable, too cursed, or too far-reaching in their magical influence. Others are sealed by divine ordinance, pact-breach, or because of their history in birthing calamities.

This registry is maintained as a _living document_ by the MDO. Access is granted to vetted commanders, archivists, and Delvers of **S-Rank** status or higher with official warrant.

---

## **Labyrinth Classifications**

|Label|Description|
|---|---|
|**Forbidden**|Entry is legally and magically barred. May involve divine seals, collapse events, or metaphysical hazards.|
|**Sealed**|The labyrinth is actively locked, but not forbidden. May be re-opened with authorization or under prophecy.|
|**Collapsed**|Structural integrity lost. Still dangerous due to ambient arcane emissions or relic contamination.|
|**Quarantined**|Magical or physical infection has spread from the labyrinth. Entry restricted to sanctioned research teams.|
## **Known Entries**

### _The Labyrinth of Shard-Kin_

- **Status:** Forbidden
    
- **Location:** Beneath the Old Bone Steppes
    
- **Cause:** Creation of artificial lifeforms immune to both healing and banishment. Breached containment during Delver War II.
    
- **Notes:** The last team to enter emerged as crystalline versions of themselves, bearing no pulse and whispering in reverse tongue. Entry blocked by living sigil-web.
    

---

### _The Black Bloom Crater_

- **Status:** Quarantined
    
- **Location:** Ruins of Eld-Veyra
    
- **Cause:** Magical flora from within labyrinth spread spores across four provinces, mutating livestock and children.
    
- **Notes:** The bloom regenerates when burned. All attempts to teleport within fail. A single harp song echoes from the crater center.
    

---

### _The Hollow Bastion of Surn IX_

- **Status:** Sealed
    
- **Location:** Skyward Isles, locked in mid-collapse
    
- **Cause:** Pact vault containing over 300 sentient contracts. One broke loose and overrode the soul of the lead archivist.
    
- **Notes:** The vault hovers just 30 feet above ground now, tethered by anchor-runes. Still shifts phase every solstice.
    

---

### _The Dreamcoil Vault_

- **Status:** Forbidden
    
- **Location:** No stable coordinates; appears only in dreams of high-level spellcasters
    
- **Cause:** Enters the physical world when three spellcasters simultaneously dream of the same gate.
    
- **Notes:** Those who exit the Vault cannot distinguish between memory and reality. One Delver team believed to have overwritten themselves into Aerathon’s past.
    

---

### _The Pit of the Unbodied_

- **Status:** Collapsed
    
- **Location:** Beneath Southmark’s Hollow Ridge
    
- **Cause:** Contained incorporeal entities that devoured ego, language, and lineage. Structure collapsed inward.
    
- **Notes:** Occasionally still emits screams in languages unknown to any known record. The entrance remains visibly sealed but _emotionally accessible_—some adventurers report sensing it in grief or dreams.
    

---

### _The Silver Mouth_

- **Status:** Forbidden
    
- **Location:** Vein-Lands beneath Halvask
    
- **Cause:** Spoken word contagion—language used in the labyrinth rewrites the lexicon of anyone who hears it.
    
- **Notes:** The “Silver Tongue Effect” caused a brief incident of mass poetic speech and language drift across 11 districts. Now magically muted. Entering is punishable by erasure.
    

---

## **Containment Measures**

- **Warding Pylons:** Arcane towers erected near entrances, disrupt planar breach events. Maintained by Arcanet Technicians.
    
- **Divine Seals:** Applied only through Oculi writ or Saint-borne petition. Cannot be broken by mortal magic.
    
- **Memory Fog Protocol:** Implemented in affected border areas; uses enchantment fields to dissuade memory of the labyrinth’s presence.
    
- **Livecast Lockouts:** Forbidden zones are blocked from broadcast. Any magically enhanced scrying within results in feedback corruption.

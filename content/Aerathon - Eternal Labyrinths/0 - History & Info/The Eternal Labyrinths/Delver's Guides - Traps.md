
____
## **Overview**

The labyrinths of Aerathon are not simply filled with beasts and puzzles—they are living machines, cunning in their cruelty and ancient in their design. Many traps are static mechanisms, but others adapt, shift, or even **respond to audience choices** in livecast-enabled labyrinths.

This guide documents a wide range of observed traps—mechanical, magical, psychological, and environmental—organized by type, frequency, and severity. The following information is publicly distributed to all registered Delvers with C-Rank clearance or higher.

---

## **Trap Classification**

|Type|Description|
|---|---|
|**Mechanical**|Trigger-based devices: pressure plates, blades, darts|
|**Magical**|Enchantments, illusions, glyphs, summoning circles|
|**Environmental**|Shifting terrain, gravity fields, temperature traps|
|**Psychological**|Hallucinations, memory traps, emotional manipulation|
|**Livecast-Bound**|Audience-triggered events, vote-based hazards|

## **Commonly Documented Traps**

### _The Echoing Step_

- **Type:** Mechanical
    
- **Effect:** Pressure plate activates on repeated footsteps in rhythm; delays the third trigger by 6 seconds.
    
- **Location:** _Unbound Maze_, _Shattered Throne_
    
- **Countermeasure:** Disrupt pacing with irregular stride; rogue-led team advised.
    

---

### _Glyph of Withering Insight_

- **Type:** Magical
    
- **Effect:** Explodes psychic energy upon attempted identification; drains 1d4 INT permanently if failed.
    
- **Location:** _Hollow Veil_, _Bonehold Sanctum_
    
- **Countermeasure:** Detect Magic, avoid attempts to “read” the glyph directly. Report all variants to Arcanet Core.
    

---

### _False Floor of Blooming Thorns_

- **Type:** Environmental
    
- **Effect:** Drop pit hidden beneath shifting flora. Victims fall into necrotic vines that animate on contact.
    
- **Location:** _Verdant Coil_, _Bloom Crater Remnants_
    
- **Countermeasure:** Probe ahead with polearms or summoned familiars. Druid magic unreliable here.
    

---

### _Pactmirror Puzzle Lock_

- **Type:** Psychological
    
- **Effect:** User must answer a question “as if they were their truest self.” Incorrect answer causes identity confusion for 1d4 hours.
    
- **Location:** _Silver Mouth_, _Obsidian Crown_
    
- **Countermeasure:** Have allies ask the afflicted questions they would _never_ ask themselves.
    

---

### _The Applause Trap_

- **Type:** Livecast-Bound
    
- **Effect:** Room lights up, and audience voting opens. The most-voted character becomes a “target of fate,” increasing enemy aggro and trap focus for 3 rounds.
    
- **Location:** _Unbound Maze_, _Hollow Veil_ (livecast-enabled only)
    
- **Countermeasure:** Use decoys or suppress live feedback feed temporarily.
    

---

### _Chalice of Emptying Hope_

- **Type:** Magical
    
- **Effect:** Resembles a healing item. Drains all temporary hit points and inspiration; triggers on drinking.
    
- **Location:** _Salt-Rot Vaults_, _Dreamcoil Vault_
    
- **Countermeasure:** Arcana check (DC 17) reveals minor delay in fluid refraction. Trust no unattended magical liquids.
    

---

### _The Reversal Arch_

- **Type:** Environmental
    
- **Effect:** Passing through this arch reverses the effect of beneficial auras and spells for 1 minute.
    
- **Location:** _Shattered Throne_, _Vault of Nine Doors_
    
- **Countermeasure:** Dispel Magic before passage or send non-magical allies first.
    

---

### _Memory Glass_

- **Type:** Psychological
    
- **Effect:** A room lined with mirrors. Each reflects a false version of the viewer, feeding them contradictory memories until their identity collapses (WIS save DC 18).
    
- **Location:** _Obsidian Crown_
    
- **Countermeasure:** Close eyes. Let another guide you through. Some guilds use wax eye-covers or blindfolds.
    

---

## **Trap Ratings**

|Threat Tier|Description|
|---|---|
|**Minor**|Inconvenience or minor damage only|
|**Moderate**|Could severely wound or disorient|
|**Major**|Deadly unless countered properly|
|**Cataclysmic**|Causes party wipes, shifts entire room/floor|

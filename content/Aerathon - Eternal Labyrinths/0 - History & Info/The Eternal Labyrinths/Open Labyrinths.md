## **Overview**

Not all labyrinths are sealed or forbidden. Many remain active, open, and officially sanctioned for exploration. These sites are cleared through Delver Accords, stabilized by Arcanet pylons, and monitored via livecasting networks for both audience engagement and safety regulation.

This registry details the most prominent labyrinths that are currently operational and host active guild delving efforts. Their hazards are real, their rewards legendary, and they remain at the center of Aerathon’s economy, politics, and heroism.

Each listing includes:

- **Name of the Labyrinth**
    
- **Primary Theme or Threat**
    
- **Delving Tier Range**
    
- **Location**
    
- **Notable Traits or History**
    

---

## **Active Labyrinths**

### _The Labyrinth of the Verdant Coil_

- **Theme:** Plantlife, Mutagenic Spores, Beasts
    
- **Delving Tier:** C to A
    
- **Location:** Western Gloomhollow Basin
    
- **Notables:** Changes floor arrangement weekly. Known for rare alchemical ingredients and symbiotic familiars. Dangerous in spring due to bloom frenzy.
    

---

### _The Labyrinth of the Shattered Throne_

- **Theme:** Ruins, Elemental Guardians, Echoes of Royalty
    
- **Delving Tier:** B to S
    
- **Location:** Mountains of Caldrith's Spine
    
- **Notables:** Contains remnants of a forgotten empire. Artifacts retrieved from this site tend to influence charisma-based classes. Livecast views spike whenever teams reach the inner court.
    

---

### _The Hollow Veil_

- **Theme:** Shadow, Illusion, Dream Invasion
    
- **Delving Tier:** A to S
    
- **Location:** Near the border of the Twilight Fen
    
- **Notables:** Floors are not always physical; some are entirely within constructed dreamscapes. Requires a Dreamwarden on standby for recovery.
    

---

### _The Emberdream Nest_

- **Theme:** Heat, Fire Elementals, Memory Constructs
    
- **Delving Tier:** D to B
    
- **Location:** Deep under the Magmara Span
    
- **Notables:** Well-suited for new adventuring teams. Famed for “soul-embers,” which can store or replay personal memories. Boasts the highest Delver survival rate.
    

---

### _The Unbound Maze_

- **Theme:** Teleportation, Dimensional Warping, Logic Puzzles
    
- **Delving Tier:** B to A
    
- **Location:** Central Arclands, floating structure tethered to pylons
    
- **Notables:** Changes room layouts with audience votes during livecasts. Designed by an unknown artificer—possibly extraplanar. Used as a Delver exam labyrinth by the Arcanet Core.
    

---

### _The Salt-Rot Vaults_

- **Theme:** Undead, Sea Magic, Forgotten Contracts
    
- **Delving Tier:** A to S
    
- **Location:** Sunken coast of the Drowned Marches
    
- **Notables:** High magic suppression zones. Contains multiple sealed pact altars. Guilds specializing in divine magic often compete fiercely for deeper runs.
    

---

### _The Obsidian Crown_

- **Theme:** Constructs, Political Intrigue, Memory Games
    
- **Delving Tier:** C to A
    
- **Location:** Buried beneath the Old Sovereign Road
    
- **Notables:** Rewards cunning and charisma over brute force. Some floors require non-lethal resolution. Known for generating political artifacts tied to defunct kingdoms.
    

---

## **Standard Safety Protocols**

- **Mandatory Arcanet Tags:** All delvers must wear Arcanet-linked sigils for live monitoring.
    
- **Emergency Anchor Teams:** Magical extraction teams stand ready at all times.
    
- **Audience Influence:** Certain labyrinths permit audience-triggered events (via livecast coin). Guilds must consent beforehand.
    
- **Ranking Validation:** Each Delver team must be cleared for the labyrinth’s tier range via Arcanet Core validation.

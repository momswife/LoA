## **Overview**

Ursines—also known as bears—carry the weight of history in more ways than one. Their perfect recall is a divine gift, capturing every sight, sound, and feeling with unshakable clarity. This eidetic memory defines their place in Beast World society, where they are beloved historians, staunch allies, and iron-willed rivals.

Most commonly found in the northern peaks of Oria, ursines are built for cold winds and colder fights. Their hardy frames are matched only by their competitive spirits—an ursine will challenge their kin to a wrestling match, a storytelling contest, or a mountain climb just to prove who’s best. But this constant competition is often affectionate. For many ursines, rivalry is a way to show love.

Though often boisterous, they are deeply reflective creatures. Family and heritage are everything to them. Their long memories make every moment a thread in a tapestry, every insult long-remembered, and every triumph carefully recorded. Many seek out the Labyrinths not for gold, but for the stories and scars they can bring home.

They are stubborn, proud, and unyielding. But to have an ursine at your back is to carry the strength of ages.

## **Ursine Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 30 feet.

**Eidetic Memory.** You can accurately recall anything you’ve seen or heard. Additionally, when you make a Strength or Dexterity saving throw against a creature’s nonmagical ability, you have advantage on future saves against that same ability.

**Mnemonic Mind.** When a creature you can see within 60 feet casts a spell on your spell list, you can use your reaction to commit it to memory. You must visit an arcane library within 7 days to study it. After 8 hours of research per spell level and a successful Arcana check (DC 10 + spell level), you may add it to your spells known or copy it into a spellbook, as if from a scroll. You can only know one spell this way at a time.

---

### **Bear**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Intelligence score increases by 1.

**Size.** You are Medium.

**Toughen Up.** When you roll initiative, you may immediately spend a number of Hit Dice up to half your level (minimum 1) to gain that many temporary hit points. These last for 10 minutes.

## **Homeland Traits**

### **Allemance**

Allemagnian bears grow up among artists and academics, where debate is a sport and charisma is as valued as might. These bears are confident performers and passionate speakers, using their knowledge to disarm opponents and charm friends.

**Spirited Discourse.** You may use Intelligence instead of Charisma for Performance and Persuasion checks. Once per long rest, when a creature within 30 feet fails a save against being charmed, you may grant them a reroll as a reaction.

---

### **Oria**

In the icy north, young bears risk life and limb to prove themselves. A dangerous tradition sends them up dragon-claimed volcanoes to steal obsidian, which they forge into trophies. These keepsakes are worn with pride, each one proof of defiance and survival.

**Obsidian Bauble.** You gain proficiency with smith’s tools. With 8 hours of work and 30 gp, you can forge a unique bauble from obsidian that stores a 1st-level spell cast into it. Anyone holding it can release the spell once. The bauble is destroyed in the process.

---

### **Vinyot**

In the courts and offices of Vinyot, ursines' perfect memories make them ideal lawyers, dealmakers, and judges. Their command of logic and precedent is so thorough, some say they bend the rules of magic as easily as those of law.

**Rules Lawyer.** Choose two 1st-level spells. They count as being on all class spell lists for you.

---

### **Arneria**

In the gem-laced mountains of the Bey’s Head, red-furred bears dig deep. Their hunger to succeed often leads them to follow the radiant call of Aubadism. Strength, legacy, and a mark upon the world—these are the goals of Arnerian bears.

**Born of the Bey’s Head.** You gain proficiency with the war pick. If you hit with a war pick on the first round of combat, you deal an additional 2d6 piercing damage.

---

### **Al’Ar**

Al’ari bears found their niche not through mimicry, but through mastery. They teach strength-based martial arts to feline peers, using their bulk and balance to instruct others in the grace of brute force. Their wrestling schools are as popular as their cooking.

**Instructive Muscle.** You are proficient in Athletics. When you succeed on a Strength (Athletics) check, creatures that share a language with you and saw the attempt are treated as proficient in Athletics when attempting the same task.

## **Overview**

In the wild and wondrous realms of the Beast World, the _Bovine_ folk are earthbound guardians, born with an uncanny communion with soil and sprout. Angus and bison kin wield this gift to awaken lifeless lands, conjuring verdant pasture from desolation. Within the depths of labyrinths, these sturdy folk can glean survival lore from lichen and moss, as if the very walls whispered secrets to them.

Though their bulk renders them poor scouts or delicate tinkerers, what they lack in subtlety they more than make up for in might. Angus bulls bring brute force to the frontlines, hurling their weight like living siege weapons, while bison-born warriors cleave through foes with titanic weapons few others could lift. They hail from open lands—anguses roam Allemance's green expanse, while bison endure the Beylik's sun-scorched sands.

In any delving party, they are the wall that will not break, the fury that answers insult with horn and fist. Just pray you don’t spill their ale.

## **Bovine Traits**

**Creature Type.** You are a Humanoid.

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Strength score increases by 2.

**Speed.** Your walking speed is 30 feet.

**March On Hooves.** You have advantage on saving throws you make to avoid suffering exhaustion from a forced march.

**Powerful Build.** You count as one size larger when determining your carrying capacity and the weight you can push, drag, or lift.

**Head Down, Feet Forward.** If you move at least 10 feet in a straight line toward a creature as part of your turn, your first melee attack against that creature is made with advantage. If this attack scores a critical hit, you can roll one of the weapon’s damage dice one additional time and add it to the extra damage of the critical hit.

**Root Connected.** As an action, you can touch a plant to communicate with it for 1 minute. The plant can recount events in a 30-foot radius (or within its roots) from the past day, including number and type of creatures, soil disturbances, and a rudimentary visual memory. You can also sense if the plant is struggling to grow, and why.

---

### **Angus**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Size.** You are Medium, typically between 6’3” and 7’3”.

**Bullheaded.** When you take damage equal to or greater than half your maximum hit points from a single source, you gain advantage on melee weapon attacks for 1 minute, or until you target a different creature.

---

### **Bison**

**Ability Score Increase.** Your Constitution score increases by 1.

**Size.** You are Medium, typically between 6’0” and 7’0”.

**Unreal Grip.** You may wield two-handed melee weapons with one hand if holding no other weapons. Versatile weapons wielded one-handed deal their two-handed damage.

## **Homeland Traits**

### **Allemance**

Alley bovines live in harmony with nature’s rhythms, proud of their quiet strength and disdainful of fast-talking outsiders. They’re dependable, occasionally gruff, but fiercely loyal.

**Tenacious Curiosity.** Your Intelligence modifier is treated as 3 higher when learning a new language or tool proficiency.

---

### **Oria**

Bovines of Oria are rare wanderers drawn by dreams and the call of mountains. They bear an instinctual knack for magical engineering passed down through generations.

**Ingenuity in Artificing.** With 24 hours of work, you can attempt to repair a broken magic item. A successful check (based on item type and rarity) restores its magical function. On a failed check, materials are retained, but time required for the next attempt doubles.

---

### **Vinyot**

Vinyotian bovines often serve as caretakers, temple guardians, or casino bouncers. Chauntea’s influence runs deep in their roots, but not all follow the sacred path.

**The Bouncer’s Kid.** You gain proficiency in Perception and one gaming set of your choice. You have advantage on Perception checks to detect cheating when using any gaming set you’re proficient with.

---

### **Arneria**

The desert’s bison are both pathfinders and nobles. Their role in shaping the Beylik's land and law has given them lasting reverence.

**Reader of Maps and Moss.** You gain proficiency with cartographer’s tools. Once per long rest, by examining an underground plant for 1 minute, you can determine the nearest path to the surface and identify the most common non-Humanoid, non-Beast creature type within 300 feet.

---

### **Al’Ar**

Al’ari bovines are rare, adventurous migrants to a feline-dominated island realm. Smaller than their mainland cousins, they’ve adapted with subtlety and stealth.

**Light-Touched Farmhand.** You gain proficiency in Stealth. When traveling overland, you make Dexterity (Stealth) checks to avoid being tracked with advantage.

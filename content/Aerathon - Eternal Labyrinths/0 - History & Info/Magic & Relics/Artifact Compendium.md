## **Overview**

Throughout the shifting vaults and unfathomable chambers of Aerathon’s labyrinths, relics of tremendous power lie dormant—forgotten by time, entombed in myth, and sealed behind riddles, trials, and beasts unspoken. This compendium collects the most notable magical artifacts retrieved by Delvers from across the realm.

Each entry includes:

- **Name**
    
- **Description**
    
- **Properties**
    
- **Origin Labyrinth**
    
- **Notable Bearers**
    

---

## **Artifact Index**

### _The Godsplitter_

- **Type:** Greatsword
    
- **Description:** A blade forged from divine ore, said to sever not flesh, but fate itself.
    
- **Properties:**
    
    - +3 weapon, deals radiant damage
        
    - Once per day, can negate divine intervention or a _Wish_ spell targeting the bearer
        
    - Bearer is immune to divination magic
        
- **Origin:** _Labyrinth of the Shattered Throne_
    
- **Bearer:** Kurk of the Golden Showers (Deceased)
    

---

### _Veil of the Hollow Dream_

- **Type:** Wondrous Item (Cloak)
    
- **Description:** Ethereal, woven from shadow-silk harvested in the dream-realm beneath the Veil Labyrinth.
    
- **Properties:**
    
    - Grants invisibility in dim light or darkness (1 min/day)
        
    - Allows the wearer to walk through walls (once/day, lasts 6 seconds)
        
    - Vulnerability to radiant damage while worn
        
- **Origin:** _Labyrinth of the Hollow Veil_
    
- **Bearer:** Tessari the Silent
    

---

### _Heart of the False Phoenix_

- **Type:** Wondrous Item (Amulet)
    
- **Description:** A burning crystal pulsing with imitation life, stolen from the core of a mimicry engine.
    
- **Properties:**
    
    - Once per long rest, upon death, the bearer is reborn in flames (1 HP, no gear)
        
    - Immune to fire damage
        
    - Vulnerable to cold damage
        
- **Origin:** _Labyrinth of Cinderglass Spires_
    
- **Bearer:** Seraphine Aelthorn
    

---

### _Gilded Throat of Garrux_

- **Type:** Wondrous Item (Necklace)
    
- **Description:** Forged from the melted gold of a thousand crowns, enchanted with ancient war-chants.
    
- **Properties:**
    
    - Bearer can issue a _Command_ affecting all within 60 ft (once/short rest)
        
    - Bonus to Persuasion and Intimidation checks (+5)
        
- **Origin:** _Labyrinth of Sovereign Echoes_
    
- **Bearer:** Garrux Flintmaw
    

---

### _Judgement, the Silent Maul_

- **Type:** Weapon (Maul)
    
- **Description:** This heavy maul hums with divine fury and glows when near betrayal.
    
- **Properties:**
    
    - +2 weapon, deals an additional 2d8 radiant vs fiends or oathbreakers
        
    - Cannot be used to speak or cast verbal spells while wielded
        
- **Origin:** _Labyrinth of the Crimson Throne_
    
- **Bearer:** Sister Judiith
    

---

### _The Hollow Crown_

- **Type:** Wondrous Item (Headgear)
    
- **Description:** A floating circlet that cannot be seen in reflections. Worn by tyrants and prophets alike.
    
- **Properties:**
    
    - Allows access to forbidden knowledge (_Legend Lore_, 1/week)
        
    - Slowly alters the wearer’s ideals to match the labyrinth's last ruler
        
- **Origin:** _Labyrinth of Ten Thousand Lies_
    
- **Bearer:** Unknown (rumored to be lost again)

## **Artifact Retrieval Notes**

- Many artifacts are semi-sentient and choose their bearer.
    
- Artifacts often alter with time or shift in power when removed from the labyrinth's influence.
    
- Replicas exist, though they often carry curses or diminished effects.
    
- Some artifacts respond to livecast audience fervor and increase in power temporarily when a bearer gains fame.

## **Danger Classifications**

| Danger Level | Description                                                                             |
| ------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Low          | Artifact is stable and widely understood.                                               |
| Moderate     | Artifact has unpredictable effects or side-effects.                                     |
| High         | Artifact alters bearer’s personality, memories, or fate.                                |
| Forbidden    | Artifact is sealed or unfit for mortal use. Retrieval punishable by the Delver Accords. |

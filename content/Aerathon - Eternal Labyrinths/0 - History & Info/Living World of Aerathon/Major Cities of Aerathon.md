> _“From soaring spires to misted coasts, the cities of Aerathon pulse with magic, commerce, and conflict alike.”_

___

## **Overview**

The world of **Aerathon** is a realm reborn by chaos, reshaped through unity, and driven by ambition. As Labyrinths tore into the fabric of existence, old empires crumbled and new powers emerged. While many towns and settlements dot the lands, there are a few **central cities** that have withstood the tests of time, monsters, and magic alike. These cities are not only political or economic hubs but are deeply entwined with delver culture, Arcanet integration, and the rise of meritocratic power.

---

## **Halvask — The Gilded Spire**

**Status:** Capital of Unified Aerathon | Power Center of the Arcanet Core  
**Population:** ~6.2 Million (est.)  
**Known For:** Arcanet Core, Grand Colosseum, Divine Oculi Headquarters, Global Labyrinth Nexus

The undisputed center of the modern world, **Halvask** stands as both a monument to unity and a reminder of ambition’s cost. Towering marble structures gleam with veins of mana-conductive silver, and enchanted banners ripple in skies woven with surveillance glyphs.

- **Seat of the High Arbiter**, who oversees international policy and the Adventurer Codex
    
- **Core Source of the Arcanet**, a reality-binding magical web that ranks adventurers and transmits livecasts
    
- **Host of the Grand Colosseum**, where elite delvers compete in internationally viewed exhibitions
    
- **Primary Gatehold to the Labyrinth of Crowned Ash**, the deepest known Tier 7 Labyrinth
    

---

## **Draventh — The Iron Womb**

**Status:** Industrial Capital  
**Population:** ~1.9 Million  
**Known For:** Forging Guilds, Mercenary Courts, Arcane Steelcraft

An ever-smoking city of gear and grit, **Draventh** churns day and night. Though unpolished in appearance, it’s here where the finest arcsteel arms are forged, and where mercenary guilds sign their blood-bound contracts.

- Birthplace of the **Ironback Guild** and **Covenant of Embers**
    
- Known to house **underground arenas** that evade GSN regulation
    
- Tensions with Halvask over **autonomous delver jurisdiction**
    

---

## **Caldrithil — The Whispered Vault**

**Status:** Scholarly Dominion  
**Population:** ~1.1 Million  
**Known For:** Ancient Archives, Runecraft Universities, Spellweaving Symposiums

A city of floating towers and scroll-stuffed halls, **Caldrithil** is the domain of the mind. Scholars, mages, and archaeologists flock here to decode the arcane and divine. Delvers often begin their journeys here to decipher relics or research dungeon lore.

- Hosts the **Oraculum Spire**, an ancient library said to be built atop a sealed minor labyrinth
    
- Source of many magical education livecasts and theory debates
    
- City-state ruled by an academic conclave known as **The Ring of Nine**
    

---

## **Vandrossa — The Tidevault Enclave**

**Status:** Maritime Trade Hub  
**Population:** ~2.4 Million  
**Known For:** Sea Labyrinths, Elemental Temples, Mercantile Clans

Built into the cliffs of the Sapphire Coast, **Vandrossa** is half city, half port, and wholly enchanted by the sea. With intricate bridges over ever-shifting tides, Vandrossa commands both oceanic trade and naval delver expeditions into submerged labyrinths.

- Access point to the **Leviathan’s Hollow**, a waterbound labyrinth only accessible during a full flood moon
    
- Influenced by four great **Mercantile Clans**, each controlling a different element of trade
    
- Frequently hosts **Aether Regattas**, dangerous sea-based delving competitions
    

---

## **Orrakar — The Ember Throne**

**Status:** Frontier Bastion  
**Population:** ~680,000  
**Known For:** Desert Labyrinths, Nomadic Alliances, Obsidian Trade

Rising like a mirage in the crimson dunes, **Orrakar** is both a refuge and a test. Built around an oasis tied to ancient ley-lines, it’s a crossroads for delvers who seek the deep and deadly fire-wracked ruins of the southern wilds.

- Houses the **Crimson Banner Guild**, known for aggressive livecast antics
    
- The nearby **Ashglass Labyrinth** has been deemed “unstable” for decades
    
- Ruled by the **Sandspire Accord**, a rotating council of nomadic and settled leaders
    

---

## **Zal’Vareth — The Wound That Breathes**

**Status:** Magical Anomaly Zone | Forbidden City  
**Population:** Unknown  
**Known For:** Temporal Rifts, Arcane Radiation, Forbidden Technologies

Zal’Vareth is whispered more than mentioned. No one enters freely. Once a city of promise, it now lies fractured, partially merged with a collapsed labyrinth. Though sealed by the Divine Oculi, rumors persist of warlocks and rogue delvers venturing near.

- **Marked as an Arcanet Black Zone** — no signals enter or leave
    
- Some say the **original version of the Arcanet Core** was hidden here
    
- Still appears in some old livecast echoes — though distorted

## **Overview: The Cinderpaw Pact

**Motto:** _“What was forged in flame cannot be broken.”_  
**Headquarters:** **Ashglen Bastion, The Emberwatch Cliffs (Northern Arneria)**  
**Guild Type:** Beastfolk-Only Delver Syndicate, Sovereign Pact Nation, Ancestral Reclaimers  
**Notable Members:**

- **High Fang Uroth Emberhorn** (Guildmaster & Firstborn of the Pact)
    
- **Matriarch Nayra Flamehide** (Battle-Seer & Voice of the Line-Flames)
    
- **Vrekka Quickmaw** (UDMI: 9021, Dual-Axe Champion of Ashglen)
    

The **Cinderpaw Pact** is not merely a guild—it is a declaration. Formed from the ashes of forced servitude and cultural erasure, the Pact was established by Beastfolk elders to create a sovereign, ancestral society rooted in pride, survival, and reckoning. The Pact does not accept non-beastfolk, and its members do not seek applause. Every delve is a ritual. Every relic reclaimed is a piece of themselves restored.

- **Blood and Memory:** Members are bonded by ancient pacts, spiritual brands, and the stories passed down in fireside rites.
    
- **Honor Without Audience:** The Cinderpaw does not permit livecasting. Boons from spectators are refused. Their merit is measured in truth, not spectacle.
    
- **Unbreakable Unity:** Where other guilds train ranks, the Pact trains packs—each crew bound by loyalty, trial, and shared oaths.

## **What the Cinderpaw Pact Offers**

Membership in the Cinderpaw Pact is a rite of passage. It offers no luxury, no titles, no coin-based glory—but it grants something rarer: belonging, purpose, and vengeance sanctified.

### **Guild Benefits**

- **Ancestral Weapon Rites** – Each member receives a bound weapon, forged in the ashes of their bloodline and shaped by ritual.
    
- **Pack Formation** – All delvers operate in Packs, bound by mutual trials and shared memory scars.
    
- **Ashglen Sanctuary** – A sovereign stronghold free from outside law, where Beastfolk govern their own with no interference.
    
- **Memory Rites & Emberbrands** – Members undergo spiritual bonding with the Pact’s Line-Flames—magical marks that reflect their family legacy and personal growth.
    
- **Relic Reclamation Doctrine** – All artifacts tied to Beastfolk heritage are to be returned, not sold, and placed in the Hall of Echoes for preservation.
    

While the Pact lacks the opulence of its rivals, its strength is unmatched in unity, grit, and purpose.

## **Who Joins the Cinderpaw Pact?**

The Pact does not _recruit_. You seek it. You _prove_ yourself worthy. And if the Emberbrands accept you, you become more than a delver—you become a living legacy.

- **Beastfolk Outcasts** – Born in cities that still fear the old bloodlines, looking to reclaim what was denied.
    
- **Line-Kin Descendants** – Children of families who still carry stories of the Chain Age and want to bear them forward in flame.
    
- **Wanderers & Ashmarked** – Those who survived Labyrinth incursions alone and followed the smoke to Ashglen.
    
- **Revenant Seekers** – Individuals who claim they’ve seen their ancestors in the mist of the Labyrinths—and want answers.
    

No outsiders. No exceptions.

## **Relationship with Other Governing Bodies**

### **Autonomy from the Guild Alliance**

- The Cinderpaw Pact does not recognize the authority of the Guild Alliance, but honors mutual territory boundaries—barely.
    
- They reject the Arcanet Core’s scoring system publicly, though scores are still passively tracked via echo-mirrors.
    

### **Tense Neutrality with the Divine Oculi**

- The Pact has refused three invitations to join the Oculi’s relic-protection tribunal, stating the gods “watched in silence too long.”
    
- Clerics traveling to Ashglen must relinquish divine broadcasting tools upon entry.
    

### **Resentment Toward the Argent Banner**

- The Pact considers the Banner’s noble-backed “purity delving” a desecration of sacred ground.
    
- Ashglen lorekeepers maintain a secret list of relics the Banner has “stolen” that will one day be reclaimed.

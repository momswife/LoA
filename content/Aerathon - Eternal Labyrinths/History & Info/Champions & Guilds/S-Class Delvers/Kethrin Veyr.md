## **Overview**

**Kethrin “Brookblade” Veyr is an otterfolk S-Class delver of **Louvain’s Free Company**—a hydrokinetic scout, salvage engineer, and lock-breaker of flooded spaces. She doesn’t batter Labyrinths; she **rethreads** them. Condensation, seepage, slurry, even steam: currents reveal structure to her, and she turns that structure into routes—**ferries over lava**, **drains through curses**, **cofferdams around bosses**. Crews swear the drip changes tempo when she arrives, fog tidies at the edges, and black ooze puckers into a neat, nervous cube.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Cofferdam of Monster Meadows**  
A corridor that flooded at random became a calm trench under Kethrin’s hand. She impounded the surge into a hovering **box of water**, walked civilians through, then poured it back—**washing a mimic swarm** neatly into a drain. Post-op note: “Needed a broom.”

**The Compliance of the Ooze Choir**  
A pit of sentient slimes lured crews with imitated screams. Kethrin salted the edges, set metronome taps with dripping sconces, and trained them to sing a **four-count work bell**. The pit now functions as a warning system and morale booster. Screams discontinued.

**The Ferry at the Wanehollow Drop**  
Where a rope bridge failed nightly, she rigged a **rope-and-water funicular**—buckets rising as others sank, counterweighted by the Labyrinth’s own seepage. An entire expedition (and a stubborn mule) crossed without a slip.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**Spillway Gauntlet (very rare)**  
A clockwork bracer with folded bronze fins. As a bonus action, **redirect** a contiguous liquid/fog/ooze in a 30-ft line or 15-ft cone up to 90°. **3 charges/day** to **impound** fluid into a hovering 10-ft cube for 1 minute (moves 20 ft as a bonus action). Creatures in the flow must save or **slide 10 ft**.

**Reel-Sword of Brooksteel (rare)**  
A ribbon-blade that coils into a spool. **Finesse, reach (10–20 ft).** On hit near a liquid, target makes a Strength save or is **pulled 5–10 ft** along the current. Retracts with a satisfying click.

**Eel-Skin Half-Cloak**  
Sheds water and heat; grants advantage to resist exhaustion from wet or cold. Whisper a rumor into the hem—if it **“holds water,”** she feels a tug toward a related clue on the next delve.

**Tide-Log & Hygrometer**  
A waterproof folio that records **humidity gradients** as contour lines; advantage to locate hidden **sluices, drains, and voids**. The brass hygrometer ticks faster near damp-sealed doors.

**Salt & Chalk Kit**  
Sea-salt, kiln-chalk, and a string line. Snapping a **salted chalk-line** creates a 30-ft **dry lane** that suppresses minor ooze adhesion and illusion drift for 1 minute. Slimes hate it; quartermasters love it.

## **Overview**

**Batariel the Sealed Flame** is an S-Class paladin known across Aerathon for his **zealotry**, **imposing presence**, and **relentless will**. Once a humble scribe of a forgotten god, Batariel rose through the ranks of the **Celestial Bastion**, a powerful cult cloaked in the trappings of divine law. His voice carries the weight of sermons and steel—he preaches conquest as salvation, and his every strike is in the name of a god whose name the world is not permitted to remember.

While many delvers fight for coin, glory, or purpose, Batariel fights for **dominion over the Labyrinths**. His crew—the Penumbra Sanctum—claims to “seal away” dangerous artifacts and relics for the public good. In truth, these items are stored, hoarded, and never seen again. Whether it is divine protection or theft beneath banners of righteousness is hotly debated.

To his allies, Batariel is a radiant savior. To his enemies, a hypocritical tyrant. But all agree: when Batariel walks, the Labyrinth trembles.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Brand of Kaldrith**  
Batariel led a full purge of the Labyrinth of Kaldrith, sealing its lower vaults with radiant runes and forbidding any further delver entry. The runes still burn with celestial flame to this day.

**The Thousand-Chain Judgment**  
Upon learning a rogue delver crew had plundered a forbidden relic, Batariel personally hunted each member across five homelands. Their trial was held mid-delving, with a magical audience watching the executions.

**The Silent Benediction**  
In the aftermath of a cataclysmic delve collapse, Batariel was seen emerging alone—carrying five relics in one hand and a priest's broken mask in the other. The Penumbra Sanctum declared the delve a "holy reclamation."

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**Gilded Vow**  
A massive, radiant greatsword inscribed with divine law. Cannot be used by those with unrepentant sin. Emits a cone of radiant suppression that silences magical effects.

**Aegis of Twelve Eyes**  
A shield said to hold twelve closed eyes, opening only in the presence of great sin. When open, each eye fires a beam of radiant judgment once per long rest.

**Mantle of the First Doctrine**  
A tabard worn beneath heavy gold-inlaid plate. Grants Batariel resistance to psychic and necrotic damage, but prevents him from speaking falsehoods while worn.

## **Overview**

**Inflato the Clown Pervert**—an S-Class delver of sterling reputation and baffling brand—stands as the **Lawful Good** foil to Huldiberaht Schnofinkel’s crater-making legend. The name was spat at him in mockery by Huldiberaht; Inflato **bowed and adopted it**, grinning beneath greasepaint and brass halo. _“Fools disarm fear,”_ he says, _“so I’ll be the biggest fool of all.”_ Through pressure-ward magicks, buoyant sigils, and meticulous adherence to statute, he **turns catastrophe into harmless spectacle**—collapses into parade routes, shockwaves into confetti gusts, panic into orderly evacuation.

Where Huld is nature’s bludgeon, Inflato is **civilization’s airbag**—and he has **saved more lives** on broadcast than any other delver of the last decade.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Featherfall Carnival at Oathgrove**  
As Oathgrove’s caverns began to implode during Huldiberaht’s root-flood gambit, Inflato arrived with a ring of buoyancy and mustered volunteers. He **inflated a lattice of shimmering safety bladders**, converting a lethal sinkhole into a controlled descent. _One hundred and six civilians_ were lowered—dunk-tank–style—into shallow pools: embarrassed, damp, **alive**.

**The Masquerade at Cragspire Bastion**  
Anticipating Huld’s meteorform, Inflato **ringed the arena with mirrored shields and pressure-runes** disguised as clown masks. The impact vented into harmless wind that sent pennants snapping and cotton candy tumbling while rival delvers lived to yield. Huld snarled, “Name that trick, harlequin.” Inflato smiled: _“Compliance.”_

**The Court of Softened Blades**  
Challenged by three zealot champions, Inflato won **without a single lethal blow**: blades caught in balloon-steel tethers, hammers cushioned mid-swing, tempers cooled by a rising waltz of pressure-drops. The magistrate later codified **“Inflato Protocols”** for nonlethal victory conditions in tribunal combat.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**Codpiece of Counterpressure**  
A heart-shaped bulwark (he refuses to rename it) anchoring his pressure wards. **Drinks in kinetic force** and redistributes it across invisible membranes—turning bone-breaking impacts into theatrical gusts, streamers, and **harmless bounce**.

**Parade Standard of the Argent Banner**  
A collapsible pennant-pole inscribed with evacuation sigils. When unfurled, it **projects calming glyphwork**, marks safe routes, and **conjures floating “stepping balloons”** for children and the injured. The finial doubles as a one-use _**resilient sphere**_ for emergency triage.

**Seltzer of Sanctity**  
A brass-bound siphon spraying consecrated tonic. **Extinguishes alchemical fires**, neutralizes mundane acids, and counts as **holy water** against undead and fiends. On bystanders, it clears lungs, cleans wounds, and leaves a faint **sugared-citrus** scent.

## **Rivalry with Huldiberaht Schnofinkel**

Inflato calls Huldiberaht his **“favorite problem.”** He studies Huld’s trajectories, **posts marshals and medics before the craters form**, and proves—again and again—that **mercy properly engineered can outmuscle cruelty**. The day Huld tried to shame him with a name, Inflato turned it into a **banner**. Wherever the Sky Root aims **“DOWN,”** the Gentle Jester is already there—**lifting everyone else up**.

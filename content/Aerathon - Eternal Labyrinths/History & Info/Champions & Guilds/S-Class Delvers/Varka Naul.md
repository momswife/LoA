## **Overview**

**Varkha “Carrion-Laugh” Naul is a hyenafolk S-Class delver whose name is whispered where the walls already bleed. Varkha treats Labyrinths as **abattoirs with puzzles attached**—places to **harvest fear, break lines, and feed the gods that love shrieking**. He collects screams like coin, tallies them on bone, and spends them on doors that refuse to open.

He is a consummate professional butcher: efficient, inventive, and **utterly without pity**. Crews that survive him agree on one thing—**the dungeon gets quieter when he smiles**.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Denial of the Salt Gate**  
A flood-choked lock that had resisted three companies opened for Varkha after he **bled a volunteer** over its threshold and **laughed** until the hinge screamed back. The chamber’s spirits fled. The client never asked what he fed the sluice after.

**The Choir of Broken Glass**  
In a prism-maze that echoed spells, he tuned the reflections with a **rack of wind-chimes made from lenses and teeth**. Every cry set the maze to the right note; every note opened a pane. He left the chimes behind, “so they remember.”

**The Taker’s Truce**  
Negotiated safe transit with a ghoul prefect by offering **one living hour per delver**. He **borrowed the hours from enemies mid-fight**, leaving them still breathing when the ghouls arrived. Technically fulfilled.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**Tally-Torque of the Crimson Garden (very rare)**  
A collar of braided sinew threaded with etched wolf-bones. Each **scream within 30 ft** (hostile or allied) places a notch; spend 3 notches to **quicken** your next action, 5 to **refresh** a spent ability, 9 to **force disadvantage** on a single saving throw against your next effect. Notches fade at dawn with a faint chuckle.

**Laughing Sundial**  
A palm-sized brass dial that casts a **second shadow** when fear is present. As an action, align the shadows to **delay** a trap or lair action for 1 round; once per short rest you may **“advance noon”** to trigger the next trap **immediately** instead.

**Hyenalaughed Hook**  
A curved, serrated blade on black chain. **Reach 10 ft**, returns to the hand when released. On a hit against a **frightened** creature, deal an extra **2d6 psychic** and **drag 10 ft**. If the target stops adjacent to you, you gain **temp HP = your level**.

**Pain-Priest’s Satchel**  
Clamps, brine, smelling salts, and ritual pins. Grants proficiency (or advantage if proficient) on **Intimidation** and **Medicine** checks related to **coercion, triage under duress,** or **keeping someone awake**.

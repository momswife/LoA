## **Overview**

**Matron “Auntie Biscuit” Crumb** is an S-Class delver, quartermaster, and cafeteria tyrant whose culinary conjury has **ended more sieges than swords**. She is everyone’s favorite lunch lady and **worst nightmare** if you track mud across her clean floor. Auntie Biscuit wields battlefield cuisine the way battlemages wield fire—steam vents for cover, **pressure-pot detonations** for crowd control, and gravy so thick it **counts as difficult terrain**.

Her motto is simple: **“Full bellies make brave hearts.”** In Labyrinths, she **feeds allies, starves curses**, and serves hot, morale-boosting meals that **override fear effects**. Monsters expecting heroes find instead a bustling mess line, a ringing dinner bell, and a stern woman in a hairnet telling them to **use their inside voices**.

## **Notable Accomplishments**

**The Biscuit Bridge at Betemere**  
Facing a collapsed span over acid fog, Auntie Biscuit **baked a load-bearing biscuit lattice** glazed with quick-hardening syrup. The company crossed; the bridge was later served with stew. **Zero waste.**

**The Truce of Three Soups**  
Two rival delving crews and a troll warband agreed to **parley over chow** when Auntie rang the bell. Her **Broth of Mutual Recognition** suppressed hostile intent for one hour. Contracts signed, stew refills honored.

**The Panini Press of Porta Strega**  
When ghosts swarmed the woods, Auntie deployed the **Great Griddle**, searing sigils into the earth. Specters crisped into **harmless crusts** that crumbled into breadcrumbs to **salt a warding circle**. Fragrant. Effective.

## **Equipment and Artifacts**

**Ladle of Ladling (and Smacking)**  
A mythic iron ladle that **scoops elements** as easily as stew: **lava becomes tomato soup**, blizzards become **shaved ice**, poisonous mists condense into **pickles in brine**. Counts as a **+3 versatile mace** when discipline is needed.

**Apron of the Infinite Pantry**  
Pockets hold a **walk-in kitchen**: collapsible ovens, self-stirring cauldrons, and a **to-go counter** for healing snacks. As an action, she can produce **1d4+2 Hot Plates**—consumables that heal and grant a short, flavorful buff.

**Hairnet of Command**  
Creatures within 30 feet who can see Auntie must succeed on a **Wisdom save** to ignore a **reasonable kitchen order**: _“Wash your hands,” “Wait your turn,” “Put that bone down.”_ On a fail, they comply for **1 round**. Legendary creatures have **advantage**, but still feel bad about it.

**Tray of Unruly Deflection**  
A battered lunch tray that functions as a **+2 shield**. Once per round, she may **ricochet** a ranged attack into a to-go bin, turning it into a **covered dish** that detonates (small, nonlethal **custard burst**) at a point she designates.

## **Rumors and Anomalies**

- Her **stew spoons up memories**—the good ones—granting **temporary hit points** and **advantage vs. fear** while the taste lingers.
    
- The **Ladle** once **served the gods**, and it occasionally hums a hymn when she makes gravy.
    
- She keeps a **tab** with the Labyrinths themselves: once per delve, a corridor **opens into a kitchen** that wasn’t there before.
    
- Her **bread rises to the occasion**—literally forming **stairs, shields,** or **lifebuoys** on command.

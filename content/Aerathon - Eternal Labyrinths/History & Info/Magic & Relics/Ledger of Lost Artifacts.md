## **Overview**

While many artifacts of the labyrinths have been retrieved and documented, a more perilous and uncertain list exists: the _Lost_ and the _Sealed_. Some of these relics vanished with their bearers, others lie locked behind immortal wards, and a few were intentionally buried to prevent catastrophic misuse. Many are rumored to still whisper to the world, yearning to return.

This document records known, suspected, and theorized artifacts that remain unrecovered or deliberately hidden from mortal hands.

Each entry includes:

- **Name**
    
- **Rumored Function**
    
- **Status**
    
- **Last Known Location**
    
- **Notes of Concern**
    

---

## **Artifact Records**

### _The Lantern of Narthul_

- **Rumored Function:** Grants true sight into alternate timelines and echoes of the past
    
- **Status:** Sealed beneath Halvask in a null-magic oubliette
    
- **Last Known Location:** The Foundation Vaults under the Old Delver Parliament
    
- **Notes of Concern:** A recorded breach during the Second Arcane War nearly destabilized the realm’s fate web. All vault records regarding the lantern have been wiped or redacted.
    

---

### _The Chains of Tharozh_

- **Rumored Function:** Bind and suppress any being, mortal or divine, rendering them mute and powerless
    
- **Status:** Lost—believed to have sunk with the Sky-Fortress _Aetherion_
    
- **Last Known Location:** The Shrouded Sea, drifting above the Mael Veil
    
- **Notes of Concern:** A cult known as the Silent Choir has been searching for the chains. Their rise in recent years is troubling.
    

---

### _The Mirror of Second Birth_

- **Rumored Function:** Allows a user to rewrite a single event in their personal timeline
    
- **Status:** Sealed by the Divine Oculi under geas-lock
    
- **Last Known Location:** Unknown—Oculi records scrubbed during the Schism of Ashenlight
    
- **Notes of Concern:** Diviners report that all scrying directed at this object results in recursive madness.
    

---

### _The Bloom That Hungers_

- **Rumored Function:** A living artifact that feeds on ambition and sprouts new abilities in its host
    
- **Status:** Lost—last reported in possession of a fallen S-Class Delver
    
- **Last Known Location:** Lower strata of the Labyrinth of Verdant Silence
    
- **Notes of Concern:** The Delver’s corpse was never found. The bloom may have integrated into the labyrinth's flora.
    

---

### _The Weeping Mask_

- **Rumored Function:** Grants the wearer access to every memory of the last hundred beings it was worn by
    
- **Status:** Sealed in a plane-locked reliquary
    
- **Last Known Location:** The Vault of Masks, Eastern Deadwall
    
- **Notes of Concern:** Removed from livecast use due to psychological trauma experienced by audience-linked casters. Discontinued for public viewing.
    

---

### _The Spine of the False Saint_

- **Rumored Function:** Grants miracles through deceit, producing holy effects with corrupt intent
    
- **Status:** Lost during the Massacre at Virelin Cradle
    
- **Last Known Location:** Carried into battle by Sister Revane, never recovered
    
- **Notes of Concern:** Witness accounts suggest it granted a “blessing of burning blood” to her followers—none survived.
    

---

## **Containment Protocols**

- **Null Chambers:** Artifacts sealed in Halvask are held in anti-magical environments maintained by the Arcanet Core.
    
- **Divine Seals:** Several relics are bound by celestial or infernal contracts. Unauthorized attempts to break these are prosecuted by both mortal and extraplanar law.
    
- **MDO Escorts Required:** Any attempted retrieval of a known Lost or Sealed artifact must include at least one ranking Magister of the Ministry of Delving Oversight.

## **III. Description**

The entity is identified as a **Gloomback Strider**, a cold-leeching predator native to the dim, frost-laden halls of Floor 12. It is known for:

- **Ambient warmth siphoning**, causing local temperature drops of 10–15 degrees
    
- **Frost-burn trails** left across grass, crops, and livestock pens
    
- **Nocturnal stalking patterns**
    
- **Vocal mimicry** designed to lure isolated civilians or separated delvers
    

Witness reports confirm two livestock fatalities and multiple instances of mimic-lure calls near farm perimeters.

No evidence of major curse bloom, though **trace corruption signatures** have been detected.

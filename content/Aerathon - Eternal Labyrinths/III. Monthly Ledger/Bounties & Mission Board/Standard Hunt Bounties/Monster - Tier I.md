## **III. Description**

The target is a **Chasm Gnawer**, a subterranean vermin-beast native to the brittle plank-bridges and unstable cliff-pockets of the Labyrinth’s early floors.

Observed behaviors include:

- Rapid wood consumption
    
- Burrowing under roads and crops
    
- Panic-attacks when cornered
    
- Aggressive chewing on wagons, fence-posts, and cargo
    

Multiple farms report grain-loss and collapsed fence lines.  
No corruption signatures or curse residues detected.

---

## **IV. Mission Objectives**

1. **Track and capture or neutralize** the Chasm Gnawer.
    
2. **Prevent further structural damage** to silos, barns, and trade wagons.
    
3. **Survey** the surrounding farmland for secondary tunnel networks.
    
4. Return with **proof of subdual** or **live containment** if feasible.

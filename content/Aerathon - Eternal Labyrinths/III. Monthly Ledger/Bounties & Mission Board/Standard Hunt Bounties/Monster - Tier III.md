## **III. Description**

The target is a **Pinehide Mauler**, a thick-framed labyrinth beast characterized by:

- **Bark-plated armor** grown from symbiotic cavern flora
    
- **Explosive charge attacks** capable of snapping entire pines
    
- **Shrapnel shedding**, where splintered bark is expelled in a defensive burst
    
- **Low-light aggression**, making dusk and dawn especially hazardous
    

Native to Floor 8’s moss caverns, the Mauler has migrated into overworld forest terrain, causing:

- Crushed logging paths
    
- Uprooted trees
    
- Attacked timber crews
    
- Disruption to trade transport routes
    

A mild corruption signature has been detected.

---

## **IV. Mission Objectives**

1. **Track and neutralize** the Pinehide Mauler.
    
2. **Prevent collapse** of critical timber harvesting zones.
    
3. **Investigate potential seismic breach activity** along the northern ridge.
    
4. **Avoid proximity during charge cycles**; maintain clear escape vectors.
